The comparison is therefore not a literal rerun of CFW.  It is a
convention-matched forward-only projection of our stored scan, with the
irreducible residual that CFW sampled localization parameters while our
headline ensemble fixes them.  The corrected headline is that our
post-audit bound is factor-2.2 stronger than the CFW 2008 RS marker at
matched `g_s^*` conventions, while both analyses still point to an
`M_KK > O(10 TeV)` lower bound under anarchic flavor.
