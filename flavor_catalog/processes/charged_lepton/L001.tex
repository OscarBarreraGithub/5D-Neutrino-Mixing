\section{L001: \texorpdfstring{\(\mu\to e\gamma\)}{mu -> e gamma} Dipole LFV}

\subsection*{Process}
Standard notation: \(\mu^+\to e^+\gamma\).  This entry covers the charged-lepton
flavor-violating radiative muon decay and the dipole-style bound on the
off-diagonal \(e\mu\) lepton-flavor spurion used by the current RS lepton-sector
scan.  It is a charged-lepton-family process, not part of the quark
\(\Delta F=2\) scan lane.

\subsection*{PDG / equivalent value(s)}
The current primary-experiment limit is the MEG II 2025 result,
\[
  \mathcal{B}(\mu^+\to e^+\gamma) < 1.5\times 10^{-13}\quad (90\%\ {\rm C.L.}),
\]
from the 2021--2022 MEG II data set, with quoted search sensitivity
\(2.2\times10^{-13}\).  The local source snapshot is
\texttt{flavor\_catalog/references/L001/megii2025\_arxiv2504\_15711.txt}.
For audit context, the PDG 2025 muon listing snapshot still records
\(<3.1\times10^{-13}\) at \(90\%\) C.L.; this is the MEG+MEG II 2021 combined
limit and appears to lag the later published MEG II 2025 paper.

\subsection*{Relevance to the RS / anarchic-flavor pipeline}
In the lepton extension of the warped model, \(\mu\to e\gamma\) probes
brane-localized or loop-induced dipoles controlled by the off-diagonal
combination \((\bar Y_N\bar Y_N^\dagger)_{12}\).  The repo implements the
Perez--Randall NDA form
\(\mathcal{B}\simeq 4\times10^{-8}\,|(\bar Y_N\bar Y_N^\dagger)_{12}|^2
(3\,{\rm TeV}/M_{\rm KK})^4\), converts an experimental branching-ratio limit
to \(C=\sqrt{\mathcal{B}_{\rm lim}/(4\times10^{-8})}\), and uses the updated
MEG II limit in the scan default.  Numerically, the live default gives
\(C\simeq1.936\times10^{-3}\), compared with the paper-era \(C=0.02\).

\subsection*{Post-2008 developments}
Relative to the arXiv:0804.1954 RS-flavor baseline and the contemporaneous
Perez--Randall lepton paper, the experimental story changed substantially.
Perez--Randall used the MEGA-era \(\mathcal{B}<1.2\times10^{-11}\) input.  The
full MEG data set from 2009--2013 pushed the limit to \(4.2\times10^{-13}\).
MEG II then reported a 2021-only result of \(7.5\times10^{-13}\), whose
combination with MEG gave \(3.1\times10^{-13}\).  The 2025 MEG II 2021--2022
analysis now gives \(1.5\times10^{-13}\), and the published article states a
target sensitivity near \(6\times10^{-14}\) with additional data.

\subsection*{Constraint validity and model dependence}
The experimental bound is clean: a signal would be charged-lepton flavor
violation with negligible Standard Model background.  Its translation into this
repo is model-dependent, because it relies on the chosen RS lepton spurion, NDA
prefactor, and \(M_{\rm KK}\) convention rather than a full loop calculation.
The catalog should therefore classify it as a robust experimental limit but a
lepton-extension-only, dipole/NDA model interpretation.

\subsection*{Code coverage in this repo}
YES.  The implementation is in \texttt{flavorConstraints/muToEGamma.py}: the
module documents the \(\mu\to e\gamma\) dipole constraint at line 1, records
the paper-era \(C=0.02\) coefficient at line 20, defines
\(\texttt{PREFAC\_BR}=4.0\times10^{-8}\) at line 31, converts branching-ratio
limits to \(C\) at line 61, and exposes \(\texttt{check\_mu\_to\_e\_gamma}\) at
line 75.  The scan default is the MEG II 2025 value:
\texttt{scanParams/scan.py}:32--33 sets
\(\texttt{BR\_LIMIT\_MEGII\_2025}=1.5\times10^{-13}\), and line 264 makes it the
default \(\texttt{ScanConfig.br\_limit}\).  Tests at
\texttt{tests/test\_mu\_to\_e\_gamma.py}:40 and
\texttt{tests/test\_scan.py}:50 exercise the updated limit.

\subsection*{Implementation difficulty}
LOW for cataloging and present code coverage, because the observable is already

paper-era NDA normalization, \(M_{\rm KK}\) convention, and whether the final
catalog should expose both the paper-reproduction and MEG II 2025 modes.
