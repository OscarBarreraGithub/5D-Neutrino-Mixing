## Canonical values

Canonical process-specific singlet benchmark:

`m_T > 1.36 TeV` at `95% CL`, ATLAS 2024 / PDG 2025, for pair-produced isospin-singlet vector-like `T` with `B(T -> Wb:Ht:Zt)=1/2:1/4:1/4`.

Broad Run-2 branching-envelope cross-check:

`m_T > 1.48 TeV` at `95% CL`, CMS 2023, excluding masses below 1.48 TeV for all decays to third-generation quarks in the `bW/tZ/tH` branching triangle; CMS also quotes sensitivity up to 1.54 TeV depending on branching fractions.

Both are mass exclusion limits and are stored in `pdg_or_equivalent.values`. Dataset luminosities `140 fb^-1` and `138 fb^-1` are stored as supporting metadata only.
