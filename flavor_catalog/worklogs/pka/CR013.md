## Sources Considered but Not Used as Canonical

- ATLAS 2021 full Run-2 diphoton result is a strong channel result, but PDG 2025 and CMS 2024 supersede it for the highest diphoton RS-graviton mass reach at comparable assumptions.
- CMS 2018 remains useful for search history, but CMS 2024 supersedes it with the full 2016-2018 dataset and stronger RS-graviton limits.
- HEPData table downloads for individual Figure 3 tables were not promoted into scalar values; the record metadata was snapshotted and table IDs were cited for future cross-section-level reinterpretation.
- Spin-0 heavy scalar limits were not promoted as RS mass constraints because they require a scalar/radion/singlet model with specified production and branching fractions.
