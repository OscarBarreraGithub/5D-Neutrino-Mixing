## Assignment

CR007 covers direct LHC searches for a spin-2 RS KK graviton:
`pp -> G_KK^(1) -> ZZ, WW, gamma gamma, ell ell, t tbar`, with the bulk
Randall-Sundrum interpretation treated as the model-relevant anchor.

## Canonical Value Decision

There are two useful "best" limits, and the sidecar records both:

- Generic RS spin-2 headline: PDG 2025 lists CMS 2024 diphoton
  `m(G_KK) > 4.8 TeV` at 95% CL for `k/Mbar_Pl = 0.1`.
- Bulk-RS benchmark anchor: PDG 2025 lists CMS 2023 all-jets WW/ZZ
  `m(G_bulk) > 1.4 TeV` at 95% CL for `k/Mbar_Pl = 0.5`.

The prose treats the `1.4 TeV` value as the model-relevant bulk-RS anchor and
the `4.8 TeV`/`4.78 TeV` diphoton/dilepton values as generic RS spin-2
cross-checks. This avoids overapplying brane/local-light-fermion assumptions
to bulk-RS points with suppressed photon and lepton couplings.
