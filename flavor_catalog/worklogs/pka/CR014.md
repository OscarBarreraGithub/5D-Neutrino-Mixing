  cross-section upper limits from 21 to 119 fb for a top-philic \(Z'\)
  simplified model over its parameter choices.

## Post-2010 Search History

- arXiv:1409.7339, CMS 8 TeV lepton-plus-jets: first published CMS four-top
  search; established multivariate handling of the large \(t\bar t+\)jets
  background.
- arXiv:1702.06164, CMS 13 TeV early Run 2: moved four-top searches to the
  higher-energy LHC data set in lepton-plus-jets and opposite-sign dileptons.
- arXiv:1710.10614, CMS same-sign/multilepton: cleaner topology and first
  strong top-Higgs Yukawa interpretation.
- arXiv:1811.02305, ATLAS single-lepton/opposite-sign dilepton: complementary
  channel assumptions and combination with ATLAS multilepton searches.
- arXiv:1908.06463, CMS full Run-2 multilepton: increased luminosity and added
  scalar, pseudoscalar, top-Yukawa, and EFT interpretations.
- arXiv:2106.11683, ATLAS full Run-2 measurement: combined channels and moved
  ATLAS close to observation.
- arXiv:2303.03864, CMS evidence in all-hadronic, lepton-plus-jets, and
  opposite-sign dileptons: complementary to multilepton measurements.
- arXiv:2303.15061 and 2305.13439: ATLAS and CMS observation papers.
- arXiv:2304.01678 and CMS-B2G-25-005/arXiv:2604.14058: explicit top-philic
  resonance searches in four-top-like topologies.
