## Sources used

- `arXiv:2503.11317` (ATLAS 2025): chosen as canonical current direct longitudinal same-sign WW fiducial upper limit. Promoted value: `0.45 fb` observed 95% CL upper limit; expected limit `0.70 fb` recorded in the same value block.
- `arXiv:2009.09429` (CMS 2020): historical comparator and first CMS polarized same-sign WW measurement. Promoted historical upper limit: `1.17 fb` observed 95% CL; expected `0.88 fb`.
- `arXiv:2312.00420` (ATLAS 2024): full Run-2 same-sign WWjj measurement and aQGC interpretation; used for search history only.
- `arXiv:2004.10612` (ATLAS 2020/2023 Nature Physics): ZZjj VBS observation; used to document neutral-diboson VBS development.
- `arXiv:1611.02428` (ATLAS 2017): 8 TeV same-sign WWjj/aQGC analysis; used as early post-2010 LHC search history.
- `arXiv:2603.18630` (ATLAS 2026): current ATLAS combined aQGC/EFT interpretation. Cited as current coefficient-level context but not promoted because this draft does not quote individual coefficient intervals.
- `arXiv:1109.1570` (Contino et al. 2011): composite-Higgs/RS-adjacent theory reference for resonances in high-energy VBS.
- PDG 2025 `Anomalous W/Z Quartic Couplings (QGCs)`: used as EFT/QGC framework context only; it does not supply a single CR011 longitudinal WW fiducial limit or RS mass bound.
