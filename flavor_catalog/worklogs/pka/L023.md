# PKA Worklog: L023 neutrino trident production

I checked out `flavor-catalog/2026q2` and read the required plan,
orchestrator decisions, Opus approval, process template, and catalog README in
order. The assigned row is L023 in `charged_lepton`, with initial coverage
`NOT-SEEN`.

Source searches focused on the historical muon-trident measurements and modern
reinterpretations. Queries included `neutrino trident CCFR CHARM-II NuTeV
sigma SM 0.82 0.28`, `Neutrino tridents and W-Z interference CCFR`, `First
observation of neutrino trident production CHARM II CERN SPS`, `NuTeV neutrino
trident hep-ex/9811012`, `Neutrino tridents at DUNE arXiv 1902.06765`, and
`Belle II L_mu L_tau neutrino trident arXiv`. I used PubMed/APS metadata for
CCFR, CERN CDS for CHARM-II, arXiv source for NuTeV, Altmannshofer et al. 2014
and 2019, Kaneta-Shimomura 2017, and arXiv:0804.1954 as the requested 2008
baseline. I rejected blog summaries, phenomenology slides, and noncanonical
web tables. I downloaded the CHARM-II CDS PDF only into a temporary extraction
directory; it was image-only under `pdftotext`, and the PDF was not retained.

The measured-value block uses the latest compact experimental compilation in
the DUNE trident paper: CHARM-II `1.58 +/- 0.64`, CCFR `0.82 +/- 0.28`, and
NuTeV `0.72^{+1.73}_{-0.72}`. I also kept the CCFR primary event-count basis,
`37.0 +/- 12.4` observed versus `45.3 +/- 2.3` SM, because it independently
reproduces the `0.82 +/- 0.28` ratio. Projection numbers from DUNE and Belle-II
are intentionally in `auxiliary_context`, not `pdg_or_equivalent`.

Coverage greps were run over `quarkConstraints`, `qcd`, `flavorConstraints`,
`neutrinos`, `yukawa`, `warpConfig`, `solvers`, `scanParams`, and `tests`, plus
the plan-v1 broad greps over docs/scripts. No trident implementation was found;
the only `CHARM` hits in the required code directories are charm-quark mass
constants. Open issues for WA/CA are the CHARM-II uncertainty convention
(`0.57` versus `0.64`) and the DUNE projection wording (`25%` abstract versus
`40%` body baseline).
