## Canonical Current Limit

Selected canonical summary source: PDG 2025 "Searches for Quark and Lepton
Compositeness", with ATLAS and CMS primary papers as support.

- ATLAS full-Run-2 non-resonant dilepton search, arXiv:2006.12946:
  PDG quotes combined dielectron+dimuon 95% CL lower limits of 35.5/26.5 TeV
  for RR constructive/destructive, 35.8/26.0 TeV for LL
  constructive/destructive, and 32.5/28.8 TeV for LR
  constructive/destructive.
- CMS full-Run-2 high-mass dilepton search, arXiv:2103.02708:
  PDG quotes 23.9 TeV for the LL destructive endpoint and 36.4 TeV for the
  RR constructive endpoint. CMS paper/public page gives the rounded published
  range as 23.8/23.9--36.4 TeV.
- HEPData record `ins1849964` was used as structured support. Direct `curl`
  of the table YAML hit a Cloudflare challenge, so the tracked HEPData
  snapshot records the HEPData page text and table identifiers rather than a
  downloaded YAML table.

## Sources Considered / Rejected

- ATLAS arXiv:1906.05609 was suggested in the prompt, but it is a heavy
  charged-boson `W' -> l nu` search, not a neutral Drell-Yan high-mass
  dilepton contact-interaction result. Rejected for CR009.
- The prompt cited "Falkowski-Mimouni-Lehman arXiv:1611.04930"; that arXiv
  record is Barbieri, Murphy, and Senia on a composite leptoquark model.
  The relevant Falkowski/Gonzalez-Alonso/Mimouni four-fermion SMEFT paper is
  arXiv:1706.03783. The tracked theory snapshot records this correction.
- Publisher PDFs were not tracked. Local source snapshots are text extracts
  only.
