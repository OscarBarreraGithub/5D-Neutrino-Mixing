process id `EW003`.  I checked out `flavor-catalog/2026q2`, read plan v1
sections B, D, and F, the orchestrator decisions file, and
`flavor_catalog/latex/process_template.tex`.  The orchestrator file lists

kept only a compact text snapshot with the inclusive, exclusive, and scaled

context.  For lattice inputs I used the arXiv source of FLAG Review 2024
(`arXiv:2411.04268`) and captured the \(B\to D^*\ell\nu\) and
\(B\to\pi\ell\nu\) exclusive values.
