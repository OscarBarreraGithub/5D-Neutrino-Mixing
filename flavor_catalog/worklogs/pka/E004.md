# PKA Worklog: E004 Neutron EDM

I followed the required catalog plan and worked only under `flavor_catalog/`.
The assigned process is the neutron electric dipole moment, process id `E004`
in family `edm_neutrino`. I used PDG Live S017EDM as the canonical current
value source and checked it on 2026-05-16. The 2026 PDG datablock gives the
direct neutron EDM limit as `<0.18` in units of `10^-25 e cm`, i.e.
`|d_n| < 1.8e-26 e cm` at 90% CL, from Abel et al. 2020. PDG records the
underlying measurement as `d_n = (0.0 +/- 1.1 +/- 0.2)e-26 e cm`. I also noted
the superseded Pendlebury 2015 direct limit, `|d_n| < 3.0e-26 e cm` at 90% CL,
for the post-2008-development comparison.

Searches used included "PDG neutron electric dipole moment S017EDM",
"nEDM collaboration 2020 arXiv 2001.11966", "composite Higgs neutron EDM
quark chromomagnetic dipole", "warped neutron EDM wrong chirality Yukawa", and
"neutron EDM Weinberg three-gluon quark chromoelectric lattice". I saved
minimal text snapshots for the PDG datablock, the Abel 2020 arXiv record, the
required CFW 2008 RS-flavor context paper, Koenig/Neubert/Straub 2014 for
composite/warped dipole constraints, and Bhattacharya et al. 2022 for lattice
hadronic translation of theta, Weinberg, and quark chromo-EDM operators. I did
not cache publisher PDFs. ArXiv pages were used as text snapshots rather than
full PDFs.

For code coverage I ran the three required catalog greps and focused greps
over `quarkConstraints`, `qcd`, `flavorConstraints`, `neutrinos`, `yukawa`,
`warpConfig`, `solvers`, `scanParams`, and `tests` for neutron EDM, `d_n`,
CEDM, chromo-EDM, Weinberg, and three-gluon terms. No neutron EDM
implementation was found. The only adjacent matches are the existing
off-diagonal `mu -> e gamma` dipole helper in `flavorConstraints/muToEGamma.py`,
which is not a hadronic EDM calculation. I therefore marked code coverage
`NO` and implementation difficulty `HIGH`: a live constraint would need new
CP-odd operator matching, QCD running and threshold treatment, and neutron
matrix-element inputs. Open issue: writer/checker should decide how to split
theta, chromo-EDM, and Weinberg discussion across E004, E008, and E009.
