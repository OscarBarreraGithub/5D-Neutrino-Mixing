  `flavor_catalog/references/CR006/pdg2025_wprime_searches.txt`.
- ATLAS `arXiv:1906.05609`, full-Run-2 `W' -> l nu` search. Used for the
  6.0 TeV SSM electron-channel lower limit and ATLAS cross-section-limit
  context.
- CMS `arXiv:2202.06075`, full-Run-2 lepton plus missing-transverse-momentum
  search. Used for the CMS combined 5.7 TeV SSM limit and the PDG-canonical
  5.6 TeV muon-channel lower limit.

  current 4.3 TeV right-handed and 3.9 TeV left-handed narrow-width `tb`
  exclusions.

  post-2010 search-history context.
- ATLAS `arXiv:1801.07893` and `arXiv:1807.10473`, boosted all-hadronic and
  lepton-plus-jets `W' -> tb` searches. Used as earlier Run-2 `tb` history.
- Agashe et al. `arXiv:0810.1497`, warped charged electroweak KK gauge
  phenomenology. Used for RS model-dependence context and projection-scale
  inputs, not as measured observables.
- Agashe and Servant `hep-ph/0403143`, warped unification context. Included
  because the assignment named the Agashe-Servant warped baseline; not used as
