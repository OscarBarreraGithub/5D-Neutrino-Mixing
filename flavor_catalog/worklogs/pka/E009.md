# PKA Worklog: E009 Weinberg three-gluon operator

I followed the required catalog plan on branch `flavor-catalog/2026q2` and
worked only under `flavor_catalog/`. The assigned process is E009 in the
`edm_neutrino` family. I treated it as an EFT-interpretation entry, not a
directly measured observable: the PDG/equivalent block is reserved for the
measured neutron EDM anchor, while the Weinberg coefficient normalizations and
derived one-source-at-a-time bounds are in auxiliary YAML blocks.

Searches used included "Weinberg three gluon operator neutron EDM 20 MeV w",
"Weinberg operator neutron EDM global fit GeV^-2", "Haisch Hala sum rules
Weinberg operator neutron EDM", "Abel neutron EDM 2020 1.8e-26", "warped
composite Higgs Weinberg operator neutron EDM", and "lattice Weinberg
three-gluon operator gradient flow neutron EDM". I used PDG Live S017EDM and
Abel et al. 2020 for the measured neutron EDM input, Weinberg 1989 via INSPIRE
for the original operator, Pospelov--Ritz 2005 for the paper-era 22 MeV
benchmark normalization, Chupp/Ramsey-Musolf et al. 2014 for global-analysis
context, Koenig--Neubert--Straub 2014 for partial-compositeness/warped dipole
context, Haisch--Hala 2019 for the post-2008 sum-rule update, Bhattacharya et
al. 2022 for lattice-gradient-flow context, and CFW 2008 as the required
RS-flavor baseline. I saved only minimal text snapshots and metadata; no
publisher PDFs were cached.

For code coverage I ran focused Weinberg/neutron-EDM/CP-odd-gluon greps and
the broad catalog grep terms over `quarkConstraints`, `qcd`,
`flavorConstraints`, `neutrinos`, `yukawa`, `warpConfig`, `solvers`,
`scanParams`, and `tests`. No Weinberg-operator or hadronic EDM implementation
was found. The only nearby matches are the unrelated `mu -> e gamma` dipole
helper in `flavorConstraints/muToEGamma.py` plus existing Delta F=2 and generic
QCD-running vocabulary. I marked coverage `NO` and implementation difficulty
`HIGH`.

Unresolved issue: the two quoted Weinberg coefficient bounds are convention
dependent benchmark translations, not a correlated global EDM fit. WA/CA should
decide how prominently to display them and whether to privilege one
normalization.
