## Sources used

- PDG 2025 `B_s0` listing: canonical rounded average
  \(B(B_s^0\to\phi\gamma)=(3.4\pm0.4)\times10^{-5}\), plus PDG-listed
  \(S_{\phi\gamma}\), \(C_{\phi\gamma}\), and \(A^\Delta_{\phi\gamma}\).
- HFLAV rare decays Dec. 2024: more granular average
  \(B(B_s^0\to\phi\gamma)=(34.0\pm3.2)\times10^{-6}\), with the LHCb and Belle
  inputs.
- arXiv:1905.06284, LHCb 2019: current time-dependent CP/helicity observables,
  \(S_{\phi\gamma}=0.43\pm0.30\pm0.11\),
  \(C_{\phi\gamma}=0.11\pm0.29\pm0.11\), and
  \(A^\Delta_{\phi\gamma}=-0.67^{+0.37}_{-0.41}\pm0.17\).
- arXiv:1411.7771, Belle 2014: Belle \(B_s^0\to\phi\gamma\) measurement at
  \(\Upsilon(5S)\), one of the modern average inputs.
- arXiv:2406.00235, LHCb 2024: Run-1/Run-2 amplitude analysis of
  \(B_s^0\to K^+K^-\gamma\), useful for the resonant environment around
  \(\phi(1020)\gamma\).
- arXiv:2411.10219, LHCb 2024: very-low-\(q^2\)
  \(B_s^0\to\phi e^+e^-\) angular constraints on photon polarization.
- arXiv:0802.0876, Muheim-Xie-Zwicky: theory/formalism source for exploiting
  the \(B_s\) width difference and \(S/H\) helicity observables.
- arXiv:0804.1954, Csaki-Falkowski-Weiler: paper-era RS flavor context only.

## Sources considered and rejected

- Publisher PDFs were not tracked. For arXiv papers, local text snapshots of
  abstract-page content were sufficient.
- The earlier LHCb untagged \(A^\Delta_{\phi\gamma}\) result is superseded by
  the LHCb 2019 flavor-tagged analysis and kept only as PDG historical context,
  not as a headline observable.
- Older Belle/CDF/DELPHI limits and the original Belle 2008-era observation are
  superseded by PDG/HFLAV averages and were not separately snapshotted.
- HFLAV entries for \(B_s^0\to f_2\gamma\) and \(\phi(1680)\gamma\) are useful
  resonance context, but B013 remains the \(\phi(1020)\gamma\) process.
