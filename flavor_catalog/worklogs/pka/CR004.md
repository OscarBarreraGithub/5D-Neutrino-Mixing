## Post-2010 Search History

- `arXiv:1808.02343` (ATLAS): Run-2 combination of pair-produced
  third-generation VLQ partners over \(T\) and \(B\) decay modes; important
  because it established the branching-fraction-simplex presentation.
- `arXiv:2008.09835` (CMS): fully hadronic bottom-type VLQ pair search
  focusing on \(bH\) and \(bZ\); important because boosted hadronic
  reconstruction made the all-hadronic mode competitive.
- `arXiv:2209.07327` (CMS): leptonic VLQ pair search with single-lepton,
  same-sign dilepton, and multilepton categories; drives the current PDG
  \(B\to Wt\) pair-production corner.
- `arXiv:2210.15413` (ATLAS): \(Z+\)third-generation search; cross-check for
  \(B\to Zb\)-rich spectra.
- `arXiv:2212.05263` (ATLAS): one-lepton plus missing-energy search for
  pair-produced top and bottom partners; cross-check for \(Wt\)-rich spectra.
- `arXiv:2402.13808` (CMS): current CMS bottom-type VLQ search with
  dileptonic and fully hadronic categories; PDG source for the \(bH\) and
  \(bZ\) canonical limits and includes HEPData contours.
- `arXiv:2405.17605` (CMS): Run-2 review and search combination context; used
  as synthesis, not as a new primary measured observable.
