# PKA Worklog: E002 muon EDM

Process: E002, `d_mu`, family `edm_neutrino`.  I followed the catalog v1
instructions and treated the direct muon electric dipole moment as the
canonical measured observable.  The current PDG Live S004EDM datablock was
used for the primary value: `|d_mu| < 1.8 x 10^-19 e cm` at 95% CL.  I also
captured the PDG note that the same Bennett 2009 combination is presented as
`(0.0 +/- 0.9) x 10^-19 e cm`, and the arXiv abstract for Bennett et al.
quotes `d = -0.1(0.9) x 10^-19 e cm` with `|d| < 1.9 x 10^-19 e cm`.

Searches used: "PDG Live muon electric dipole moment", "BNL g-2 improved
limit muon electric dipole moment arXiv 0811.1207 value", and "PSI muEDM
proposal sensitivity muon electric dipole moment arXiv".  I used PDG Live,
arXiv:0811.1207 for the BNL E821 direct result, arXiv:2102.08838 for the PSI
frozen-spin proposal, arXiv:2409.20050 for the recent PSI status, and
arXiv:0804.1954 for the requested 2008 RS-flavor reference point.  I did not
download publisher PDFs.  I also did not promote the PDG-listed Ema 2022
indirect theory limit into `pdg_or_equivalent`; it is a theory-derived
indirect constraint and is explicitly separated from the direct experimental
average on the PDG page.

Code-coverage greps over `quarkConstraints/`, `qcd/`, `flavorConstraints/`,
`neutrinos/`, `yukawa/`, `warpConfig/`, `solvers/`, `scanParams/`, and
`tests/` found no muon-EDM implementation.  A broad `d_mu`-style grep produced
only false positives involving hadronic scales such as `m_d_mu_had_GeV`.  The
only nearby dipole code is the existing off-diagonal `mu->e gamma` constraint,
so I marked coverage as NO and implementation difficulty as HIGH.
