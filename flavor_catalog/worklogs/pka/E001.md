# PKA Worklog: E001 Electron EDM

I followed the required catalog plan and worked only under `flavor_catalog/`.
The assigned process is the electron electric dipole moment, process id `E001`
in family `edm_neutrino`. I used PDG Live as the canonical value source and
checked the current 2026 Review entry on 2026-05-16. The PDG S003EDM datablock
gives the current limit as `|d_e| < 4.1e-30 e cm` at 90% CL, from Roussy 2023,
and records the underlying measurement as `(-1.3 +/- 2.0 +/- 0.6)e-30 e cm`.
It also records the superseded ACME/Andreev 2018 ThO limit as
`|d_e| < 1.1e-29 e cm` at 90% CL.

Searches used included "PDG electron electric dipole moment limit",
"Roussy electron EDM arXiv 2212.11841", "ACME 2018 improved limit electron
electric dipole moment", and "electron EDM EFT two-loop Wilson coefficient".
I saved minimal text snapshots for the PDG datablock, the Roussy arXiv record
and extracted arXiv text, the CaltechAUTHORS ACME/Andreev metadata record, the
requested CFW 2008 RS-flavor context paper, and Panico/Pomarol/Riembau 2019
for EFT interpretation. I deliberately did not cache the Nature or Science
publisher PDFs. The JILA/Science publisher page and a JILA-hosted PDF were
consulted only as cross-checks and rejected for tracking under the licensing
policy.

For code coverage I ran the required catalog greps and a focused grep over
`quarkConstraints`, `qcd`, `flavorConstraints`, `neutrinos`, `yukawa`,
`warpConfig`, `solvers`, `scanParams`, and `tests` for electron-EDM terms.
No electron EDM implementation was found. The only nearby code is the existing
mu-to-e-gamma LFV dipole constraint, which is off-diagonal and process `L001`,
not this flavor-diagonal CP-odd EDM. Implementation is therefore marked HIGH:
it needs new CP-odd lepton dipole matching and likely EFT running. Open issue:
the writer/checker should decide how prominently to caveat semileptonic
CP-odd electron-nucleon operators in paramagnetic-molecule interpretations.
