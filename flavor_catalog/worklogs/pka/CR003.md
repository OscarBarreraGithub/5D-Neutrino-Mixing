## Sources considered but not promoted

- ATLAS/CMS single-production VLQ-T searches were not promoted because CR003 is explicitly pair production. They can reach higher masses for chosen electroweak couplings, but those are coupling- and width-dependent single-production interpretations.
- HEPData exclusion contours were not tracked because no contour point was used in the TeX/YAML numerical claims. If a later WA/implementation pass wants a branching-fraction-grid approximation, HEPData should be added then.
- PDG `t'(5/3)` rows were checked for context but not used because CR003 is the charge-2/3 `T` process.

## Post-2010 LHC search history

- `arXiv:1707.03347`: ATLAS 13 TeV lepton+jets search targeting `T -> Wb`; important as an early boosted-W Run-2 result.
- `arXiv:1803.09678`: ATLAS multi-b and boosted top/Higgs search for up-type VLQ pairs; important for Ht/Zt-rich branching patterns.
- `arXiv:1808.01771`: ATLAS all-hadronic heavy-VLQ pair search; important because it tests a different topology and backgrounds from lepton channels.
- `arXiv:2210.15413`: ATLAS full-Run-2 Z-lepton search; important for the pure `T -> Zt` PDG row.
- `arXiv:2209.07327`: CMS full-Run-2 leptonic combination; important for the all-branching-mixture statement.
- `arXiv:2401.17165`: ATLAS full-Run-2 lepton+jets `Wb` search; important as the current PDG-leading pure-Wb and singlet source.
