## Sources considered but not promoted

- PDG leptonic W' and Z' limits above the diboson reach were not promoted because CR012 is a diboson high-mass resonance entry, not a dilepton or lepton-plus-missing-energy search.
- Bulk-graviton and radion CMS rows were kept only as context. They are spin-2/spin-0, while CR012 is the generic spin-1 diboson entry.
- HEPData Figure 7 cross-section curves were not sampled point-by-point. A future recast should use the structured HEPData tables directly.

## Post-2010 developments

- `arXiv:1402.4431`: HVT framework; theory starting point for later ATLAS/CMS interpretations.
- `arXiv:1506.00962`: ATLAS 8 TeV boosted all-hadronic WW/WZ/ZZ search; established the channel and drove Run-2 follow-up.
- `arXiv:1705.09171`: CMS first combined VV/VH HVT interpretation across 8 and early 13 TeV searches.
- `arXiv:1708.04445`: ATLAS early 13 TeV all-hadronic boosted diboson search.
- `arXiv:1708.05379`: CMS early 13 TeV dijet search covering WW/WZ/ZZ and qV categories.
- `arXiv:1906.08589`: ATLAS full-Run-2 all-hadronic diboson search.
- `arXiv:1906.05977`: CMS partial-Run-2 all-jets search with a three-dimensional likelihood; direct predecessor of the current CMS result.
- `arXiv:2109.06055`: CMS Run-2 semileptonic WW/WZ/WH search, checking different backgrounds and systematics.
- `arXiv:2210.00043`: current CMS full-Run-2 all-jets result with ML boson taggers and simultaneous VV/VH categories.
