Open issues for WA/CA:

- The user prompt calls K020 a "charged-current LFV companion to K019", but the
  quark-level transition for `K+ -> pi+ e mu` is neutral-current-like
  `s -> d e mu`.  I used "charged-kaon companion to K019" in the prose while
  preserving the prompt's exact rationale string in the sidecar.
- A live implementation needs a choice between a general semileptonic LFV EFT
  basis and a narrower RS matching model with KK-gauge, shifted-Z, Higgs, or
  lepton-seesaw spurions.
