## Canonical Value

PDG 2025 \(K_L^0\) listing gives
\[
  {\cal B}(K_L\to\pi^0\mu^\pm e^\mp) < 7.6\times10^{-11}
\]
at 90% CL for the summed charge states.  The detailed PDG listing cites
ABOUZAID 08C / KTeV with `VALUE (units 10^-10) < 0.76`.  The KTeV arXiv
abstract reports the exact experiment value as
\({\cal B}(K_L\to\pi^0\mu e)<7.56\times10^{-11}\) at 90% CL with no signal
events.

The sidecar therefore puts the PDG-rounded limit in `pdg_or_equivalent` and
records the exact KTeV value as the experiment input under that block.  No
theory scales or dataset metadata were promoted to `pdg_or_equivalent`.
