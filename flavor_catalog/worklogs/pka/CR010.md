## Canonical current limits

Canonical CR010 doublet-specific observable:

- `m_T > 1.37 TeV` and `m_B > 1.37 TeV` at `95% CL`, for a weak-isospin `(T,B)` doublet with `|V_Tb| << |V_tB|`.
- Source: ATLAS Run-2 VLQ combination, `arXiv:1808.02343`, as recorded in the PDG 2025 `t'` and `b'` listings.
- Local snapshot: `flavor_catalog/references/CR010/pdg2025_tprime_bprime_vlq_limits.txt`.

Additional current simplified endpoints recorded in `pdg_or_equivalent.values` because they are measured mass-exclusion limits:

- `m_T > 1.70 TeV` at `95% CL` for `B(T -> W b) = 1` from ATLAS 2024 / PDG 2025.
- `m_B > 1.57 TeV`, `1.56 TeV`, and `1.54 TeV` at `95% CL` for `B(B -> H b) = 1`, `B(B -> W t) = 1`, and `B(B -> Z b) = 1`, respectively, from CMS/PDG 2025.
- `m_T > 1.48 TeV` at `95% CL` for all T decays to third-generation quarks in the CMS 2023 leptonic branching-fraction scan.

The simplified endpoints are not simultaneous `(T,B)` doublet exclusions; they are included as reach context and CHK-1-compliant measured limits.

## Post-2010 search history

- `arXiv:1301.4454`: early reinterpretation of exotic heavy-quark searches in arbitrary branching fractions; matters because `(T,B)` doublet limits are branching-fraction dependent.
- `arXiv:1706.03408`: early 13 TeV CMS boosted-object search; matters because it moved searches to LHC Run 2 topologies and quoted a doublet-scenario T limit.
- `arXiv:1808.02343`: ATLAS Run-2 combination; matters because it provides the canonical current simultaneous weak-isospin `(T,B)` doublet exclusion.
- `arXiv:2209.07327`: CMS full-Run-2 leptonic search; matters because it substantially improved simplified T/B branching-fraction reach and gives a uniform T lower edge over third-generation decays.
- `arXiv:2401.17165`: ATLAS full-Run-2 lepton+jets search; matters because it gives the current PDG `T -> Wb` simplified endpoint of 1.70 TeV.
- `arXiv:2405.17605`: CMS review; matters as a current summary and projection source, not a standalone measured limit promoted to `pdg_or_equivalent`.
