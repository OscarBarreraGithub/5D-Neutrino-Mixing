Minimal extracted values from the arXiv abstract:
COMET Phase-I searches for neutrinoless conversion of muons into electrons in
the field of an aluminium nucleus, mu- N -> e- N.  The experimental
sensitivity goal is 3.1 x 10^-15, with a 90% upper limit on the branching
ratio of 7 x 10^-15.  The abstract describes this as a factor of 100
improvement over the existing limit and quotes an expected background of 0.032
events.

Use in L004:
Post-2008 development and aluminum-target companion context for the persistent
gold-target SINDRUM II benchmark.
