Heading: LIMIT ON MUONIUM -> ANTIMUONIUM CONVERSION
PDG note: Forbidden by lepton family number conservation.

PDG observable:
R_g = G_C / G_F

PDG effective-Lagrangian convention:
The effective Lagrangian for the mu+ e- -> mu- e+ conversion is assumed to be
L = 2^(-1/2) G_C [bar psi_mu gamma_lambda (1 - gamma_5) psi_e]
                    [bar psi_mu gamma_lambda (1 - gamma_5) psi_e] + h.c.
The experimental result is then an upper limit on G_C/G_F, where G_F is the
Fermi coupling constant.
