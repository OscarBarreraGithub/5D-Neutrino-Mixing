Minimal extracted use note:
The abstract classifies muonium-antimuonium conversion as part of the
Delta L_mu = - Delta L_e = 2 charged-lepton-flavor-violation class. It notes
that this case is sensitive to two out of the three relevant dimension-six
operators in that flavor class.

Catalog use:
Post-2008 theory context and implementation-difficulty classification. This is
not the source for the current experimental limit.
