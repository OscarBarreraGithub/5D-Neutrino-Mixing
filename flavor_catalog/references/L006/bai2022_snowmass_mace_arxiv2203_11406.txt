Minimal extracted use note:
The whitepaper identifies spontaneous muonium-to-antimuonium conversion as a
charged-lepton-flavor-violation process and summarizes post-2008 theoretical
and experimental developments. It states that MACE is proposed to improve the
sensitivity to the conversion probability by more than two orders of magnitude
relative to the current PSI/MACS upper constraint.

Catalog use:
Post-2008 developments and prospects only. This is not the source for the
current experimental limit.
