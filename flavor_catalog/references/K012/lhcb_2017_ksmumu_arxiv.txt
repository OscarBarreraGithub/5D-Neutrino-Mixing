Title: Improved limit on the branching fraction of the rare decay K0_S -> mu+ mu-
Authors: LHCb collaboration, R. Aaij et al.
Submitted: 2017-06-02; revised version v3: 2017-10-18
Report numbers: CERN-EP-2017-101, LHCb-PAPER-2017-009
Journal reference: Eur. Phys. J. C 77, 678 (2017)
DOI: 10.1140/epjc/s10052-017-5230-x

Minimal abstract excerpt:
A search for K0_S -> mu+ mu- is performed using a data sample of proton-proton
collisions corresponding to an integrated luminosity of 3 fb^-1, collected by
LHCb at center-of-mass energies of 7 and 8 TeV. The observed yield is consistent
with the background-only hypothesis, yielding
B(K0_S -> mu+ mu-) < 0.8 (1.0) x 10^-9 at 90% (95%) confidence level.
This result improves the previous upper limit on the branching fraction by an
order of magnitude.
