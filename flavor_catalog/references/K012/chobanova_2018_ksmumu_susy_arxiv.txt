Title: Probing SUSY effects in K_S^0 -> mu+ mu-
Authors: Veronika Chobanova, Giancarlo D'Ambrosio, Teppei Kitahara,
Miriam Lucio Martinez, Diego Martinez Santos, Isabel Suarez Fernandez,
Kei Yamamoto
Submitted: 2017-11-29; revised version v2: 2018-05-28
Journal reference: JHEP 05 (2018) 024
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP05(2018)024

Minimal abstract excerpt:
The Standard Model predicts B(K_S^0 -> mu+ mu-) approximately 5 x 10^-12.
Flavour-violating Higgs-penguin contributions in MSSM scenarios can enhance the
branching fraction up to approximately 35 x 10^-12 or suppress it to
approximately 0.78 x 10^-12. Fine-tuned regions can bring the branching
fraction up to the then-current experimental upper bound, 8 x 10^-10.
The paper also discusses correlations with B(K_L^0 -> mu+ mu-) and CP-asymmetry
predictions in K0 -> mu+ mu- decays.
