Title: K -> mu+ mu- as a clean probe of short-distance physics
Authors: Avital Dery, Mitrajyoti Ghosh, Yuval Grossman, Stefan Schacht
Submitted: 2021-04-13; revised version v2: 2021-07-27
Journal reference: JHEP 07 (2021) 103
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP07(2021)103

Minimal abstract excerpt:
K -> mu+ mu- is often considered uninformative for fundamental parameters
because the decay is polluted by long-distance hadronic effects. Using mild
assumptions and time-dependent interference effects, B(K_S -> mu+ mu-)_{ell=0}
can be experimentally determined without separating ell=0 and ell=1 final
states. This quantity is theoretically clean and can be used to test the
Standard Model. It can extract the CKM combination
|V_ts V_td sin(beta + beta_s)| approximately |A^2 lambda^5 eta_bar| with
hadronic uncertainties below 1%.
