Title: Constraints on the K0_S -> mu+ mu- branching fraction
Authors: LHCb collaboration, R. Aaij et al.
Submitted: 2020-01-28; revised version v2: 2021-02-01
Report numbers: LHCb-PAPER-2019-038, CERN-EP-2019-285
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. Lett. 125, 231801 (2020)
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.125.231801

Minimal abstract excerpt:
A search for K0_S -> mu+ mu- is performed using proton-proton collision data
corresponding to an integrated luminosity of 5.6 fb^-1, collected with the LHCb
experiment during 2016, 2017 and 2018 at a center-of-mass energy of 13 TeV.
The observed signal yield is consistent with zero, yielding
B(K0_S -> mu+ mu-) < 2.2 x 10^-10 at 90% CL.
The limit reduces to B(K0_S -> mu+ mu-) < 2.1 x 10^-10 at 90% CL once combined
with the result from data taken in 2011 and 2012.
