Title: Search for flavour-changing neutral-current couplings between the top quark and the photon with the ATLAS detector at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV.
Journal: Phys. Lett. B 842 (2023) 137379.
DOI: 10.1016/j.physletb.2022.137379
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2205.02537
Access date: 2026-05-16
Local snapshot policy: minimal arXiv abstract/value extract only; no PDF stored.

Relevant catalog values:
- Dataset: pp collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV with 139 fb^-1.
- Search mode: photon plus top-quark final state; both FCNC production and
  FCNC top decay are considered.
- No significant excess over the background prediction was observed.
- 95% CL observed bound, left-handed tqgamma coupling:
  B(t -> u gamma) < 0.85 x 10^-5.
- 95% CL observed bound, right-handed tqgamma coupling:
  B(t -> u gamma) < 1.2 x 10^-5.

Notes for T004:
- This is the tightest flavor-separated t -> u gamma limit found for this PKA
  pass and is the headline T004 experimental constraint.
