Title: Search for flavor changing neutral current interactions of the top quark in final states with a photon and additional jets in proton-proton collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV.
Journal: Phys. Rev. D 109 (2024) 072004.
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.109.072004
URL: https://cms-results.web.cern.ch/cms-results/public-results/publications/TOP-21-013/
Access date: 2026-05-16
Local snapshot policy: minimal public-result text extract only; no PDF stored.

Relevant catalog values:
- Dataset: pp collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV with 138 fb^-1.
- Search mode: single top plus photon and ttbar events with one FCNC top decay.
- No significant deviation from the standard-model background prediction was observed.
- 95% CL observed expected upper limits:
  B(t -> u gamma) < 0.95 x 10^-5 observed, 1.20 x 10^-5 expected.
- CMS notes that the t -> u gamma bound is similar to the current best limit.

Notes for T004:
- This is the most recent primary CMS result found for the mode, but its
  observed t -> u gamma limit is slightly weaker than the ATLAS benchmark.
