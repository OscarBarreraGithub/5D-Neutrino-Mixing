Title: Precise Measurements of Direct CP Violation, CPT Symmetry, and Other Parameters in the Neutral Kaon System
Authors: KTeV Collaboration, E. Abouzaid et al.
Year: 2011
Local purpose: Post-2008 experimental input used by the PDG K002 fit.

Relevant arXiv abstract snapshot:

  The analysis uses the full KTeV data set of K -> pi pi decays collected at
  Fermilab during 1996, 1997, and 1999.  The abstract reports
  Delta m = (5270 +/- 12) x 10^6 hbar/s and tau_S = (89.62 +/- 0.05) x
  10^-12 s.  The paper also reports Re(epsilon'/epsilon) = (19.2 +/- 2.1) x
  10^-4 and CPT-test quantities, including a 95% C.L. bound
  Delta M(K0-K0bar) < 4.8 x 10^-19 GeV/c^2.
