Title: Calculation of the K_L-K_S mass difference for physical quark masses
Authors: Bigeng Wang
Year: 2020
Local purpose: Physical-quark-mass status report for the lattice Delta M_K
program.

Relevant arXiv abstract snapshot:

  The article presents the status of a Standard Model calculation of Delta m_K
  on an ensemble of 152, 64^3 x 128 gauge configurations with physical quark
  masses and inverse lattice spacing 2.36 GeV.  It discusses analysis methods
  and progress toward a final result.
