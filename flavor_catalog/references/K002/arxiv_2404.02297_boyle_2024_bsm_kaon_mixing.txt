Title: Kaon mixing beyond the standard model with physical masses
Authors: Peter A. Boyle, Felix Erben, Jonathan M. Flynn, Nicolas Garron, Julia Kettle, Rajnandini Mukherjee, J. Tobias Tsang
Year: 2024
Local purpose: Post-2008 lattice input for BSM Delta S = 2 kaon-mixing matrix
elements relevant to interpreting K002 as a new-physics constraint.

Relevant arXiv abstract snapshot:

  The paper presents non-perturbative results for BSM kaon-mixing matrix
  elements in the isospin-symmetric limit with N_f = 2+1 dynamical
  domain-wall fermions.  It simulates directly at the physical pion mass
  m_pi approximately 139 MeV for two lattice spacings and includes three
  lattice spacings in the range a = 0.11 - 0.07 fm.

  Bag-parameter results in the MSbar scheme at 3 GeV:
    B_K equivalent B_1 = 0.5240(17)(54)
    B_2 = 0.4794(25)(35)
    B_3 = 0.746(13)(17)
    B_4 = 0.897(02)(10)
    B_5 = 0.6882(78)(94)
  The first error is from lattice uncertainties and the second from
  perturbative matching to MSbar.
