Title: Long distance contribution to the K_L-K_S mass difference
Authors: N. H. Christ, T. Izubuchi, C. T. Sachrajda, A. Soni, J. Yu
Year: 2013
Local purpose: First post-2008 lattice method paper for the long-distance part
of Delta M_K.

Relevant arXiv abstract snapshot:

  The paper develops and demonstrates techniques needed to compute the
  long-distance contribution to Delta M_K in lattice QCD and performs a first
  exploratory calculation.  The setup uses 2+1 flavor domain-wall fermion
  16^3 x 32 configurations with a 421 MeV pion mass and inverse lattice
  spacing 1/a = 1.73 GeV.  The exploratory result gives Delta M_K values from
  6.58(30) x 10^-12 MeV to 11.89(81) x 10^-12 MeV for kaon masses from
  563 MeV to 839 MeV, and proposes extensions toward accurate Delta M_K and
  epsilon_K long-distance calculations.
