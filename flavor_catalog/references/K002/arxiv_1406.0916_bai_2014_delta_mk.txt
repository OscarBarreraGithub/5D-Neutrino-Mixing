Title: K_L-K_S mass difference from lattice QCD
Authors: Z. Bai, N. H. Christ, T. Izubuchi, C. T. Sachrajda, A. Soni, J. Yu
Year: 2014
Local purpose: First complete lattice-QCD calculation of Delta M_K.

Relevant arXiv abstract snapshot:

  The authors report the first complete lattice-QCD calculation of the
  K_L-K_S mass difference.  The calculation is performed with 2+1 flavor
  domain-wall fermions, a 330 MeV pion mass, a 575 MeV kaon mass, and a
  quenched charm quark of 949 MeV to implement GIM cancellation.  For those
  heavier-than-physical masses, the result is Delta M_K =
  3.19(41)(96) x 10^-12 MeV, where the first error is statistical and the
  second estimates systematic discretization error.  The abstract emphasizes
  that disconnected diagrams are important.
