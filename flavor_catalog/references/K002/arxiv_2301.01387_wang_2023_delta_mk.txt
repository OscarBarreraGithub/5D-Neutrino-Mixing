Title: Calculating Delta m_K with lattice QCD
Authors: Bigeng Wang
Year: 2023
Local purpose: Later proceedings update on physical-mass Delta m_K lattice
calculation and remaining systematics.

Relevant arXiv abstract snapshot:

  The work reports completion of a lattice-QCD calculation of Delta m_K on a
  64^3 x 128 lattice using 152 configurations with physical quark masses and
  inverse lattice spacing 1/a = 2.36 GeV.  The abstract states that the
  statistical error approaches 9%, while several sources of systematic error
  may have more significant effects.
