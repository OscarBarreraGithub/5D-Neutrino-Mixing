Relevant process-context note:

This is the requested paper-era reference point for warped/anarchic flavor.
It discusses generic flavor constraints on KK gluons and the composite
pseudo-Goldstone Higgs setup. The paper is used here as baseline context
for what was known before the LHC top-FCNC single-top program. It does not
provide the current T006 t -> u g experimental limit.

Theoretical/model-normalization numbers from this source belong in the
YAML paper_era_references section, not in pdg_or_equivalent measured
observables. Relevant examples retained for context are the ordinary-RS
KK-gluon scale near 21 TeV and the composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs setup
scale near 33 TeV.
