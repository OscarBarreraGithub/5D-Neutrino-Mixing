Relevant TeX-source excerpt:

The review gives Br(t -> c g) = (4.6^{+1.1}_{-0.9} +/- 0.2 +/- 0.4
^{+2.1}_{-0.7}) x 10^{-12}. It also states that t -> u Z, t -> u gamma,
t -> u g, and t -> u H are smaller than the corresponding charm modes by
|V_ub / V_cb|^2 approximately 0.0079.

Using the review's stated scaling, the corresponding Standard Model estimate
for t -> u g is approximately 3.6e-14. The source emphasizes that top FCNC
interactions are negligible in the Standard Model but can be enhanced in
extensions, so an observed t -> u g signal would indicate new physics.
