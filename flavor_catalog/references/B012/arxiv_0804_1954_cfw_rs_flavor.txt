Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
Published: 2008-04-14
Updated: 2008-04-24
Journal: JHEP 0809:008, 2008
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008
Category: hep-ph

Abstract excerpt:
The paper studies flavor structure in 5D warped models and re-examines flavor constraints in Randall-Sundrum scenarios. It finds that the KK gluon mass should generically be heavier than about 21 TeV in standard RS-type scenarios, and about 33 TeV in the composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs scenario. It emphasizes RS-GIM suppression, additional flavor violation from kinetic mixing, and the need for partial flavor symmetries if fully anarchic flavor is to remain plausible.
