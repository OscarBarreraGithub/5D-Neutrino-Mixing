Title: Observation of photon polarization in the b -> s gamma transition
Authors: R. Aaij et al. (LHCb Collaboration)
Published: 2014-02-27
Updated: 2014-05-01
Journal: Phys. Rev. Lett. 112, 161801 (2014)
Category: hep-ex

Abstract/value excerpt:
LHCb studies the FCNC radiative decays B+/- -> K+/- pi-/+ pi+/- gamma using 3 fb^-1 of pp collision data at 7 and 8 TeV. The sample contains nearly 14,000 selected signal events with K pi pi masses in [1.1, 1.9] GeV/c^2. The photon-direction angle relative to the hadron plane is studied in mass bins.

Reported result:
  First direct observation of photon polarization in the b -> s gamma transition with significance 5.2 sigma.

Catalog relevance:
This is a post-2008 experimental handle on photon helicity, complementary to time-dependent S_{K* gamma}.
