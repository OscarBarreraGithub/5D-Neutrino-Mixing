Relevant PDG notes:
TUMASYAN 2022A is based on 137 fb^-1 at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV of pp data. The
processes considered include both associated production of a single top quark
with a Higgs boson and the decay t -> H u in ttbar production using
H -> gamma gamma.

HAYRAPETYAN 2025AD is based on 138 fb^-1 at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV of pp data.
Events with t and H are considered, produced via ttbar or single-top
production and anomalous Hut couplings.

AAD 2024AU is based on 140 fb^-1 at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV of pp data. The
processes considered include both associated production of a single top quark
with a Higgs boson and the decay t -> H u in ttbar production using either
three or five Higgs decay modes, depending on the row.
