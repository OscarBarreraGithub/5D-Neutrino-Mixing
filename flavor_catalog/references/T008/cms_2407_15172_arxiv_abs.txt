Title: Search for flavor-changing neutral current interactions of the top
quark mediated by a Higgs boson in proton-proton collisions at 13 TeV.
Authors: CMS Collaboration.
Submitted: 2024-07-21; last revised: 2025-08-21.
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. D 112 (2025) 032008.
Related DOI: 10.1103/95q6-vvlp.

Relevant abstract excerpt:
The search uses pp collision data collected in 2016-2018 at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV,
corresponding to 138 fb^-1. Same-sign lepton-pair events with at least one jet
are considered.

No significant excess is observed. The same-sign search observed (expected)
95% CL limits are B(t -> H u) < 0.072% (0.059%) and
B(t -> H c) < 0.043% (0.062%).

Combining with CMS searches in H -> gamma gamma and H -> b b final states gives
observed (expected) 95% CL limits B(t -> H u) < 0.019% (0.027%) and
B(t -> H c) < 0.037% (0.035%).
