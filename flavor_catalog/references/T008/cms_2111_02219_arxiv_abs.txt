Title: Search for flavor-changing neutral current interactions of the top
quark and Higgs boson in final states with two photons in proton-proton
collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV.
Authors: CMS Collaboration.
Submitted: 2021-11-03; last revised: 2022-07-14.
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. Lett. 129 (2022) 032001.
Related DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.129.032001.

Relevant abstract excerpt:
The analysis uses pp collision data collected at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV with the CMS
detector, corresponding to 137 fb^-1. Final states with two photons are used
to search for top-Higgs FCNC signatures.

No significant excess is observed. The observed (expected) 95% CL upper limits
are B(t -> H u) < 0.019% (0.031%) and
B(t -> H c) < 0.073% (0.051%). The abstract describes these as the strictest
limits yet determined at the time of publication.
