Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
Journal reference: JHEP 09 (2008) 008
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008

Relevant abstract extract:
  The paper studies flavor constraints in 5D warped models with a
  composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs. It re-examines flavor constraints
  in standard Randall-Sundrum scenarios and finds that KK gluon masses
  should generically be heavier than about 21 TeV. In the composite
  pseudo-Goldstone scenario, kinetic mixing and gauge-sized Yukawa
  couplings can make bounds more stringent, with KK gluon masses at
  least about 33 TeV. These are paper-era theory-normalization and
  model-context numbers, not measured observables.
