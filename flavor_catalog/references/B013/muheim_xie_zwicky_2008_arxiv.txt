Title: Exploiting the width difference in B_s -> phi gamma
Authors: Franz Muheim, Yuehong Xie, Roman Zwicky
Journal reference: Phys. Lett. B 664, 174-179 (2008)
DOI: 10.1016/j.physletb.2008.05.032

Relevant abstract extract:
  Photon polarization in B -> V gamma is a sensitive probe of
  right-handed currents. In the time-dependent B_s -> phi gamma decay
  rate, the coefficients S and H multiplying the sin(Delta m_s t) and
  sinh(Delta Gamma_s t / 2) terms are sensitive to right-handed
  currents. The sizable B_s width difference gives the additional
  measurable observable H, which can be measured from the untagged
  B_s decay rate.

  The paper quotes Standard Model predictions
    S_phi gamma = 0 +/- 0.002
    H_phi gamma = 0.047 +/- 0.025 +0.015
  and discusses LHCb sensitivity.
