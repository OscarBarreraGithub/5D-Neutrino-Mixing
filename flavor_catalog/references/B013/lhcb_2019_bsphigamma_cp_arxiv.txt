Title: Measurement of CP-violating and mixing-induced observables in
  B_s^0 -> phi gamma decays
Authors: LHCb Collaboration, R. Aaij et al.
Report number: LHCb-PAPER-2019-015, CERN-EP-2019-077
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. Lett. 123, 081802 (2019)
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.123.081802

Relevant abstract extract:
  A time-dependent analysis of the B_s^0 -> phi gamma decay rate is
  performed to determine the CP-violating observables S_phi gamma and
  C_phi gamma, and the mixing-induced observable A^Delta_phi gamma.
  The measurement is based on 3 fb^-1 of pp collision data recorded by
  LHCb at center-of-mass energies of 7 and 8 TeV.

  Measured values:
    S_phi gamma = 0.43 +/- 0.30 +/- 0.11
    C_phi gamma = 0.11 +/- 0.29 +/- 0.11
    A^Delta_phi gamma = -0.67 +0.37 -0.41 +/- 0.17
  The first uncertainty is statistical and the second systematic. This
  is the first measurement of S and C in radiative B_s^0 decays, and
  the results are consistent with Standard Model predictions.
