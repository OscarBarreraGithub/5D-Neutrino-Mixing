Title:
The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs

Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
Submitted: 2008-04-14; last revised: 2008-04-24, version v2
Journal reference: JHEP 0809 (2008) 008
Related DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008
arXiv DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.0804.1954

Relevant abstract excerpt:

The paper studies the flavor structure of 5D warped models that provide a
dual description of a composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs. It re-examines flavor
constraints in standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios and finds that the KK
gluon mass should generically be heavier than about 21 TeV. The authors find
additional sources of flavor violation in the composite-Higgs setup, including
kinetic mixing among SM fields, and conclude that fully anarchic warped flavor
is strongly constrained unless partial flavor symmetries remove some sources
of flavor violation.
