Title:
Top quark FCNC in Randall-Sundrum models: post-LHC allowed rates and searches
at e+e- and mu+mu- colliders

Authors: Sagar Airen, Roberto Franceschini
Submitted: 2026-01-21
Report number: CERN-TH-2025-236
arXiv DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.2601.14966

Relevant abstract excerpt:

The paper presents sensitivity to flavor-changing neutral currents in
interactions involving the top quark at future e+e- and mu+mu- machines.
It considers the Ztc vertex and four-fermion contact interactions involving
top and charm quarks. To incorporate limits from the LHC and HL-LHC, it
considers FCNC from Randall-Sundrum models and recasts LHC searches for the
resonances that give rise to the FCNC effects.

The abstract reports that LHC currently improves on the limit set by previous
machines, including LEP indirect sensitivity to heavy vectors. Future direct
searches at HL-LHC may reach a level equivalent to
BR(t -> c Z) approximately 10^-6. It also studies sensitivity at a Higgs
factory with E_cm in [200,240] GeV and at a high-energy muon collider with
E_cm = 10 TeV.
