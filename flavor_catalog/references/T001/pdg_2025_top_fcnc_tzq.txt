PDG note 1:
AAD 23AS is based on 139 fb^-1 of pp data at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV. Anomalous
FCNC left-handed and right-handed couplings are searched for through single-top
production in association with a Z boson and in the decay of a top quark in
ttbar production. Events with three leptons plus at least one jet, one b-tag,
and missing transverse momentum are used. The SM predictions of the
corresponding branching ratios are of the order of 10^-14.

PDG note 2:
SIRUNYAN 17E is based on 19.7 fb^-1 of pp data at sqrt(s) = 8 TeV. The final
states ttbar -> l+ l- l' +/- nu + jets are investigated and the cross section
sigma(pp -> t Z q -> l nu b l+ l- q) = 10^{+8}_{-7} fb is measured, giving
no sign of FCNC decays of the top quark.
