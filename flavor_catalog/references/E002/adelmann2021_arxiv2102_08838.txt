Title: Search for a muon EDM using the frozen-spin technique.
Authors: A. Adelmann et al.
Submitted: 2021-02-17.
arXiv DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.2102.08838.

Proposal context:
  The paper is based on a letter of intent submitted to the PSI CHRISP research
  committee in January 2021. It proposes a frozen-spin muon-EDM search at PSI.

Numerical proposal values from the arXiv abstract:
  Muon momentum: p = 125 MeV/c.
  Magnetic field magnitude: |B| = 3 T.
  Rest-frame electric field: E = 1 GV/m.
  Expected sensitivity at the PSI muE1 beam line: sigma(d_mu) <= 6 x 10^-23 e cm.
  Current direct limit quoted in the abstract: |d_mu| <= 1.8 x 10^-19 e cm at 95% CL.
