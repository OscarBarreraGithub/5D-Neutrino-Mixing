Abstract summary relevant to flavor-catalog context:
  The paper studies flavor in 5D warped models, re-examines flavor constraints
  on the new-physics scale in Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios, and reports that
  generic RS KK-gluon masses are constrained above about 21 TeV. It emphasizes
  RS-GIM suppression, additional sources of flavor violation, and the possible
  need for partial flavor symmetries if fully anarchic flavor is too constrained.
