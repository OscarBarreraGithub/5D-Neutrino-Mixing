Observable: mu electric dipole moment, d.
PDG note: A nonzero value is forbidden by both T invariance and P invariance.

Datablock: S004EDM
Table units: VALUE (10^-19 e cm), CL%.

Canonical direct experimental result:
  <1.8, CL = 95%, Bennett 2009, method MUG2 +/- storage ring.
Converted value:
  |d_mu| < 1.8 x 10^-19 e cm at 95% CL.

PDG notes on the Bennett 2009 entries:
  The canonical limit is the combination of the mu+ and mu- Bennett 2009 measurements.
  The combined result is also presented as a measurement of (0.0 +/- 0.9) x 10^-19 e cm.
  The mu+ entry is -0.1 +/- 1.0 in units of 10^-19 e cm, also reported as |d(mu+)| < 2.1 x 10^-19 e cm at 95% CL.
  The mu- entry is -0.1 +/- 0.7 in units of 10^-19 e cm, also reported as |d(mu-)| < 1.5 x 10^-19 e cm at 95% CL.

PDG non-averaged/theory entry not used as the canonical measured observable:
  Ema 2022 gives <0.19 in units of 10^-19 e cm at 90% CL as an indirect theory limit.
  The PDG page states this entry is not used for averages, fits, limits, etc.
