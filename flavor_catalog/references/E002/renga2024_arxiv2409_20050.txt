Title: Status of the muEDM experiment at PSI.
Authors: Francesco Renga for the muEDM collaboration.
Submitted: 2024-09-30.
Comments: Proceedings for ICHEP 2024.
arXiv DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.2409.20050.

Status context:
  The proceedings describes the PSI muEDM experiment as a frozen-spin search.
  Muons are to be stored in a solenoid with a radial electric field tuned to
  suppress magnetic-moment spin precession; residual longitudinal precession
  would indicate a nonzero EDM.

Numerical/prospect statements from the arXiv abstract:
  The first phase is intended to demonstrate feasibility by 2026 while reaching
  sensitivity competitive with parasitic measurements in muon g-2 experiments.
  The ultimate goal is to improve that sensitivity by a factor of 100 by the early 2030s.
