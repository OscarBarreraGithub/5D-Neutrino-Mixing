Title: An Improved Limit on the Muon Electric Dipole Moment.
Authors: G.W. Bennett et al. (Muon g-2 Collaboration).
Submitted: 2008-11-07. Last revised: 2009-07-26, v2.
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. D 80, 052008 (2009).
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.80.052008.
arXiv DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.0811.1207.

Experiment and method:
  Three searches for mu+ and mu- EDMs were performed with spin-precession data
  from the Brookhaven National Laboratory E821 muon g-2 storage ring.

Numerical results from the arXiv abstract:
  Combined measurement: d = -0.1(0.9) x 10^-19 e cm.
  Published direct limit: |d| < 1.9 x 10^-19 e cm at 95% CL.
  The abstract states that this is a factor-5 improvement over the previous best muon-EDM limit.

Use in E002:
  This is the primary experimental paper behind the PDG direct muon-EDM limit.
  PDG 2026 rounds/combines the direct bound as |d_mu| < 1.8 x 10^-19 e cm at 95% CL.
