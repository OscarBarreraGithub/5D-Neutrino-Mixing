Relevant extraction:
The paper treats B(K^+ -> pi^+ nu nubar) and B(K_L -> pi^0 nu nubar) as
theoretically clean quantities for CKM determinations, and emphasizes the
CP-violating neutral mode as a precise probe of Im(V_ts^* V_td) and the
Jarlskog invariant J_CP.
