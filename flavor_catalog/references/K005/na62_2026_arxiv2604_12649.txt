Source: Xiafei Chang for the NA62 Collaboration
Title: New measurement of K^+ -> pi^+ nu nubar branching ratio at the
NA62 experiment
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12649
Accessed: 2026-05-16
arXiv: 2604.12649v3 [hep-ex]
DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.2604.12649

Relevant extracted numbers:
Dataset: NA62 data collected in 2016--2024.
Measured branching fraction:
B(K^+ -> pi^+ nu nubar) = (9.6 +1.9/-1.8)e-11.
The source states the result is compatible with the SM prediction with
precision better than 20%.

Catalog use: related charged golden-mode input, relevant for the
Grossman-Nir relation and the K -> pi nu nubar program, not a direct
measurement of K_L -> pi^0 nu nubar.
