Relevant extracted values:
- Flavor-tagged time-dependent angular analysis of B_s^0 -> phi phi using
  pp collision data at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV, integrated luminosity 6 fb^-1.
- Helicity-independent fit:
  phi_s^{s sbar s} = -0.042 +/- 0.075(stat) +/- 0.009(syst) rad.
  |lambda| = 1.004 +/- 0.030(stat) +/- 0.009(syst).
- Polarization-dependent determinations showed no evidence for polarization
  dependence.
- Combination with previous LHCb 7 and 8 TeV measurements:
  phi_s^{s sbar s} = -0.074 +/- 0.069 rad.
  |lambda| = 1.009 +/- 0.030.
- CDS records this as the most precise study of time-dependent CP violation
  in a penguin-dominated B meson decay, consistent with CP symmetry and SM
  predictions.
