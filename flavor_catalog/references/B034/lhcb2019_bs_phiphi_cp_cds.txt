Relevant extracted values:
- Time-dependent CP-violating asymmetry in B_s^0 -> phi phi.
- Data sample: 5.0 fb^-1 collected at sqrt(s) = 7 TeV in 2011,
  8 TeV in 2012, and 13 TeV in 2015 and 2016.
- Signal yield: around 9000 B_s^0 -> phi phi decays.
- Helicity-independent phase:
  phi_s^{s sbar s} = -0.073 +/- 0.115(stat) +/- 0.027(syst) rad.
- Helicity-independent direct-CP parameter:
  |lambda| = 0.99 +/- 0.05(stat) +/- 0.01(syst).
- B^0 -> phi phi limit:
  B(B^0 -> phi phi) < 2.7 x 10^-8 at 90% confidence level.
- Results were consistent with CP conservation in b -> sbar s sbar
  transitions in the CDS text convention.
