Relevant extracted context:
- The paper re-examines flavor constraints in Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios.
- It reports that generic RS KK gluons should be heavier than about 21 TeV,
  and in the composite pseudo-Goldstone setup at least about 33 TeV, subject
  to assumptions such as gluon boundary kinetic terms.
- It frames the 2008-era baseline: warped anarchic flavor is highly
  constrained and motivates cataloging additional flavor observables beyond
  the Delta F = 2 neutral-meson mixing lane.
