Title: Search for the lepton-flavour violating decay D0 -> e^\pm mu^\mp
Authors: LHCb Collaboration
Journal reference: Phys. Lett. B754 (2016) 167
DOI: 10.1016/j.physletb.2016.01.029
Submitted: 2015-12-01
Last revised: 2016-01-26

Extracted abstract facts:
  A search for D0 -> e^\pm mu^\mp used 3.0 fb^-1 of proton-proton collision
  data collected by LHCb at centre-of-mass energies of 7 TeV and 8 TeV.
  Candidate D0 mesons were selected using D*+ -> D0 pi+, and the branching
  fraction was measured using D0 -> K- pi+ as a normalisation channel.
  No significant excess over expected background was seen.
  The reported limit is
    B(D0 -> e^\pm mu^\mp) < 1.3 x 10^-8 at 90% confidence level.
  The abstract states this is an order of magnitude lower than the previous
  limit and constrains some leptoquark and R-parity-violating supersymmetry
  parameter space.
