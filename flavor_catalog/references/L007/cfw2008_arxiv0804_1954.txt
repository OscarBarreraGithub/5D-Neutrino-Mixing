Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
Journal: JHEP 0809 (2008) 008
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008
Submitted: 2008-04-14; last revised: 2008-04-24

Relevant excerpt:

The paper studies the flavor structure of 5D warped models and re-examines
flavor constraints on the mass scale of new physics in standard
Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios. It reports that the KK gluon mass should
generically be heavier than about 21 TeV, and argues that fully anarchic
warped flavor is implausible without partial flavor symmetries that remove
some sources of flavor violation.
