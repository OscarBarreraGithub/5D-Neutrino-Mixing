The g_s*=3 headline crossings are M_KK = 47.26 TeV at 50% acceptance and
127.13 TeV at 95% acceptance. The corresponding perturbative code-default
crossings are 16.54 TeV and 44.50 TeV. These are quoted from the post-audit
8M-draw RUNA reference scan under the input and scope conventions summarized
in the methodology-note appendices.

Later in the note, the baseline RS-anarchy scan is summarized as:

M_KK^min at 50% acceptance = 16.54 TeV (perturbative convention) and
47.26 TeV (g_s*=3).

M_KK^min at 95% acceptance = 44.50 TeV (perturbative convention) and
127.13 TeV (g_s*=3).

The corresponding finite-Monte-Carlo Wilson-score 95% intervals include
47.19--47.32 TeV at 50% acceptance and 126.88--127.38 TeV at 95%
acceptance for g_s*=3.
