PDG explanatory notes:

AAD 24N is based on 140 fb^-1 of pp data at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV. Pair
production of vector-like t' is searched for in the lepton+jets mode. The
data are consistent with the SM background predictions and limits are
obtained for different branching ratios. The >1700 GeV limit is for t' in a
weak-isospin doublet; the same limits also apply to t' of charge -4/3. The
>1360 GeV limit is for a singlet model.

AAD 23AV is based on 139 fb^-1 of pp data at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV. Pair
production of vector-like t' is searched for in the dilepton-Z plus jets or
three-lepton modes. The data are consistent with SM background predictions
and limits are obtained for different branching ratios.

TUMASYAN 23V is based on 138 fb^-1 of pp data at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV. Pair
production of vector-like t' is searched for in the single-lepton,
same-sign dilepton, and multilepton channels. The data are consistent with
SM background predictions and limits are obtained for different branching
ratios. Masses below 1.48 TeV are excluded for all decays to third-generation
quarks.
