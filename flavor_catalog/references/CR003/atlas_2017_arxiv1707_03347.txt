Title: Search for pair production of heavy vector-like quarks decaying to
high-pT W bosons and b quarks in the lepton-plus-jets final state in pp
collisions at sqrt(s)=13 TeV with the ATLAS detector
Authors: ATLAS Collaboration
Submitted: 2017-07-11
Updated: 2018-05-15
Journal reference: JHEP 10 (2017) 141
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP10(2017)141

Abstract extract:
A search is presented for the pair production of heavy vector-like T and B
quarks, primarily targeting the T quark decays to a W boson and a b-quark.
The search is based on 36.1 fb^-1 of pp collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV
recorded in 2015 and 2016 with the ATLAS detector.

Data are analysed in the lepton-plus-jets final state, including at least one
b-tagged jet and a large-radius jet identified as originating from the
hadronic decay of a high-momentum W boson. No significant deviation from the
Standard Model expectation is observed in the reconstructed T mass
distribution. The observed 95% confidence level lower limit on the T mass is
1350 GeV assuming 100% branching ratio to Wb. In the SU(2) singlet scenario,
the lower mass limit is 1170 GeV.

This search is also sensitive to a heavy vector-like B quark decaying to Wt
and other final states. Mass limits on both T and B production are set as a
function of the decay branching ratios.
