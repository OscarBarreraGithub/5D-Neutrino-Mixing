Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
Journal reference: JHEP 0809:008, 2008
Submission history: submitted 14 Apr 2008; last revised 24 Apr 2008.

Relevant abstract text, normalized:

The authors study the flavor structure of 5D warped models that provide a dual
description of a composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs. They re-examine flavor
constraints on the mass scale of new physics in standard Randall-Sundrum-type
scenarios and find that the KK gluon mass should generically be heavier than
about 21 TeV.

They find new contributions to flavor violation in composite Higgs models and
state that in the pseudo-Goldstone scenario the KK gluon mass should be at
least about 33 TeV. The abstract concludes that fully anarchic warped flavor is
implausible without partial flavor symmetries.
