Extracted numerical values:
- |Vcb| inclusive = (42.2 +/- 0.5) x 10^-3.
- |Vcb| exclusive = (39.8 +/- 0.6) x 10^-3.
- PDG average |Vcb| = (41.1 +/- 1.2) x 10^-3 after scale factor sqrt(chi2/1)=3.0; PDG cautions that the inclusive and exclusive determinations are marginally consistent, about 3.0 sigma.
- |Vub| inclusive = (4.13 +/- 0.12_exp +0.13/-0.14_theo +/- 0.18_model) x 10^-3.
- |Vub| exclusive = (3.70 +/- 0.10 +/- 0.12) x 10^-3.
- PDG average |Vub| = (3.82 +/- 0.20) x 10^-3 after scale factor sqrt(chi2/1)=1.4; p(chi2)=15%.
- PDG also quotes |Vub|/|Vcb| = 0.098 +/- 0.006 inclusive and 0.093 +/- 0.004 exclusive.

Relevance note:
The review says HFLAV provides many of the numerical inputs. It frames e/mu semileptonic amplitudes as SM-like extraction channels, while semitauonic channels may be sensitive to beyond-SM effects.
