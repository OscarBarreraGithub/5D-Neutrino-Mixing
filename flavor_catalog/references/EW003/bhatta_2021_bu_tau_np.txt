Extracted framework:
- Effective Hamiltonian for b -> u tau nu:
  H_eff = 4 G_F/sqrt(2) V_ub [(1+V_L) O_VL + V_R O_VR + S_L O_SL + S_R O_SR + T_L O_TL].
- The Wilson coefficients V_L, V_R, S_L, S_R, T_L vanish in the Standard Model in this convention.
- Operator basis:
  O_VL = (ubar gamma_mu P_L b)(taubar gamma^mu P_L nu)
  O_VR = (ubar gamma_mu P_R b)(taubar gamma^mu P_L nu)
  O_SL = (ubar P_L b)(taubar P_L nu)
  O_SR = (ubar P_R b)(taubar P_L nu)
  O_TL = (ubar sigma_mu_nu P_L b)(taubar sigma^mu_nu P_L nu)

Relevance note:
This source gives the standard model-independent charged-current b->u tau nu translation used to connect |Vub|-sensitive B->pi/rho/omega tau nu and Bs->K(*) tau nu modes to BSM Wilson coefficients.
