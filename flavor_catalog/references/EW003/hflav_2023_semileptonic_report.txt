Extracted numerical values for semileptonic |Vxb|:
- HFLAV semileptonic chapter uses experimental results generally available through autumn 2023.
- Inclusive |Vcb| central value chosen by HFLAV from Finauri-Gambino q2-moment analysis: |Vcb| = (41.97 +/- 0.48) x 10^-3.
- Alternative inclusive |Vcb| q2 analysis: |Vcb| = (41.69 +/- 0.63) x 10^-3.
- Exclusive combined fit using Vub/Vcb constraints, B->pi l nu, B->D l nu, B->D* l nu, and Bs->Ds(*) mu nu: |Vub| = (3.43 +/- 0.12) x 10^-3 and |Vcb| = (39.77 +/- 0.46) x 10^-3, with rho(Vub,Vcb)=0.239.
- The combined exclusive fit has chi2=3.9 for 1 degree of freedom, p(chi2)=4.8%.
- HFLAV states that the exclusive combined |Vub| differs from inclusive GGOU |Vub| = (4.06 +/- 0.12 +/- 0.11) x 10^-3 by about 3 sigma, and exclusive combined |Vcb| differs from inclusive |Vcb| = (41.97 +/- 0.48) x 10^-3 by more than 3 sigma.
- Inclusive |Vub| framework comparison: BLNP = 4.25 +/- 0.13 +0.19/-0.19, DGE = 3.87 +/- 0.10 +0.09/-0.10, GGOU = 4.06 +/- 0.12 +0.11/-0.11, all in units of 10^-3.

Extracted numerical values for semitauonic context:
- HFLAV averages R(D*) = 0.286 +/- 0.012 and R(D) = 0.342 +/- 0.026, with correlation -0.39.
- HFLAV SM averages used for comparison are R(D*) = 0.254 +/- 0.005 and R(D) = 0.296 +/- 0.004.
- The combined R(D)-R(D*) difference from the SM is about 3.14 sigma, with chi2=12.78 for 2 degrees of freedom and p=1.68 x 10^-3.
