Title: Flavor & new physics opportunities with rare charm decays into leptons
Authors: Stefan de Boer and Gudrun Hiller

Abstract-page metadata extract:

Updated Standard Model predictions for D_(s) -> P l+ l-, with l=e,mu and
P=pi,K, and inclusive decays are presented. Model-independent constraints on
|Delta C|=|Delta U|=1 Wilson coefficients are worked out. New-physics
opportunities arise in semileptonic branching ratios for very large couplings
only, but are not excluded outside resonance regions yet. The work also
discusses resonance-assisted CP asymmetries and angular observables, and gives
predictions for leptoquark models including lepton-flavor-violating and
dineutrino decays.
