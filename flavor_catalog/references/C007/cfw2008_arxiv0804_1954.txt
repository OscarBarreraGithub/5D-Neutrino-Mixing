Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler

Abstract/source extract from pgbflavor.tex:

The paper studies the flavor structure of 5D warped models with a composite
pseudo-Goldstone Higgs. It re-examines flavor constraints in standard
Randall-Sundrum scenarios and finds the KK gluon mass should generically be
heavier than about 21 TeV. In the pseudo-Goldstone scenario, additional flavor
effects make the bound at least about 33 TeV. The authors describe how
RS-GIM suppresses many flavor-changing neutral currents but is not sufficient
for the strongest CP-violating kaon constraints. They frame the main
anarchic-flavor issue as new KK states with flavor-nonuniversal couplings
that contribute to flavor-changing neutral currents.
