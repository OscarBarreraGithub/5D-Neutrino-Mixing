Extracted internal methodology text:
The gs=3 headline crossings are M_KK = 47.26 TeV at 50% acceptance and
127.13 TeV at 95% acceptance. The corresponding perturbative code-default
crossings are 16.54 TeV and 44.50 TeV. These are quoted from the post-audit
8M-draw RUNA reference scan under the input and scope conventions summarized
in that note.

High-statistics validation extract:
The baseline RS-anarchy scan used 8 x 10^6 draws, yielding 1,532,640
PDG-passing draws. The 50%-acceptance crossings are
M_KK|min_50% = 16.54 TeV in the perturbative convention and 47.26 TeV in the
gs = 3 convention. The 95%-acceptance crossings are 44.50 TeV and 127.13 TeV.
The finite-Monte-Carlo Wilson-score intervals quoted in the note are
47.19-47.32 TeV at 50% acceptance and 126.88-127.38 TeV at 95% acceptance
for gs = 3.
