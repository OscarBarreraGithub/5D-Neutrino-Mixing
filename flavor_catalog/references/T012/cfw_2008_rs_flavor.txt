Relevant normalized extract:
The paper re-examines flavor constraints in standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios
and finds that generic KK-gluon masses must be much heavier than the few-TeV scale
unless additional flavor structure is present.  It also emphasizes that electroweak
precision tests and protection of the Z b bbar vertex are central parts of viable
warped flavor model building.
