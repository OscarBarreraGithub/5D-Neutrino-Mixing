Relevant normalized extract:
The paper studies gauge-boson couplings to Standard Model fermions in a warped model
with bulk gauge and matter fields, including the ordinary W and Z bosons.  It analyzes
electroweak precision observables, Z b bbar couplings, flavor-changing Z couplings, and
the dependence on fermion profiles and 5D Yukawa structure.

Use in this process note:
This is the most direct post-CFW RS reference for interpreting Z-pole quark-coupling
observables.  The formulas and model-dependence discussion apply to diagonal Z c cbar
coupling shifts as part of the same Z f fbar coupling system, even though Z b bbar is
usually the sharper custodial benchmark.
