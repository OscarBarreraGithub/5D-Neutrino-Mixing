Relevant normalized extract:
The paper describes FCC-ee Z-pole opportunities and highlights heavy-quark precision
measurements.  It states that R_b, R_c, A_FB^b, and A_FB^c could be measured at FCC-ee
with substantially better precision than at LEP/SLC, relying on improved flavor tagging,
control samples, and future detector performance studies.

Use in this process note:
This is a post-2008 prospective measurement reference.  It motivates why the legacy
LEP/SLC charm observables remain relevant for future electroweak-flavor programs, but
it does not supersede the current PDG values.
