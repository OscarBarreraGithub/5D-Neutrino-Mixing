Relevant normalized extract:
The paper completes the fermionic electroweak two-loop calculation for Z-boson
partial widths.  It gives predictions for Z partial widths and commonly used
branching ratios, including heavy-quark ratios, and finds the remaining missing-order
uncertainties to be below the current experimental errors for Z-pole observables.

Use in this process note:

of R_c and related Z-pole pseudo-observables.  It is not a replacement for the measured
PDG/LEP-SLC inputs.
