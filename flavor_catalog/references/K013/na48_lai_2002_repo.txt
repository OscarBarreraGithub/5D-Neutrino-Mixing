Title: Precise measurement of the decay K-L -> pi(0) gamma gamma.
Authors: A. Lai et al. (NA48 Collaboration).
Journal: Physics Letters B 536 (2002) 229-240.
DOI: 10.1016/S0370-2693(02)01863-4.

Abstract excerpt:
The decay rate of K-L -> pi(0) gamma gamma was measured with the NA48 detector
at the CERN SPS. A total of 2558 candidates was observed with residual
background of 3.2%. The branching ratio was determined as
(1.36 +/- 0.03(stat) +/- 0.03(syst) +/- 0.03(norm)) x 10^-6.
The vector coupling constant was measured as
a(v) = -0.46 +/- 0.03(stat) +/- 0.04(syst).

The abstract also notes that the result suggests CP-violation effects dominate
in K-L -> pi(0) e+ e- and gives an upper limit for the two-photon mass region
m(gamma gamma) < m(pi0).
