arXiv:hep-ph/0105006 [hep-ph], version 2 submitted 2001-08-01.
Title: K_L -> pi0 gamma gamma and the bound on the CP-conserving K_L -> pi0 e+e-.
Authors: F. Gabbiani and G. Valencia.
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. D 64, 094008 (2001).
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.64.094008.

Abstract excerpt:
The authors state that the CP-conserving component of K_L -> pi0 e+ e- can be
obtained from amplitudes in K_L -> pi0 gamma gamma, but that describing the
latter with only the parameter a_V is not sufficient for a model-independent
extraction. They conclude that a model-independent analysis requires extraction
of three parameters.
