Title: Final Results from the KTeV Experiment on the Decay KL -> pi0 gamma gamma.
Authors: E. Abouzaid et al. (KTeV Collaboration).
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. D 77, 112004 (2008).
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.77.112004.

Abstract excerpt:
The KTeV detector measurement reconstructs 1982 events with an estimated
background of 608. The reported branching fraction is
B(KL -> pi0 gamma gamma) = (1.29 +/- 0.03(stat) +/- 0.05(syst)) x 10^-6.
The vector-meson-exchange parameter is
aV = -0.31 +/- 0.05(stat) +/- 0.07(syst).

The abstract states that these results use the full KTeV data set collected
from 1997 to 2000 and supersede earlier KTeV measurements of the branching
ratio and aV.
