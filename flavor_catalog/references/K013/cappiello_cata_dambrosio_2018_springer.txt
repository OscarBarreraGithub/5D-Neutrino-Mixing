Title: Closing in on the radiative weak chiral couplings.
Authors: Luigi Cappiello, Oscar Cata, and Giancarlo D'Ambrosio.
Journal: European Physical Journal C 78, 265 (2018).
DOI: 10.1140/epjc/s10052-018-5748-6.

Abstract/context excerpt:
The paper states that current experimental status of radiative kaon decays can
determine a subclass of O(p^4) counterterms of the weak chiral lagrangian. It
emphasizes K^+- -> pi^+- pi0 l+ l- measurements by NA48/2 and NA62, and says
consistency with other radiative kaon decay measurements gives clean predictions
for weak couplings entering that mode.

The introduction describes radiative kaon decays as tools for Delta S = 1
physics, chiral tests, and CP studies. It notes that K^+- -> pi^+- gamma gamma
is among the measured radiative modes used to constrain weak counterterm
combinations.
