Title:
Technical design of the phase I Mu3e experiment

Published:
2020-09-24, updated to v3 on 2021-08-26.
Journal reference: Nucl. Instrum. Meth. A 1014 (2021) 165679.

Minimal arXiv summary excerpt:
The Mu3e experiment aims to find or exclude the lepton flavour violating decay
mu -> eee at branching fractions above 10^-16. A first phase using an existing
beamline at PSI is designed to reach a single event sensitivity of 2e-15.
The phase-I detector must handle up to 10^8 muon decays per second and uses
ultra-thin HV-MAPS tracking plus scintillating fibres and tiles for timing.
