Title:
The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs

Authors:
Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler.

Journal reference:
JHEP 0809 (2008) 008.

Minimal arXiv summary excerpt:
The paper re-examines flavor constraints on standard Randall-Sundrum-type
scenarios and finds that the KK gluon mass should generically be above about
21 TeV. In the composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs version the flavor bounds become
more stringent, with the KK gluon mass at least about 33 TeV under the assumptions
studied. The authors conclude that fully anarchic flavor in warped extra
dimensions is implausible without additional flavor symmetries.
