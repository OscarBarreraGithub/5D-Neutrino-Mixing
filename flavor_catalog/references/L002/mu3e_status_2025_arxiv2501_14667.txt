Minimal arXiv abstract excerpt:
Mu3e is under construction at PSI to search for mu+ -> e+ e- e+.
The Standard-Model-with-neutrino-masses rate is approximately O(10^-54), so any
observation would be evidence for physics beyond the Standard Model.
Mu3e aims for O(10^-15) sensitivity in Phase I and O(10^-16) after future upgrades.
The Phase-I design uses 10^8 muon stops per second, ultra-thin pixel detectors,
scintillating fibres and tiles, and a triggerless DAQ. Detector commissioning beam
time was planned for 2025.
