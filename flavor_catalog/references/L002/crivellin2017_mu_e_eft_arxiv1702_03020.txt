Title:
Renormalisation-group improved analysis of mu -> e processes in a systematic
effective-field-theory approach

Authors:
Andreas Crivellin, Sacha Davidson, Giovanni Marco Pruna, Adrian Signer.

Journal reference:
JHEP 05 (2017) 117.

Minimal arXiv summary excerpt:
The article performs a complete analysis of mu -> e gamma, mu -> 3e, and coherent
nuclear mu -> e conversion using dimension-six operators below m_W. Leading-order
QCD and QED running between m_W and the experimental scale is included, with
analytic and numerical evolution matrices. Experimental limits are interpreted
as functions of Wilson coefficients and operator correlations.

Use in L002:
Theory/operator-basis context for classifying mu -> 3e as a four-lepton/contact
LFV observable with possible dipole mixing and RG dependence.
