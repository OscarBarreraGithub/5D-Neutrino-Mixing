Minimal arXiv summary excerpt:
The paper studies bounds on anarchic Randall-Sundrum flavor models from lepton
flavor violation. It finds few-TeV-level constraints from muon-electron conversion,
rare muon decays, and rare tau decays. It emphasizes the tension between
loop-induced dipole decays such as mu -> e gamma and tree-level processes such as
muon-electron conversion because of opposite dependences on five-dimensional
Yukawa couplings.
