Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:
The search uses data collected in the 2021-2022 physics runs. The sensitivity
of the search is 2.2 x 10^-13, a factor 2.4 better than the full MEG dataset.
The result is consistent with expected background and yields

  B(mu+ -> e+ gamma) < 1.5 x 10^-13 at 90% C.L.

Relevant published-article conclusion excerpt:
The MEG II collaboration reports the same 2021-2022 result, with
S_90 = 2.2 x 10^-13 and B(mu+ -> e+ gamma) < 1.5 x 10^-13 at 90% C.L.
The article states that data from 2023-2024 are expected to add a 1.5-fold
larger statistic and that planned 2025-2026 data should add another 2.6-fold,
with a target sensitivity near 6 x 10^-14.
