Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:
MEG II reports a search using data from the first physics run in 2021. No
excess over background is observed. The 2021-only upper limit is

  B(mu+ -> e+ gamma) < 7.5 x 10^-13 at 90% C.L.

The combination with the MEG result gives

  B(mu+ -> e+ gamma) < 3.1 x 10^-13 at 90% C.L.

The abstract also notes that a ten-fold larger data sample was being collected
during 2022-2023 and that data-taking would continue.
