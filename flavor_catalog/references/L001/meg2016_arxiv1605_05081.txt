Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:
The final MEG search uses data collected at PSI in 2009-2013, totaling
7.5 x 10^14 stopped muons on target. No significant excess is observed.
The resulting upper limit is

  BR(mu+ -> e+ gamma) < 4.2 x 10^-13 at 90% confidence level.

Extraction note:
This is the post-2008 MEG full-dataset predecessor to the MEG II limits.
