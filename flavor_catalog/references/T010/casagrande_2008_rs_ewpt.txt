Relevant abstract excerpt, normalized:
The paper gives a complete discussion of tree-level flavor-changing effects in the
Randall-Sundrum model with brane-localized Higgs sector and bulk gauge and matter fields.
It studies gauge-boson couplings, including the standard W and Z bosons, and analyzes
electroweak precision observables including S, T, and Zbb couplings.
