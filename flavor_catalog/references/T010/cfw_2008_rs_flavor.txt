Relevant abstract excerpt, normalized:
The paper re-examines flavor constraints in standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios and
finds generic KK-gluon mass bounds at about 21 TeV, with stronger bounds in the composite
pseudo-Goldstone Higgs case. It emphasizes RS-GIM suppression, residual flavor violation,
and the need for partial flavor symmetries in fully anarchic warped models.
