Relevant abstract excerpt, normalized:
The paper complements the calculation of fermionic electroweak two-loop corrections to
Z-boson total width and hadronic peak cross section by giving same-order contributions
to Z-boson partial widths. It states that these are the last missing pieces for a complete
fermionic two-loop description of Z-pole physics, and that remaining missing-order theory
uncertainties are small compared with current experimental errors.
