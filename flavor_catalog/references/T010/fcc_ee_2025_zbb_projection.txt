Relevant abstract excerpt, normalized:
The paper proposes an exclusive b-hadron tagging technique for measuring R_b and A_FB^b
at FCC-ee using O(10^12) Z-boson decays. It states that the approach can reduce leading
systematic uncertainties and obtain total relative uncertainties of order 0.01 percent for
both observables.

Use in this process note:

PDG/LEP-SLC values.
