Title: Natural Neutrino Masses and Mixings from Warped Geometry
Authors: Gilad Perez, Lisa Randall
Published: 2009
Journal reference: JHEP01(2009)077
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2009/01/077

Relevant abstract text:
The authors show how warped-geometry flavor symmetries can explain large
neutrino mixing angles and the difference between quark and lepton flavor
sectors. In the described lepton minimal flavor violation setup, a GIM-like
mechanism prevents dangerous charged-lepton flavor-changing processes; allowed
flavor-changing processes are tied to the neutrino Yukawa interaction and can
be consistent with a cutoff of 10 TeV and 3 TeV Kaluza-Klein masses.
