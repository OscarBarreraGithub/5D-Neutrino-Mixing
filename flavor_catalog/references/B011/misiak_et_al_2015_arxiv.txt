Title:
Updated NNLO QCD predictions for the weak radiative B-meson decays

Relevant excerpt:

Weak radiative decays of the B mesons belong to the most important flavor
changing processes that provide constraints on physics at the TeV scale. In
the derivation of such constraints, accurate standard model predictions for
the inclusive branching ratios play a crucial role.

For the CP- and isospin-averaged branching ratios, we find

B_s gamma = (3.36 +/- 0.23) x 10^-4

and

B_d gamma = 1.73^{+0.12}_{-0.22} x 10^-5

for E_gamma > 1.6 GeV.

Extraction note:
This is the NNLO Misiak et al. prediction quoted in the PDG 2024 b-hadron
review excerpt.
