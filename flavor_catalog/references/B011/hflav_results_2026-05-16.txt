Section: Charmless Decays

Updated LHCb measurements show previous anomalies in R_K and R K* have vanished,
now agreeing with the SM. However, tensions in angular observables and branching
fractions for b -> s l+ l- persist. New limits on lepton-flavor-violating decays,
especially involving tau leptons, have been set. In two-body hadronic decays, the
"K pi CP puzzle" remains.
