Extracted value block: B0 mixing: oscillations and mass difference.
- Combined time-dependent oscillation-frequency result:
  Delta m_d = 0.5069 +- 0.0019 ps^-1
  Inputs listed by HFLAV: ALEPH, DELPHI, L3, OPAL, CDF, D0, BABAR, BELLE, LHCb.
- Time-integrated mixing probability input:
  chi_d = 0.182 +- 0.015
  Inputs listed by HFLAV: ARGUS and CLEO.
- World averages after assuming no CP violation in mixing, no width difference in the B0 system, and using tau(B0) = 1.517 +- 0.004 ps:
  Delta m_d = 0.5069 +- 0.0019 ps^-1
  x_d = 0.7697 +- 0.0035
  chi_d = 0.1860 +- 0.0011
  Inputs listed by HFLAV for chi_d world average: ALEPH, DELPHI, L3, OPAL, CDF, D0, BABAR, BELLE, LHCb, ARGUS, CLEO.
