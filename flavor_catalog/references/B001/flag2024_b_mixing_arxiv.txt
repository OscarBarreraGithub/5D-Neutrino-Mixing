Extracted context from the arXiv abstract:
- FLAG reviews lattice-QCD results related to pion, kaon, D-meson, B-meson, and nucleon physics.
- For the heavy-quark sector, FLAG provides results for mc and mb and for decay constants, form factors, and mixing parameters of charmed and bottom mesons and baryons.
- The abstract states these heavy-quark quantities are relevant for CKM matrix elements and the global CKM unitarity-triangle fit.
