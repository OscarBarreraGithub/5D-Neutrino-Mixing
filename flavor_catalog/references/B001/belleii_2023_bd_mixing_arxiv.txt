Extracted result from the arXiv abstract:
- The analysis measures the B0 lifetime and flavor-oscillation frequency using B0 -> D(*)- pi+ decays in Belle II 2019-2021 data.
- Data sample: 190 fb^-1 at the Upsilon(4S).
- Signal decays: 33000 reconstructed signal decays.
- Results:
  tau_B0 = 1.499 +- 0.013(stat) +- 0.008(syst) ps
  Delta m_d = 0.516 +- 0.008(stat) +- 0.005(syst) ps^-1
- The abstract states these results are consistent with the world averages.
