A_b
This quantity is directly extracted from a measurement of the left-right
forward-backward asymmetry in b b production at SLC using polarized electron beam.
Value: 0.923 +/- 0.020 OUR FIT.

A_FB^(0,b) charge asymmetry in e+ e- -> b b
OUR FIT refers to the Z pole asymmetry.
Asymmetry (%): 9.92 +/- 0.16 OUR FIT.
Converted dimensionless value used in T011: 0.0992 +/- 0.0016.
