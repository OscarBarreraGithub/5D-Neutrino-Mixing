Title: Search for the decays B_s^0 -> tau+ tau- and B^0 -> tau+ tau-
Authors: LHCb Collaboration, R. Aaij et al.
Journal: Phys. Rev. Lett. 118, 251802 (2017)
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.118.251802

Relevant abstract-page facts:
  The analysis uses pp collision data collected with the LHCb detector,
  corresponding to an integrated luminosity of 3 fb^-1 in 2011 and 2012.
  Tau leptons are reconstructed through tau^- -> pi^- pi^+ pi^- nu_tau.
  Assuming no contribution from B^0 -> tau+ tau-, the limit is
  B(B_s^0 -> tau+ tau-) < 6.8e-3 at 95% confidence level.
  Assuming no contribution from B_s^0 -> tau+ tau-, the limit is
  B(B^0 -> tau+ tau-) < 2.1e-3 at 95% confidence level.
  The abstract identifies these as the first direct limit on B_s^0 -> tau+ tau-
  and the world's best limit on B^0 -> tau+ tau- at publication.
