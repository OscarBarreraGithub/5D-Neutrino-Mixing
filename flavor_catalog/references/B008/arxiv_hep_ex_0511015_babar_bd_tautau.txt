Title: A Search for the rare decay B0 --> tau+ tau- at BABAR
Authors: BABAR Collaboration, B. Aubert et al.
Journal: Phys. Rev. Lett. 96, 241802 (2006)
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.96.241802

Relevant abstract-page facts:
  The search used (232 +/- 3) x 10^6 Upsilon(4S) --> B Bbar decays.
  No evidence for B0 --> tau+ tau- was found.
  The reported upper limit is B(B0 --> tau+ tau-) < 4.1 x 10^-3
  at the 90% confidence level.
