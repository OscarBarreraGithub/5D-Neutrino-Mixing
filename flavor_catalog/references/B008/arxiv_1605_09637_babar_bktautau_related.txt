Title: Search for B+ -> K+ tau+ tau- at the BaBar experiment
Authors: BABAR Collaboration, J. P. Lees et al.
Journal: Phys. Rev. Lett. 118, 031802 (2017)
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.118.031802

Relevance note:
  This is not the neutral-B leptonic B008 observable. It is a related
  semileptonic b -> s tau+ tau- search and was considered because it was
  suggested in the Wave-8 prompt.

Relevant abstract-page facts:
  The search used the full BABAR data sample, 424 fb^-1, corresponding to
  471 million B Bbar pairs.
  The measured branching fraction was reported as
  (1.31^{+0.66}_{-0.61}(stat)^{+0.35}_{-0.25}(syst)) x 10^-3.
  The 90% CL upper limit was B(B+ -> K+ tau+ tau-) < 2.25 x 10^-3.
