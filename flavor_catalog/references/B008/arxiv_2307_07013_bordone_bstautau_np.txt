Title: tau_Bs/tau_Bd and DeltaGamma_s confront new physics in b -> s tau tau
Authors: Marzia Bordone and Mario Fernandez Navarro
Journal: Eur. Phys. J. C 83, 842 (2023)
DOI: 10.1140/epjc/s10052-023-12013-9

Relevant abstract-page facts:
  The paper studies how new physics enhancing b -> s tau tau transitions can
  affect tau_Bs/tau_Bd and DeltaGamma_s.
  It compares indirect constraints from lifetime observables with direct
  searches for b -> s tau tau modes.

Relevant value notes extracted from the arXiv PDF for provenance:
  The paper quotes an indirect lifetime-ratio bound
  B(B_s -> tau tau)_NP < 5.5 x 10^-2 at 95% CL and notes that the direct
  LHCb bound B(B_s -> tau tau) < 6.8 x 10^-3 is stronger at the time.
  The paper emphasizes that scalar operators make B_s -> tau tau especially
  sensitive because of chiral enhancement.
