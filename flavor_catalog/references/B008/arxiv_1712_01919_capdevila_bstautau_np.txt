Title: Searching for New Physics with b -> s tau+ tau- processes
Authors: Bernat Capdevila, Andreas Crivellin, Sebastien Descotes-Genon,
  Lars Hofer, Joaquim Matias
Journal: Phys. Rev. Lett. 120, 181802 (2018)
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.120.181802

Relevant abstract-page facts:
  Motivated by lepton-flavor-universality hints, the paper studies new-physics
  effects in b -> s tau+ tau- Wilson coefficients.
  It notes that new physics related to R_D, R_D*, and R_J/psi can enhance
  b -> s tau+ tau- rates by about three orders of magnitude relative to the
  Standard Model.
  The paper identifies B_s -> tau+ tau- and related semileptonic b -> s tau+ tau-
  modes as possible future probes at LHCb and Belle II.
