The Belle II Physics Book.

Authors:
E. Kou, P. Urquijo, W. Altmannshofer, et al.

Journal reference:
PTEP 2019 (2019) 123C01.

Relevant tau-LFV extract:
The tau and low-multiplicity chapter states that Belle performed searches for
46 lepton-flavour-violating tau decay modes with nearly the full Belle data
sample, finding no evidence and setting 90% CL upper limits at the O(10^-8)
level. It states that Belle II is expected to reach O(10^-9)--O(10^-10)
sensitivity for tau LFV modes with 50 ab^-1, with largely background-free
channels benefiting from near-linear luminosity scaling.

Process note:
The book treats tau -> 3mu as a golden purely leptonic mode and notes that
processes with mu replaced by e are part of the same LFV program. This source
is used only for future-prospect and operator-context discussion, not as a
current L010 experimental limit.
