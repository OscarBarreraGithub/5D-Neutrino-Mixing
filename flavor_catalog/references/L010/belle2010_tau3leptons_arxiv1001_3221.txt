Title:
Search for Lepton Flavor Violating Tau Decays into Three Leptons with
719 Million Produced Tau+Tau- Pairs.

Authors:
K. Hayasaka, K. Inami, Y. Miyazaki, et al. (Belle Collaboration).

Journal reference:
Phys. Lett. B 687 (2010) 139-143.

Relevant abstract/result context:
The search uses 782 fb^-1 of Belle data at the KEKB asymmetric-energy e+e-
collider, corresponding to 719 million produced tau+tau- pairs. No evidence
for the six tau decays to three charged leptons is observed.

Relevant TeX-source table/result extract for L010:
For tau- -> e- e+ e-, the nominal-selection efficiency is 6.0%, the expected
background is 0.21 +/- 0.15 events, the systematic uncertainty is 9.8%, no
events are observed in the signal region, and
BR(tau- -> e- e+ e-) < 2.7 x 10^-8 at 90% confidence level.

Summary extract:
The paper reports BR(tau- -> e- e+ e-) < 2.7 x 10^-8 at 90% CL and says the
six limits improve previous published limits by factors from 1.3 to 1.6.
