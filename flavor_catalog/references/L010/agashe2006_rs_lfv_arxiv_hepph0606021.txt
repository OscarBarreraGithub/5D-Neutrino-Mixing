Title:

Authors:

Minimal arXiv summary extract:
The paper studies bounds on anarchic Randall-Sundrum flavor models from lepton
flavor violation. It discusses rare muon and rare tau decays, and emphasizes
the complementarity between loop-induced dipole decays and tree-level charged
LFV processes.
