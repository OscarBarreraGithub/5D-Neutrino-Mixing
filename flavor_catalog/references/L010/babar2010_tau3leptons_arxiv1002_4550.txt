Title:
Limits on tau Lepton-Flavor Violating Decays in three charged leptons.

Authors:
J. P. Lees et al. (BaBar Collaboration).

Journal reference:
Phys. Rev. D 81 (2010) 111101.

Relevant abstract/result context:
The search uses 468 fb^-1 collected with the BaBar detector at PEP-II. For all
six tau -> three-lepton decay modes the observed event counts are compatible
with background expectations. Upper limits on branching fractions are set in
the range (1.8--3.3) x 10^-8 at 90% confidence level.

Relevant TeX-source table/result extract for L010:
For tau- -> e- e+ e-, the efficiency is 8.6 +/- 0.2%, the expected background
is 0.12 +/- 0.02 events, the expected 90% CL branching-fraction upper limit is
3.4 x 10^-8, zero events are observed, and the observed 90% CL upper limit is
2.9 x 10^-8.

Use in L010:
Independent BaBar cross-check of the B-factory-era tau -> 3e limit; PDG lists
it below the Belle 2010 selected value.
