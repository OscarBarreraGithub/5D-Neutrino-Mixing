Abstract-level statement:
  The authors estimate hadronic matrix elements of CP-violating three-gluon
  and four-gluon Weinberg operators using QCD sum-rule techniques. In the
  three-gluon case, they reproduce expressions from earlier work and provide
  a systematic uncertainty analysis.

Operator definition:
  The dimension-six operator O6 is a Weinberg-type three-gluon operator built
  from f^{ABC}, the dual gluon field strength, and two gluon field strengths.

Numerical result in the Haisch-Hala O6 normalization:
  (d_n / e)_O6 = 74 (1 +/- 0.5) MeV.

Uncertainty statement:
  The final relative error is 50%; the dominant source is the scale-ratio
  variation in the sum rule.

Use in E009:
  Post-2008 theory update for the Weinberg operator matrix element. E009 uses
  it as an auxiliary normalization and benchmark one-source-at-a-time bound,
  not as a measured PDG observable.
