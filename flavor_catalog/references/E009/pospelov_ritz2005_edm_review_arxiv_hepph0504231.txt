Low-energy CP-odd Lagrangian excerpt:
  At dimension six one encounters the CP-odd three-gluon Weinberg operator.
  In the review notation,
  L_dim=6 = (1/3) w f^{abc} G^a_{mu nu} G~^{nu beta,b}
  G^c_{beta}^{mu} + four-fermion terms + ...

Weinberg-operator neutron EDM estimate:
  |d_n(w)| approximately equals |mu_n| [3 g_s m_0^2 /(32 pi^2)]
  w ln(M^2/mu_IR^2), and for M/mu_IR = 2 with g_s = 2.1 the review quotes
  |d_n(w)| about e * 22 MeV * w(1 GeV).

Use in E009:
  Paper-era normalization and benchmark translation from the Weinberg
  coefficient w(1 GeV) to the neutron EDM. This is a theory normalization, not
  a measured PDG value.
