Global-analysis scope:
  The paper performs a model-independent global analysis of neutron, neutral
  atom, and molecular EDM searches in terms of leptonic, semileptonic, and
  nonleptonic CP-violating interactions.

Relevant Weinberg-operator text:
  The paper lists O_Ge as the CP-violating Weinberg three-gluon operator,
  sometimes called the gluon CEDM.

Model-dependence statement:
  The analysis observes that more CP-violating sources exist than independent
  low-energy observables. For diamagnetic systems, including the neutron,
  many underlying hadronic CPV sources can contribute to the same low-energy
  parameters; extra assumptions are needed to isolate one source.

Use in E009:
  Justifies treating Weinberg-operator bounds as EFT/global-fit-dependent
  interpretations, not direct experimental measurements.
