Relevant abstract extract:
The paper considers a generic composite Higgs model based on the coset
SO(5)/SO(4) and studies its phenomenology beyond the leading low-energy
effective lagrangian approximation. It introduces the lowest-lying, possibly
narrow, resonances in a controllable way, called partial UV completion.

The simplest cases include a scalar in either singlet or tensor representation
of SO(4) and vectors in the adjoint of SO(4). The paper studies the impact of
these resonances on high-energy vector-boson scattering, pointing out for each
resonance characteristic depletion and enhancement patterns relative to the
leading-order chiral lagrangian.
