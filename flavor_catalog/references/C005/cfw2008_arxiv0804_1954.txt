arXiv API abstract snapshot:
  The paper studies flavor in 5D warped models with a composite pseudo-Goldstone
  Higgs, re-examines flavor constraints in standard Randall-Sundrum-type
  scenarios, and finds that the KK gluon mass should generically be heavier
  than about 21 TeV. It compares composite Higgs flavor to the RS model,
  discusses RS-GIM suppression and additional flavor-violation sources, and
  states that the pseudo-Goldstone scenario implies a KK gluon mass of at
  least about 33 TeV under the assumptions described in the paper.
