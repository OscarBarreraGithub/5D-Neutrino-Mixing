Title: Rare Charm Decays in the Standard Model and Beyond
Authors: Gustavo Burdman, Eugene Golowich, JoAnne Hewett, Sandip Pakvasa
Submitted: 2001-12-18; last revised: 2002-03-01
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. D 66 (2002) 014009
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.66.014009

Minimal theory snapshot:
  This rare-charm reference studies Standard Model and beyond-Standard-Model
  contributions to rare charm decays, including c -> u l+ l- transitions,
  short-distance QCD corrections, and long-distance effects. For leptonic
  D0 modes it treats the D0 -> gamma gamma -> l+ l- long-distance path and
  emphasizes that rare charm observables are sensitive to new short-distance
  physics only after the mode-dependent long-distance structure is understood.
