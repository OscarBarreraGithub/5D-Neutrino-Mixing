Title: Measurement of CP violation in the decay B+ -> K+ pi0
Authors: LHCb Collaboration, R. Aaij et al.
Journal: Phys. Rev. Lett. 126, 091802 (2021)
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.126.091802

Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:
LHCb reports direct CP violation in B+ -> K+ pi0 using 5.4 fb^-1 at
sqrt(s) = 13 TeV.  The measured CP asymmetry is
0.025 +/- 0.015 +/- 0.006 +/- 0.003.  The abstract identifies this observable
as a key input to the long-standing K pi puzzle and states that it enhances
the anomalous difference between the direct CP asymmetries of B0 -> K+ pi-
and B+ -> K+ pi0.
