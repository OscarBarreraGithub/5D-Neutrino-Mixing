Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs

Journal: JHEP 0809:008 (2008)
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008

Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:
The paper re-examines flavor constraints in warped five-dimensional models
and finds that the KK-gluon mass is generically pushed above roughly 21 TeV
in standard RS-type scenarios.  It emphasizes the RS-GIM suppression, the
possible strengthening or weakening from boundary kinetic terms, and the
need for additional flavor structure in fully anarchic warped models.
