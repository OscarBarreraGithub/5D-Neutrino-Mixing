Title: Measurement of branching fractions and direct CP asymmetries for B -> K pi and B -> pi pi decays at Belle II
Authors: Belle II Collaboration, I. Adachi et al.
Journal: Phys. Rev. D 109, 012001 (2024)
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.109.012001

Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:
Belle II reports branching fractions and direct CP asymmetries for
B0 -> K+ pi-, B+ -> K+ pi0, B+ -> K0 pi+, and B0 -> K0 pi0, using
387 x 10^6 bottom-antibottom meson pairs at the Upsilon(4S).  The analysis
also tests the Standard Model isospin-based sum rule and obtains
-0.03 +/- 0.13 +/- 0.04 for that sum-rule quantity.
