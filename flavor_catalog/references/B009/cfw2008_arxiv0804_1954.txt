Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
Year: 2008

Excerpt:
The paper studies the flavor structure of 5D warped models and re-examines flavor constraints on the new-physics mass scale in standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios. The abstract states that the KK gluon mass should generically be heavier than about 21 TeV in standard RS-type scenarios, and about 33 TeV in the pseudo-Goldstone Higgs scenario under the assumptions described there.
