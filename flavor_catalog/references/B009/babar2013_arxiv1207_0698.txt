Title: Evidence of B -> tau nu decays with hadronic B tags
Authors: BABAR Collaboration
Year: 2013

Excerpt:
The BaBar analysis searches for B+ -> tau+ nu using 467.8 x 10^6 BB pairs collected at the Y(4S), with one B- completely reconstructed in hadronic decay modes. Tau+ modes include e+ nu nu, mu+ nu nu, pi+ nu, and rho+ nu.

The abstract reports a 3.8 sigma excess including systematic uncertainties and a branching-fraction central value
B(B+ -> tau+ nu) = (1.83^{+0.53}_{-0.49}(stat.) +/- 0.24(syst.)) x 10^-4.
