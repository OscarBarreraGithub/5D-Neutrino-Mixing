Title: Measurement of B^+ -> tau^+ nu_tau branching fraction with a hadronic tagging method at Belle II
Authors: Belle II Collaboration, I. Adachi et al.
Year: 2025

Excerpt:
The Belle II analysis uses (387 +- 6) x 10^6 Upsilon(4S) events collected between 2019 and 2022. It reconstructs the accompanying B- meson with hadronic tagging and identifies B^+ -> tau^+ nu_tau candidates in the recoil.

The abstract reports evidence at 3.0 standard deviations, including systematic uncertainties, and measures
B(B^+ -> tau^+ nu_tau) = [1.24 +- 0.41 (stat.) +- 0.19 (syst.)] x 10^-4.
