Title: Current Status of the Standard Model Prediction for the B_s -> mu+ mu- Branching Ratio
Authors: Mateusz Czaja, Mikolaj Misiak
Local snapshot purpose: current SM prediction and comparison value.

ArXiv metadata excerpt:
Submitted on 4 Jul 2024. Contribution to the Symmetry Special Issue "Symmetries and Anomalies in Flavour Physics".

Abstract excerpt:
The rare decay B_s -> mu+ mu- provides an important constraint on possible deviations from the Standard Model in b-s-l-l interactions.
The present weighted average of its branching ratio measurements amounts to (3.34 +/- 0.27) x 10^-9.
The theoretical prediction is (3.64 +/- 0.12) x 10^-9 within the Standard Model.
The paper reviews calculations that have contributed to this prediction and discusses the associated uncertainties.
