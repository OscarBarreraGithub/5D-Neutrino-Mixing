Values used in EW002:
- The abstract states two Cabibbo-angle tensions: Vus determinations from
  K_mu2, K_l3, and tau decays disagree at about 3 sigma, and beta-decay plus
  averaged Vus inputs imply a first-row unitarity deficit also at about
  3 sigma.
- Their input table includes:
  Vud = 0.97367 +/- 0.00032 from superallowed 0+ -> 0+ nuclear beta decays.
  Vus = 0.22330 +/- 0.00053 from K_l3.
  Vus = 0.22314 +/- 0.00051 as a world average over K, tau, and hyperons.
  Vus/Vud = 0.23047 +/- 0.00043 for selected ratios.
  Global-fit Vud = 0.97379 +/- 0.00025 and Vus = 0.22405 +/- 0.00035.

Relevance notes:
- The paper connects the anomaly to modified W-quark couplings. By SU(2)_L
  invariance these imply correlated Z-quark shifts and possible FCNC effects.
- Vector-like quark completions are highlighted because they can change
  W-quark couplings at tree level and require flavor-observable checks,
  including loop effects in Delta F = 2 processes.
