  |Vus| f_+(0) = 0.21654(41).

  f_K+-/f_pi+- = 1.1934(19) for Nf = 2+1+1.

  |Vus|/|Vud| = 0.23126(50).
- |Vub| input for the first-row test:
  |Vub| = 3.82(24)e-3.
- First-row sum using Nf = 2+1+1 f_+(0) and the PDG beta-decay Vud:
  |Vu|^2 = 0.99802(66), about a 3.0 sigma deviation from unity.
- First-row sum using Nf = 2+1+1 f_K+-/f_pi+- and the same Vud:
  |Vu|^2 = 0.99888(67), about a 1.7 sigma deviation from unity.
- Nf = 2+1 comparison:
  f_+(0) plus beta-decay Vud gives |Vu|^2 = 0.99824(69), about 2.5 sigma;
  f_K+-/f_pi+- gives |Vu|^2 = 0.99903(72), about 1.4 sigma.
