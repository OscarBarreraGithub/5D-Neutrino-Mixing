Values used in EW002:
- Survey covers 23 superallowed 0+ -> 0+ beta decays and 222 individual
  measurements from 174 references.
- Fifteen corrected Ft values reach precision of 0.3% or better.
- The averaged Ft value combined with the muon lifetime gives
  Vud = 0.97373 +/- 0.00031.
- The result is lower than the 2015 survey by about one standard deviation and
  has a 50% larger uncertainty because of updated radiative-correction theory.
- The lower Vud increases the tension in the top-row CKM unitarity test.

Use in EW002:
- This is the Hardy-Towner 2020+ superallowed-beta baseline cited for the
  beta-decay Vud input, with PDG 2025 carrying a closely related updated
  value after later radiative/nuclear-structure treatments.
