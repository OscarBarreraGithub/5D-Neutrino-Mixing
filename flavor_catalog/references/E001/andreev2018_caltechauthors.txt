Repository abstract summary:
The article presents an improved experimental limit on the electron electric
dipole moment using electron spin precession in a superposition of quantum
states subjected to a huge intramolecular electric field. The record states
that the sensitivity is more than one order of magnitude better than previous
measurements.

Numerical value as recorded by the PDG S003EDM datablock:
ACME/Andreev 2018 gives |d_e| < 1.1e-29 e cm at 90% CL, corresponding to
(4.3 +/- 3.1 +/- 2.6) x 10^-30 e cm.
