Title: EFT approach to the electron Electric Dipole Moment at the two-loop level.
Authors: Giuliano Panico, Alex Pomarol, Marc Riembau.
Submitted: 2018-10-22. Last revised: 2021-11-04, v3.
Journal reference: JHEP 04 (2019) 090.
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP04(2019)090.
arXiv DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.1810.09413.

Abstract summary relevant to E001:
The paper discusses the ACME 2018 electron-EDM bound
|d_e| < 1.1 x 10^-29 e cm at 90% CL and describes its interpretation in an
EFT treatment. It classifies corrections to CP-violating electron dipole
operators and presents anomalous-dimension contributions up to two-loop level.
It notes that the bound gives relevant constraints on TeV-scale BSM physics
even when the eEDM arises at two loops.
