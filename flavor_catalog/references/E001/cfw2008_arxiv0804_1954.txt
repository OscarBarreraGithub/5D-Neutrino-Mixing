Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs.
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler.
Submitted: 2008-04-14. Last revised: 2008-04-24, v2.
Journal reference: JHEP 0809:008 (2008).
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008.
arXiv DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.0804.1954.

Abstract summary relevant to flavor-catalog context:
The paper studies flavor in 5D warped models and re-examines flavor
constraints on the new-physics scale in Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios.
It reports that generic RS KK-gluon masses are constrained above about
21 TeV, and that the composite pseudo-Goldstone scenario gives about
33 TeV in the assumptions of that work. The abstract emphasizes RS-GIM
suppression, additional flavor-violating effects, and the need for partial
flavor symmetries if fully anarchic flavor is too constrained.
