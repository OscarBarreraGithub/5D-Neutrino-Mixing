ArXiv abstract metadata summary:
The experiment uses electrons confined inside molecular ions, with a large
intramolecular electric field and coherence up to 3 s. The result is
consistent with zero and improves the previous best upper bound by about 2.4.
The abstract states sensitivity to 10^-19 eV shifts in molecular ions and
constraints on broad classes of new physics above 10^13 eV.
