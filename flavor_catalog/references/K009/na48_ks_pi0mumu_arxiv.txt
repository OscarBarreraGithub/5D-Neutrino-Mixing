Title: Observation of the rare decay K_S -> pi0 mu+ mu-
Authors: J. R. Batley et al. (NA48/1 Collaboration)

Relevant extraction:
NA48/1 collected a high-intensity K_S beam data set in 2002.  Six events were
found with background expectation 0.22 +0.18 -0.11 events.  Using a vector
matrix element and unit form factor, the measured branching ratio was
  B(K_S -> pi0 mu+ mu-) =
  (2.9 +1.5 -1.2 stat +/- 0.2 syst) x 10^-9.

Use in K009 catalog:
This supplies the short-lived neutral-kaon input entering the indirect-CP
amplitude of K_L -> pi0 mu+ mu-.
