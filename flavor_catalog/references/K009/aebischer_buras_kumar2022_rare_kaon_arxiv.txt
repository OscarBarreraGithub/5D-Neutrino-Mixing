Title: On the Importance of Rare Kaon Decays: A Snowmass 2021 White Paper
Authors: Jason Aebischer, Andrzej J. Buras, Jacky Kumar

Relevant abstract extraction:
The white paper emphasizes precise measurements of rare kaon modes including
K+ -> pi+ nu nubar, K_L -> pi0 nu nubar, K_{L,S} -> mu+ mu-, and
K_{L,S} -> pi0 l+ l-.  It highlights branching ratios and q^2 distributions,
correlations with epsilon'/epsilon, epsilon_K, and Delta M_K, sensitivity to
|V_cb| for most observables, and SMEFT interpretations of very short-distance
scales beyond direct LHC reach.

remains the KTeV/PDG bound.
