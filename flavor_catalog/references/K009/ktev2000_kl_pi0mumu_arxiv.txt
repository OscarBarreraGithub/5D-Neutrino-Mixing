Title: Search for the Decay K_L -> pi0 mu+ mu-
Authors: A. Alavi-Harati et al. (KTeV Collaboration)

Relevant extraction:
The KTeV search used 1997 data and reported two observed candidates with an
expected background of 0.87 +/- 0.15 events.  The resulting upper limit was
  B(K_L -> pi0 mu+ mu-) < 3.8 x 10^-10
at 90% confidence level.

Physics context extracted from the abstract:
The paper describes this mode as having a significant CP-violating contribution;
a direct measurement would test the CKM mechanism for CP violation or point to
new physics.
