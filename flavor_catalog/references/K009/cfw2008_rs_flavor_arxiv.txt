Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler

Relevant abstract extraction:
The paper studies flavor constraints in warped models with a composite
pseudo-Goldstone Higgs.  It finds that standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios
generically require KK gluon masses heavier than about 21 TeV; in the
composite-Higgs variant, kinetic mixing can tighten the quoted bound to about
33 TeV.  The abstract states that fully anarchic flavor in warped extra
dimensions is difficult without partial flavor symmetries.

ratio limit for K009.
