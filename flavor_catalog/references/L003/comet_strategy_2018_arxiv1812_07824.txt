The abstract describes the staged COMET plan. Phase-I aims at a factor 100
improvement over the current limit. Phase-II seeks another factor 100, for a
total factor 10,000, with a single-event sensitivity of 2.6 x 10^-17 after
2 x 10^7 seconds of data-taking. It also notes that an additional
order-of-magnitude improvement is being considered through design and
operation refinements.
