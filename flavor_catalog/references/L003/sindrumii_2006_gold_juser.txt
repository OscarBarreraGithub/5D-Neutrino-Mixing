Title:
A search for mu-e conversion in muonic gold

Authors:
W. Bertl et al. (SINDRUM II Collaboration)

Publication:
European Physical Journal C 47, 337-346 (2006).

Minimal extracted value:
The record abstract reports a search for mu-e conversion in muonic gold with
Gamma(mu- Au -> e- Au(g.s.)) / Gamma_capture(mu- Au) < 7 x 10^-13 at 90% C.L.

Use in L003:
This is the current published all-target benchmark limit cited by Mu2e and
COMET aluminum projections. It is not an aluminum-target measurement.
