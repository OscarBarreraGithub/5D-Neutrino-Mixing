Minimal extracted value:
The abstract states that Mu2e searches for neutrinoless muon-to-electron
conversion in a nucleus and aims for a single-event sensitivity of
2.8 x 10^-17, corresponding to an expected upper limit on the conversion rate
of 6.7 x 10^-17. It describes this as a four-order-of-magnitude improvement
over existing limits.
