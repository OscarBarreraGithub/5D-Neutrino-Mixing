The abstract identifies COMET Phase-I as a search for mu-e conversion in an
aluminium nucleus. It gives a Phase-I experimental sensitivity goal of
3.1 x 10^-15, or a 90% upper-limit branching-ratio sensitivity of
7 x 10^-15, with expected background 0.032 events. It also specifies a
3.2 kW, 8 GeV proton beam from J-PARC.
