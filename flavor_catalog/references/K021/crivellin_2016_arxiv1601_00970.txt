Title: Lepton flavor (universality) violation in rare kaon decays.

Lewis C. Tunstall.

Abstract excerpt, ASCII-normalized:
The paper observes that four-fermion operators relevant to B-physics anomalies
have analogs in the kaon sector and analyzes their impact on
K -> pi l l' and K -> l l' decays for l = mu, e. It also notes physics
opportunities at NA62 and discusses sensitivity improvements needed to test
such new-physics ideas in kaon decays.
