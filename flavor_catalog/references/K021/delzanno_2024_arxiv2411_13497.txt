Title: Global analysis of mu -> e interactions in the SMEFT.
Authors: Filippo Delzanno, Kaori Fuyuto, Sergi Gonzalez-Solis,
Emanuele Mereghetti.

Abstract excerpt, ASCII-normalized:
The paper studies bounds on charged-lepton-flavor-violating mu-e interactions
in SMEFT. For quark-flavor violating operators it includes CLFV meson decays;
at high energy it considers Higgs, Z, top, and pp -> e mu searches. It connects
low- and high-energy observables through renormalization-group running and
matching between CLFV operators.

Role in K021:
  Recent global-analysis context for mu-e semileptonic operators and their RG
  matching, relevant to any implementation of K_L -> pi0 e mu in an SMEFT-like
  extension.
