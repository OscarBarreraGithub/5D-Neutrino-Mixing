Title: High-pT LHC constraints on SMEFT operators affecting rare kaon and
hyperon decays.
Authors: Arnab Roy and German Valencia.

Abstract excerpt, ASCII-normalized:
The paper constrains new physics affecting rare kaon and hyperon decay using a
low-energy effective theory matched onto dimension-six SMEFT operators. It
computes contributions to charged dilepton, monolepton, monojet, and VH
searches at ATLAS and CMS. Rare kaon decays are generally more restrictive,
while high-pT measurements complement them along less sensitive directions in
parameter space.
