Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs.
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler.
Journal reference: JHEP 0809:008 (2008).
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008.

Abstract excerpt, ASCII-normalized:
The paper studies flavor in 5D warped models and re-examines flavor constraints
in standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios. It finds that the KK gluon mass
should generically be heavier than about 21 TeV, and that in the
pseudo-Goldstone scenario the KK gluon mass should be at least about 33 TeV,
with the strength of bounds depending on boundary kinetic terms. It concludes
that fully anarchic warped flavor requires partial flavor symmetries to remove
some sources of flavor violation.
