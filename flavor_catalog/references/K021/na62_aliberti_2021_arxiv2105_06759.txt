Title: Search for lepton number and flavour violation in K+ and pi0 decays.
Authors: R. Aliberti et al. (NA62 Collaboration).
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. Lett. 127, 131802 (2021).
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.127.131802.

Abstract excerpt, ASCII-normalized:
NA62 searched for K+ -> pi- mu+ e+, K+ -> pi+ mu- e+, and pi0 -> mu- e+
using 2017-2018 CERN data. No evidence was found. The 90% CL limits were:

  BR(K+ -> pi- mu+ e+) < 4.2 x 10^-11
  BR(K+ -> pi+ mu- e+) < 6.6 x 10^-11
  BR(pi0 -> mu- e+) < 3.2 x 10^-10

The abstract states these results improve previous results for these decay
modes by one order of magnitude.

Role in K021:
  Post-2008 adjacent experimental development for charged-kaon and pion LFV;
  it does not supersede the neutral K_L -> pi0 e mu limit.
