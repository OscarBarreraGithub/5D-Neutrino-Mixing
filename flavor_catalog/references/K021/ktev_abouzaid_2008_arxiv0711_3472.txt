Title: Search for Lepton Flavor Violating Decays of the Neutral Kaon.
Authors: E. Abouzaid et al. (KTeV Collaboration).
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. Lett. 100, 131803 (2008).
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.100.131803.

Abstract excerpt, ASCII-normalized:
The Fermilab KTeV experiment searched for lepton flavor violating decays of
the K_L meson in three decay modes. It observed no events in the signal region
for any of the modes studied and set 90% CL upper limits:

  BR(K_L -> pi0 mu e) < 7.56 x 10^-11
  BR(K_L -> pi0 pi0 mu e) < 1.64 x 10^-10
  BR(pi0 -> mu e) < 3.59 x 10^-10

Role in K021:
  This is the experiment input behind the current PDG K_L -> pi0 mu+- e-+
  limit. PDG rounds the K021 limit to < 7.6 x 10^-11.
