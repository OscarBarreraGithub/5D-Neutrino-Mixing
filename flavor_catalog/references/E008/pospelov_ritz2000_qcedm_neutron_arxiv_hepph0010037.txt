Title: Neutron EDM from Electric and Chromoelectric Dipole Moments of Quarks
Authors: Maxim Pospelov, Adam Ritz
arXiv:hep-ph/0010037
Published DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.63.073015

Relevant arXiv abstract extract:
  Using QCD sum rules, the paper calculates the neutron EDM induced by
  CP-violating operators up to dimension five.
  The abstract states that chromoelectric dipole moments of quarks tilde d_i,
  including strange-quark tilde d_s, provide significant contributions
  comparable to quark EDM contributions.
  With the theta term removed by Peccei-Quinn symmetry, the abstract gives
    d_n = (1 +/- 0.5) [ 1.1 e (tilde d_d + 0.5 tilde d_u)
                        + 1.4 (d_d - 0.25 d_u) ].

Catalog use:
  E008 uses this as the neutron qCEDM translation formula. The sidecar records
  central one-at-a-time bounds derived by combining the formula with the
  current PDG neutron EDM limit.
