Title: Neutron electric dipole moment from isovector quark chromo-electric
dipole moment
Authors: Tanmoy Bhattacharya, Vincenzo Cirigliano, Rajan Gupta,
Emanuele Mereghetti, Jun-Sik Yoo, Boram Yoon
arXiv:2404.13516

Relevant arXiv abstract extract:
  The paper presents lattice-QCD results for the contribution of the isovector
  quark cEDM operator to the neutron EDM. The calculation uses four 2+1+1
  flavor highly improved staggered quark ensembles from MILC and Wilson-clover
  quarks for correlation functions. It uses the nonsinglet axial Ward identity,
  with corrections up to O(a), to control power-divergent mixing of the
  isovector qCEDM operator with the lower-dimensional pseudoscalar operator.
  Results are presented after conversion to the MS scheme at leading-log order.
