Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
arXiv:0804.1954
Published DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008

Relevant arXiv abstract extract:
  The paper studies flavor in 5D warped models and composite pseudo-Goldstone
  Higgs models. It reports that in standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios
  the KK gluon mass should generically be heavier than about 21 TeV.
  In the pseudo-Goldstone scenario, with Yukawa couplings set by a gauge
  coupling, the KK gluon mass should be at least about 33 TeV.
  The abstract states that these strong bounds make a fully anarchic approach
  to flavor in warped extra dimensions implausible unless partial flavor
  symmetries eliminate some sources of flavor violation.
