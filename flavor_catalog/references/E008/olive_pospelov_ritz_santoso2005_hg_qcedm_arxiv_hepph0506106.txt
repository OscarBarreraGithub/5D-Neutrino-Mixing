Title: CP-odd Phase Correlations and Electric Dipole Moments
Authors: Keith A. Olive, Maxim Pospelov, Adam Ritz, Yudi Santoso
arXiv:hep-ph/0506106
Published DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.72.075001

Relevant extracted text, Section 3.2:
  In the presence of the Peccei-Quinn mechanism, QCD sum rules give
    d_n(d_q, tilde d_q) =
      (1.4 +/- 0.6)(d_d - 0.25 d_u)
      + (1.1 +/- 0.5) e (tilde d_d + 0.5 tilde d_u).
  The light-quark EDMs and CEDMs are normalized at a hadronic scale assumed
  to be 1 GeV.

Relevant extracted text, Section 3.3:
  The mercury EDM depends on the Schiff moment and CP-odd pion-nucleon
  couplings. The paper quotes
    gbar_piNN^(1)(tilde d_q) ~ 4^{+8}_{-2} (tilde d_u - tilde d_d) GeV^{-1},
  with large uncertainty from cancellations between leading contributions.
  Combining atomic, nuclear, and QCD ingredients, the paper gives
    d_Hg = 7 x 10^-3 e (tilde d_u - tilde d_d) + 10^-2 d_e
           + additional four-fermion and semileptonic terms.
  It states that d_Hg is especially sensitive to the triplet/isovector CEDM
  combination tilde d_u - tilde d_d, with the overall coefficient known only
  with poor precision.
