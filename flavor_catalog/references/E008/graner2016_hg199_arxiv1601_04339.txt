Title: Reduced Limit on the Permanent Electric Dipole Moment of 199Hg
Authors: B. Graner, Y. Chen, E. G. Lindahl, B. R. Heckel
arXiv:1601.04339
Published DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.116.161601
Erratum DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.119.119901

Relevant arXiv abstract extract:
  The experiment measures the permanent electric dipole moment of neutral
  199Hg atoms. The reported EDM is
    d_Hg = (2.20 +/- 2.75_stat +/- 1.48_syst) x 10^-30 e cm.
  The result is used to place the limit
    |d_Hg| < 7.4 x 10^-30 e cm at 95% C.L.
  The arXiv abstract states that this improved the previous limit by a factor
  of 4.
