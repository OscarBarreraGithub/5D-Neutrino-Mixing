Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler

Minimal extract for B007.

Abstract/context:
  The paper studies the flavor structure of 5D warped models with a
  composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs.  It re-examines flavor constraints on
  the new-physics mass scale in standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios,
  compares composite-Higgs flavor structure to RS flavor, and discusses
  additional flavor-violation sources that remain RS-GIM suppressed but
  enhance flavor-violating amplitudes.

Paper-era reference-scale statements:
  The abstract states that the KK gluon mass should generically be heavier
  than about 21 TeV in standard RS-type scenarios and at least about 33 TeV
  in the pseudo-Goldstone scenario.  These are theory reference scales, not
  measured observables for B007.
