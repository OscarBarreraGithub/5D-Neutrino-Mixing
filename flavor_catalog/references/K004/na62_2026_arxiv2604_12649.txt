Title: New measurement of K+ -> pi+ nu nubar branching ratio at the NA62 experiment
Authors: Xiafei Chang for the NA62 Collaboration
Year: 2026
Source status: 6-page contribution to the 2026 Electroweak session of the
60th Rencontres de Moriond; preliminary NA62 result.

Relevant extracted facts:
- Data sample: NA62 data collected in 2023--2024 are presented and combined
  with the earlier 2016--2022 sample.
- The arXiv abstract states that the experiment doubled its signal sample
  with the new data while reducing the background in proportion.
- Branching-ratio result used as the current experimental-equivalent value in
  this K004 draft:
  B(K+ -> pi+ nu nubar) = (9.6^{+1.9}_{-1.8}) x 10^{-11}.
- The abstract describes the result as compatible with the Standard Model
  prediction and with precision better than 20%.

Catalog extraction note:
This is newer than the published 2016--2022 observation paper and is therefore
used as the leading experimental number in the PKA draft, with the caveat that
it is a preliminary conference contribution rather than a journal publication.
