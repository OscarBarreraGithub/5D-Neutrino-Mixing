Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
Year: 2008

Relevant extracted facts:
- The abstract says the authors re-examine flavor constraints in standard
  Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios.
- The abstract quotes a generic RS KK-gluon mass bound of about 21 TeV.
- For the composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs scenario, the abstract quotes a
  stronger mass scale of at least about 33 TeV.
- The source is used here only as the requested 2008-era comparison baseline,
  not as a numerical input for the K+ -> pi+ nu nubar branching fraction.

Catalog extraction note:
This baseline is Delta F = 2 dominated and precedes the modern NA62 charged
rare-kaon measurements. It motivates the "post-2008 developments" subsection.
