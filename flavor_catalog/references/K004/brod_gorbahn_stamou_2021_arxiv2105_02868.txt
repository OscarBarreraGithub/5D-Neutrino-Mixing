URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2105.02868

Title: Updated Standard Model Prediction for K -> pi nu nubar and epsilon_K
Authors: Joachim Brod, Martin Gorbahn, Emmanuel Stamou
Year: 2021

Relevant extracted facts:
- The paper provides an updated Standard Model prediction for the rare
  K -> pi nu nubar decay modes and summarizes the SM status of epsilon_K.
- Charged-mode result:
  BR(K+ -> pi+ nu nubar) = 7.73(61) x 10^{-11}.
- Neutral-mode result:
  BR(K_L -> pi0 nu nubar) = 2.59(29) x 10^{-11}.
- The abstract states that the uncertainties are dominated by parametric input.

Catalog extraction note:
This is retained as an independent post-2008 SM prediction benchmark. The TeX
draft quotes the 2022 Buras-Venturini number as the comparison headline and
uses this source to show the recent range of SM inputs.
