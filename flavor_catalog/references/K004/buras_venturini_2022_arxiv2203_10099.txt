Title: Standard Model Predictions for Rare K and B Decays without |V_cb| and |V_ub| Uncertainties
Authors: Andrzej J. Buras and Elena Venturini
Year: 2022

Relevant extracted facts:
- The paper constructs rare-decay Standard Model predictions designed to avoid
  direct dependence on the inclusive/exclusive |V_cb| and |V_ub| tensions.
- The charged-kaon Standard Model benchmark used in this K004 draft is:
  B(K+ -> pi+ nu nubar)_SM = (8.60 +/- 0.42) x 10^{-11}.
- The same abstract quotes:
  B(K_L -> pi0 nu nubar)_SM = (2.94 +/- 0.15) x 10^{-11}.
- The authors describe these as determinations obtained under the assumption
  of no new physics in |epsilon_K| and S_{psi K_S}.
