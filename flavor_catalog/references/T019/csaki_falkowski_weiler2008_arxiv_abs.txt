Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
arXiv: 0804.1954v2
Submitted: 2008-04-14; last revised: 2008-04-24
Journal reference: JHEP 0809:008 (2008)
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008

Relevant abstract extract:

The paper studies flavor in 5D warped models with a composite pseudo-Goldstone
Higgs. It re-examines flavor constraints in standard Randall-Sundrum-type
scenarios, finds that the KK gluon mass should generically be heavier than
about 21 TeV, and in the composite-Higgs setup quotes a scale of at least
about 33 TeV under its assumptions. It frames the 2008 baseline before direct
LHC Higgs LFV searches existed.
