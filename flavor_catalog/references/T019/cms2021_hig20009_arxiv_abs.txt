Title:
Search for lepton-flavor violating decays of the Higgs boson in the mu tau
and e tau final states in proton-proton collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV

Authors: CMS Collaboration
arXiv: 2105.03007v2
Submitted: 2021-05-06; last revised: 2021-09-02
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. D 104, 032013 (2021)
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.104.032013

Relevant abstract extract:

The CMS search considers LFV Higgs decays to mu tau and e tau using
137 fb^-1 of pp collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV. No significant excess is
found, and results are interpreted as upper limits on LFV Higgs branching
fractions.

The observed (expected) upper limits on the branching fractions at 95% CL are:
  B(H -> mu tau) < 0.15% (0.15%).
  B(H -> e tau) < 0.22% (0.16%).
