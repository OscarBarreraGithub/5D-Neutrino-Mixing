Title: Flavor Violating Higgs Decays
Authors: Roni Harnik, Joachim Kopp, Jure Zupan
arXiv: 1209.1397v3
Submitted: 2012-09-06; last revised: 2015-08-14
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP03(2013)026

Relevant abstract extract:

The paper studies flavor-violating Higgs couplings to leptons and quarks in
new-physics frameworks including two-Higgs-doublet models, extra dimensions,
and compositeness. It rederives rare-decay, dipole-moment, and meson-oscillation
constraints and argues that h -> tau mu and h -> tau e branching ratios of
order 10% can be allowed by low-energy constraints. It also motivates
dedicated h -> tau mu and h -> tau e searches.
