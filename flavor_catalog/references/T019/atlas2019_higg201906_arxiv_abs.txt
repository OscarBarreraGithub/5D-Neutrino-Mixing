Title:
Searches for lepton-flavour-violating decays of the Higgs boson in
sqrt(s) = 13 TeV pp collisions with the ATLAS detector

Authors: ATLAS Collaboration
arXiv: 1907.06131v2
Submitted: 2019-07-13; last revised: 2020-02-03
Journal reference: Phys. Lett. B 801 (2020) 135069
DOI: 10.1016/j.physletb.2019.135069

Relevant abstract extract:

The ATLAS Letter searches for H -> e tau and H -> mu tau using
36.1 fb^-1 at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV. No significant excess over Standard Model
background is observed.

The observed (median expected) 95% CL upper limits on the LFV branching ratios
are:
  B(H -> e tau) < 0.47% with median expected 0.34% (+0.13%, -0.10%).
  B(H -> mu tau) < 0.28% with median expected 0.37% (+0.14%, -0.10%).
