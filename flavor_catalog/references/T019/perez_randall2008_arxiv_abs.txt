Title: Natural Neutrino Masses and Mixings from Warped Geometry
Authors: Gilad Perez, Lisa Randall
arXiv: 0805.4652v3
Submitted: 2008-05-30; last revised: 2009-01-28
Journal reference: JHEP 01 (2009) 077
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2009/01/077

Relevant abstract extract:

The paper shows how flavor symmetries in warped geometry can distinguish the
quark and lepton flavor sectors and generate neutrino masses. With lepton
minimal flavor violation, a GIM-like mechanism suppresses dangerous
charged-lepton flavor-changing processes, permitting a cutoff of 10 TeV and
3 TeV Kaluza-Klein masses in the model considered.
