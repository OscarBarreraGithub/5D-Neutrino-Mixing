Title: Precision Measurement of the Ratio of the Charged Kaon Leptonic Decay Rates
Authors: NA62 Collaboration, C. Lazzeroni et al.

Abstract-value excerpt:
The NA62 experiment at CERN collected a dedicated charged-kaon sample during
2007-2008 and measured the ratio R_K = Gamma(K+- -> e+- nu) /
Gamma(K+- -> mu+- nu).  The paper reports
R_K = (2.488 +- 0.007_stat +- 0.007_syst) x 10^{-5}.

Catalog extraction notes:
This is the dominant modern measurement used in the PDG S010R28 average.  The
PDG listing footnote states that the ratio is fully inclusive, including
internal bremsstrahlung.  Direct-emission radiation is treated separately in the
experimental convention.
