Title: pi/K -> e nu branching ratios to O(e^2 p^4) in Chiral Perturbation Theory
Authors: Vincenzo Cirigliano and Ignasi Rosell

Value excerpt for kaon LFU:
The Standard Model prediction used by the kaon LFU literature is
R_K^SM = (2.477 +- 0.001) x 10^{-5}.

Catalog extraction notes:
The calculation includes radiative corrections through O(e^2 p^4) in chiral
perturbation theory.  The same number is quoted by later experimental and PDG
summaries as the SM reference for R_K comparisons.
