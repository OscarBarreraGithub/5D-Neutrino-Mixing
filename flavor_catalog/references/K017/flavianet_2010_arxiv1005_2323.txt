Title: An evaluation of |V_us| and precise tests of the Standard Model from world data on leptonic and semileptonic kaon decays
Authors: FlaviaNet Working Group on Kaon Decays

Scope excerpt:
The FlaviaNet analysis evaluates world kaon data for K_l3 and K_mu2 decays,
tests CKM unitarity, and discusses tests of lepton universality in kaon
semileptonic and leptonic observables.  It provides the post-2008 kaon-decay
global-fit context used by later PDG and experiment summaries.
