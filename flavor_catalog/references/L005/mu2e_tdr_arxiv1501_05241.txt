Minimal extracted use note:
The abstract states that Mu2e searches for charged-lepton flavor violation via
coherent mu- N -> e- N conversion and aims for sensitivity approximately four
orders of magnitude better than the current world's best limits for this
process.

Use in L005:
Post-2008 development showing that the SINDRUM II titanium bound now sits in a
broader next-generation conversion-search program, even though Mu2e uses
aluminum rather than titanium.
