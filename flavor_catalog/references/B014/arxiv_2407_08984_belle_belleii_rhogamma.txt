Title: Measurement of branching fractions, CP asymmetry, and isospin asymmetry for B -> rho gamma decays using Belle and Belle II data
Authors: Belle II Collaboration, I. Adachi et al.
Published: 2024-07-12

Abstract-page value extract:
  Data samples: 772 x 10^6 B Bbar pairs from Belle and 387 x 10^6 B Bbar pairs from Belle II at the Upsilon(4S).
  Fitted yields: 114 +/- 12 B+ -> rho+ gamma decays and 99 +/- 12 B0 -> rho0 gamma decays.
  B(B+ -> rho+ gamma) = (13.1 +2.0 +1.3 -1.9 -1.2) x 10^-7.
  B(B0 -> rho0 gamma) = (7.5 +/- 1.3 +1.0 -0.8) x 10^-7.
  First uncertainty is statistical and second is systematic.
  A_I(B -> rho gamma) = (10.9 +11.2 +7.8 -11.7 -7.3)%.
  A_CP(B+ -> rho+ gamma) = (-8.2 +/- 15.2 +1.6 -1.2)%.
