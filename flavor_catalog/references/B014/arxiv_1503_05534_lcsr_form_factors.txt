Title: B -> V l+ l- in the Standard Model from Light-Cone Sum Rules
Authors: Aoife Bharucha, David M. Straub, Roman Zwicky
Published: 2015-03-18
Journal reference: JHEP 08 (2016) 098
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP08(2016)098

Abstract-page extract:
  The paper presents B_q -> rho, B_q -> omega, B_q -> K*, B_s -> K*, and B_s -> phi form factors from light-cone sum rules.
  It provides fits to the LCSR results, including a full error correlation matrix, and discusses tensor-to-vector form-factor ratios and SM predictions for radiative and semileptonic B -> V modes.

Role for B014: post-2008 theory input for exclusive B -> rho/omega gamma form factors and the model-dependence of translating branching fractions into short-distance dipole constraints.
