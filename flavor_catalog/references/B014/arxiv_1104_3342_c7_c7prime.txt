Title: Exploring New Physics in the C7-C7' plane
Authors: Sebastien Descotes-Genon, Diptimoy Ghosh, Joaquim Matias, Marc Ramon
Published: 2011-04-17
Journal reference: JHEP 06 (2011) 099
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP06(2011)099

Abstract-page extract:
  The paper studies constraints on the magnitude and sign of the electromagnetic dipole Wilson coefficient C7 and its chirality-flipped counterparts.
  It uses radiative and semileptonic B observables such as B -> X_s gamma and B -> K* gamma and provides semi-numerical expressions in terms of low-scale Wilson coefficients.

Role for B014: post-2008 Wilson-coefficient context for chirality-enhanced electromagnetic dipoles; directly applicable in structure to b -> d gamma even though the quoted observables are mainly b -> s transitions.
