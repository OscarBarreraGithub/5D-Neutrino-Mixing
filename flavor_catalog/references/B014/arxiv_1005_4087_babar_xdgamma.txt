Abstract-page value extract:
  Data sample: 471 million B Bbar events collected with BaBar.
  The analysis studies seven exclusive final states B -> X_s(d) gamma with hadronic mass up to 2.0 GeV/c^2.
  After correcting for unobserved decay modes:
    B(b -> d gamma) = (9.2 +/- 2.0(stat.) +/- 2.3(syst.)) x 10^-6 in this mass range.
    B(b -> s gamma) = (23.0 +/- 0.8(stat.) +/- 3.0(syst.)) x 10^-5 in the same mass range.
    BF(b -> d gamma) / BF(b -> s gamma) = 0.040 +/- 0.009(stat.) +/- 0.010(syst.).
    |V_td/V_ts| = 0.199 +/- 0.022(stat.) +/- 0.024(syst.) +/- 0.002(th.).

Role for B014: post-2008 semi-inclusive b -> d gamma context and CKM-ratio comparison to b -> s gamma.
