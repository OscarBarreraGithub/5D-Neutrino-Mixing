Page header:
Combination of Direct and Indirect CP Violation
(updated 16 April 2025)

Definitions and fit inputs:
Delta A_CP = A_CP(KK) - A_CP(pi pi).
The combination uses measurements of Delta A_CP and A_Gamma to fit
Delta a_CP^dir and a_CP^ind.  HFLAV states that statistical and systematic
uncertainties are added in quadrature when calculating the chi^2.

Selected rows from the HFLAV table:
2019 LHCb, Delta A_CP = (-0.09 +/- 0.08 stat +/- 0.05 syst)%,
6 fb^-1 at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV, B -> D0 mu X flavor tag,
reference: R. Aaij et al. (LHCb Collaboration), arXiv:1903.08726.

2019 LHCb, Delta A_CP = (-0.182 +/- 0.032 stat +/- 0.009 syst)%,
6 fb^-1 at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV, prompt D* flavor tag,
reference: R. Aaij et al. (LHCb Collaboration), arXiv:1903.08726.

Fit result as displayed by HFLAV:
Agreement with no CP violation: CL = 6.9 x 10^-8.
Text below the plot: Delta chi^2 from the no-CPV point is 32.3,
corresponding to CL = 9.7 x 10^-8 for two degrees of freedom,
or 5.3 standard deviations.

Central values and one-standard-deviation errors:
a_CP^ind = (-0.010 +/- 0.012)%
Delta a_CP^dir = (-0.159 +/- 0.029)%
