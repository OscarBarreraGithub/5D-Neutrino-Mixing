Record title: Search for resonant and nonresonant new phenomena in high-mass dilepton final states at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV
Collaboration: CMS
Publication year: 2021

Relevant HEPData record extract:
The record describes a CMS search using electron or muon pairs with high
invariant mass in proton-proton collisions at sqrt(s)=13 TeV from 2016-2018,
with integrated luminosity up to 140 fb^{-1}. No significant deviation from
the standard-model background is observed.

The record states that upper limits are set on the ratio of production cross
section times branching fraction for a new narrow dilepton resonance relative
to the Z boson, and that these limits are converted into lower limits on
hypothetical-particle masses. It gives the same summary limits used here:
a spin-1 Z'_SSM is excluded below 5.15 TeV and a spin-1 Z'_psi below 4.56 TeV
at 95% confidence level.
