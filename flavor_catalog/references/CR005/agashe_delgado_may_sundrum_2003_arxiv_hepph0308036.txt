Abstract extract:
The paper studies precision electroweak constraints in an RS1 model with
bulk gauge fields and fermions. It enlarges the electroweak gauge symmetry to
SU(2)_L x SU(2)_R x U(1)_{B-L}, providing a custodial isospin symmetry that
suppresses excessive contributions to the T parameter. The models are
presented as complete hierarchy-problem solutions with distinctive collider
signals.

CR005 use:
This is a custodial-RS baseline explaining why collider limits on electroweak
KK gauge resonances are model-dependent: the same custodial structure that
softens precision electroweak constraints changes the coupling and branching
pattern of the neutral resonances.
