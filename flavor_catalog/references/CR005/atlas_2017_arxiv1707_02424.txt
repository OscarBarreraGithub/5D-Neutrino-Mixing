Title: Search for new high-mass phenomena in the dilepton final state using 36 fb^{-1} of proton-proton collision data at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV with the ATLAS detector
Authors: ATLAS Collaboration
Published: 2017-07-08
Updated: 2017-11-15
Primary category: hep-ex
Report number: CERN-EP-2017-119
Journal reference: JHEP 10 (2017) 182
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP10(2017)182

Abstract extract:
ATLAS searches for resonant and non-resonant high-mass phenomena in
dielectron and dimuon final states using 36.1 fb^{-1} of 13 TeV pp data from
2015 and 2016. No significant deviation from the Standard Model is observed.
The paper sets 95% credibility-level upper limits on cross section times
branching ratio for dilepton resonances, converting them into lower mass
limits for benchmark models, and also sets contact-interaction scale limits.
