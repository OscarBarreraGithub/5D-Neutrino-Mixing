Title: Search for high-mass resonances in dilepton final states in proton-proton collisions at sqrt(s)=13 TeV
Authors: CMS Collaboration
Published: 2018-03-16
Updated: 2018-07-03
Primary category: hep-ex
Report number: CMS-EXO-16-047, CERN-EP-2018-027
Journal reference: JHEP 06 (2018) 120
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP06(2018)120

Abstract extract:
CMS searches for new high-mass resonances decaying to electron or muon pairs
using 36 fb^{-1} of 13 TeV pp data collected in 2016. Observations agree with
standard-model expectations. The paper calculates upper limits on production
cross section times branching fraction to dileptons and interprets the
limits for narrow spin-1 resonances. It reports a 95% CL lower mass limit of
4.50 TeV for Z'_SSM and 3.90 TeV for Z'_psi.

CR005 use:
This is the 2016-data CMS predecessor to the full 2016-2018 CMS result used
as the canonical current CR005 limit.
