Record title: Search for high-mass dilepton resonances using 139 fb^{-1} of pp collision data collected at sqrt(s)=13 TeV with the ATLAS detector
Collaboration: ATLAS
Publication year: 2019

Relevant HEPData record extract:
The record describes an ATLAS search for high-mass dielectron and dimuon
resonances in the 250 GeV to 6 TeV mass range. The data were recorded at
sqrt(s)=13 TeV during LHC Run 2 and correspond to 139 fb^{-1}. Limits are set
on the fiducial cross section times branching ratio to dielectrons and
dimuons for generic resonances with relative natural widths from zero to 10%.

Relevant data tables listed by HEPData:
  - Observed dilepton cross-section limits
  - Expected dilepton cross-section limits
  - Ratio of observed limits to SSM cross section
  - Observed exclusion contours on Heavy Vector Triplet couplings

This CR005 draft uses the paper text for the numerical ATLAS 5.1 TeV Z'_SSM
limit and the about-0.014 fb high-mass fiducial cross-section limit, with
this HEPData record retained as the structured-table provenance.
