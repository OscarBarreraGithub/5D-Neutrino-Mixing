Title: Search for high-mass resonances decaying to dilepton final states in pp collisions at a center-of-mass energy of 7 TeV with the ATLAS detector
Authors: ATLAS Collaboration
Published: 2012-09-12
Updated: 2013-03-25
Primary category: hep-ex
Report number: CERN-PH-EP-2012-190
Journal reference: JHEP 1211 (2012) 138
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP11(2012)138

Abstract extract:
ATLAS searches for high-mass e+e- and mu+mu- resonances in 7 TeV pp data,
with 4.9 fb^{-1} in the dielectron channel and 5.0 fb^{-1} in the dimuon
channel. The search is sensitive to heavy neutral Z' gauge bosons,
Randall-Sundrum gravitons, Z* bosons, techni-mesons, Kaluza-Klein Z/gamma
bosons, and Torsion-model bosons. The paper reports a 95% CL exclusion of a
Standard-Model-like Z' below 2.22 TeV.

CR005 use:
This is a post-2010 Run-1 history point showing that high-mass dilepton
resonance searches already included KK Z/gamma interpretations before the
full Run-2 ATLAS/CMS limits.
