Title: A search for heavy Kaluza-Klein electroweak gauge bosons at the LHC
Authors: Gideon Bella, Erez Etzion, Noam Hod, Yaron Oz, Yiftah Silver, Mark Sutton
Published: 2010-04-14
Primary category: hep-ph
Journal reference: JHEP 09 (2010) 025
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP09(2010)025

Abstract extract:
The paper studies the feasibility of observing a leptonic Kaluza-Klein hard
process in pp collisions at the LHC. The focus is on KK excitations of the
Standard Model gamma and Z^0 gauge bosons in an S^1/Z_2 TeV^{-1} extra
dimension, using the hard process
  f fbar -> sum_n (gamma*/Z*)_n -> F Fbar,
where F is a final-state lepton.

CR005 use:
This is the closest process-level theory reference for the CR005 final state,
although it is not the same as a custodial anarchic-flavor RS model. It
anchors the notation pp -> (gamma^(1), Z^(1))_KK -> l+ l-.
