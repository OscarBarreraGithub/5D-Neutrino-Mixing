Abstract extract:
The paper studies the consequences of putting Standard Model gauge fields in
the bulk of the Randall-Sundrum localized-gravity model. It finds strongly
coupled Kaluza-Klein excitations of these gauge fields and emphasizes the
tension with precision electroweak data in the minimal bulk-gauge setup.

CR005 use:
This source is a paper-era theory baseline for bulk electroweak gauge
excitations. Its precision-electroweak bounds are theory/reference context,
not current measured LHC observables and therefore are not promoted to
pdg_or_equivalent.
