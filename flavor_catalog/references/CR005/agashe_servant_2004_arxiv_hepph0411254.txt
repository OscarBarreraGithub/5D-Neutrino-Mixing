Abstract extract:
The paper embeds an SU(2)_L x SU(2)_R x U(1)_{B-L} warped electroweak
structure into SO(10)- or Pati-Salam-like unified models and discusses
phenomenology associated with light Kaluza-Klein fermions and warped-GUT
model building.

CR005 use:
This source is retained as Agashe-Servant warped-model context for extended
electroweak/custodial structures. It is not used to define a measured
dilepton mass limit.
