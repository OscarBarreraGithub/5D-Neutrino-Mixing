Title:
Z lepton flavour violation as a probe for new physics at future e+e- colliders

Authors:
Lorenzo Calibbi, Xabier Marcano, Joydeep Roy

DOI:
10.1140/epjc/s10052-021-09777-3

Relevant abstract extract:
The work assesses searches for lepton-flavour-violating Z decays,
Z -> l_i l_j, at proposed circular e+e- colliders CEPC and FCC-ee. Both
projects plan to run at the Z pole as a Tera Z factory, collecting
O(10^12) Z decays. The paper revisits LFV Z decays in Standard Model
effective field theory and studies indirect constraints from LFV mu and tau
decays on operators that can induce Z -> l_i l_j. It finds that Z -> mu e
rates are beyond expected sensitivities, while a Tera Z factory is promising
for Z -> tau l decays.
