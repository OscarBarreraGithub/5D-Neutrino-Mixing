Title:
Search for charged lepton flavor violating Z and Z' boson decays in
proton-proton collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV

Authors:
CMS Collaboration

Journal reference:
Phys. Rev. D 112 (2025) 112011

DOI:
10.1103/8lw4-6lyk

Relevant abstract extract:
A search for flavor violating decays of the Z boson to charged leptons is
performed using data from proton-proton collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV
collected with the CMS detector at the LHC, corresponding to an integrated
luminosity of 138 fb^-1. Each of the decays Z -> e mu, Z -> e tau, and
Z -> mu tau is considered. The data are consistent with the backgrounds
expected from standard model processes. For the Z -> e mu channel the observed
(expected) 95% confidence level upper limit on the branching fraction is
1.9 (2.0) x 10^-7; the corresponding limits for the Z -> e tau and
Z -> mu tau channels are 13.8 (11.4) x 10^-6 and 12.0 (5.3) x 10^-6,
respectively.
