Title:
Natural Neutrino Masses and Mixings from Warped Geometry

Authors:
Gilad Perez, Lisa Randall

Journal reference:
JHEP01(2009)077

DOI:
10.1088/1126-6708/2009/01/077

Relevant abstract extract:
The paper shows how flavor symmetries in warped geometry can explain large
lepton mixing angles and the distinction between quark and lepton flavor
sectors. The model uses lepton minimal flavor violation, in which only Yukawa
matrices break the flavor symmetries. A GIM-like mechanism prevents dangerous
charged-lepton flavor-changing processes and allows consistency with a cutoff
of 10 TeV and 3 TeV Kaluza-Klein masses when the neutrino Yukawa scale is
slightly smaller than one in curvature units.
