Title:
The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs

Authors:
Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler

Journal reference:
JHEP 0809:008, 2008

DOI:
10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008

Relevant abstract extract:
The paper studies the flavor structure of 5D warped models with a composite
pseudo-Goldstone Higgs. It re-examines flavor constraints on new-physics mass
scales in Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios and finds that the KK gluon mass
should generically be heavier than about 21 TeV. In the composite-Higgs
scenario, kinetic mixing and gauge-sized Yukawa couplings imply a KK gluon
mass of at least about 33 TeV. The abstract emphasizes that strong flavor
bounds suggest that fully anarchic warped flavor is implausible without at
least partial flavor symmetries.
