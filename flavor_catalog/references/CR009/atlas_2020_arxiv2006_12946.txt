Title: Search for new non-resonant phenomena in high-mass dilepton final
states with the ATLAS detector
Authors: ATLAS Collaboration
Journal reference: JHEP 11 (2020) 005; erratum JHEP 04 (2021) 142
Report number: CERN-EP-2020-066

Abstract extract:
ATLAS searches for non-resonant new physics in dielectron and dimuon final
states above 2 TeV using 139 fb^-1 of 13 TeV pp data from Run 2. The
benchmark signal is a two-quark/two-lepton contact interaction that would
enhance the dilepton event rate in the TeV-scale tail. A functional form is
fit to the lower-mass data and extrapolated into the signal region. No
significant deviation is observed.

Result extract:
The arXiv abstract reports 95% CL upper limits on event counts and visible
cross-section times branching fraction for new-physics processes. The
observed (expected) 95% CL lower contact-interaction scale reaches
35.8 (37.6) TeV.
