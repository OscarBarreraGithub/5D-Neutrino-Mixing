1. arXiv:1112.4462, ATLAS, "Search for contact interactions in dilepton
events from pp collisions at sqrt(s) = 7 TeV with the ATLAS detector".
The abstract reports 2011 ATLAS dielectron and dimuon data at 7 TeV with
1.08 fb^-1 and 1.21 fb^-1, respectively. No significant deviation is found.
For the LL isoscalar model and a prior flat in 1/Lambda^2, ATLAS sets
95% CL lower limits Lambda^- > 10.1 TeV and Lambda^+ > 9.4 TeV in the
electron channel, and Lambda^- > 8.0 TeV and Lambda^+ > 7.0 TeV in the
muon channel.

2. arXiv:1407.2410, ATLAS, "Search for contact interactions and large
extra dimensions in the dilepton channel using proton-proton collisions at
sqrt(s) = 8 TeV with the ATLAS detector". The abstract reports 20 fb^-1
at 8 TeV. The dilepton invariant-mass spectrum and forward-backward
asymmetry are used. No significant deviation is observed; 95% credibility
level lower limits on llqq Lambda range from 15.4 to 26.3 TeV.

3. arXiv:1412.6302, CMS, "Search for physics beyond the standard model in
dilepton mass spectra in proton-proton collisions at sqrt(s) = 8 TeV". The
abstract reports 8 TeV dimuon and dielectron spectra with 20.6 fb^-1 and
19.7 fb^-1. No evidence for BSM physics is observed. In the left-left
isoscalar contact model, CMS quotes 95% CL Lambda lower limits of
12.0 (15.2) TeV for destructive (constructive) interference in the dimuon
channel and 13.5 (18.3) TeV in the dielectron channel.

4. arXiv:1607.03669, ATLAS, "Search for high-mass new phenomena in the
dilepton final state using proton-proton collisions at sqrt(s)=13 TeV with
the ATLAS detector". The abstract reports 3.2 fb^-1 at 13 TeV from 2015.
No significant deviation is found; 95% credibility-level lower limits on
the llqq contact-interaction scale range from 16.7 to 25.2 TeV.

5. arXiv:1707.02424, ATLAS, "Search for new high-mass phenomena in the
dilepton final state using 36 fb^-1 of proton-proton collision data at
sqrt(s) = 13 TeV with the ATLAS detector". The abstract reports 36.1 fb^-1
at 13 TeV from 2015-2016. No significant deviation is observed; lower
limits on the qqll contact-interaction scale range from 24 to 40 TeV,
depending on the model.

6. arXiv:1812.10443, CMS, "Search for contact interactions and large extra
dimensions in the dilepton mass spectra from proton-proton collisions at
sqrt(s) = 13 TeV". The abstract reports 36 fb^-1 at 13 TeV from 2016. No
significant deviation is observed; contact-interaction lower limits range
from 20 to 32 TeV, and ADD cutoff limits are also reported.
