Record:
CMS, 2021, "Search for resonant and nonresonant new phenomena in high-mass
dilepton final states at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV", JHEP 07 (2021) 208.

HEPData abstract extract:
The record states that the analysis uses 13 TeV pp collisions recorded in
2016-2018 with up to 140 fb^-1, observes no significant deviation from the
SM background expectation, and sets lower limits on the four-fermion
contact-interaction ultraviolet cutoff parameter Lambda. The HEPData record
summarizes the CI lower-limit range as 23.6--36.6 TeV; the CMS paper text
and CMS public page give the rounded published range 23.8/23.9--36.4 TeV.

Relevant HEPData tables:
- Table 30: Limits on the CI scale Lambda in the dielectron channel.
- Table 31: Limits on the CI scale Lambda in the dimuon channel.
- Table 32: Limits on the CI scale Lambda for the combined channels.
- Table 37: Data and background estimate for the CI limit calculation.
- Table 39: Bin-by-bin correlation for the CI limit calculation.

CR009 use:
HEPData is used as structured support for the CMS CI-limit measurement and
for the statement that a full reinterpretation would need binned
likelihood-style information rather than only the headline Lambda bounds.
