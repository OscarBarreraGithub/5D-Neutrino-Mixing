Why this is included for E006:
  PDG does not present the 199Hg atom EDM as a standalone particle-listing
  observable in this datablock.  Instead, the neutron EDM listing records
  theory-derived neutron limits that use the measured 199Hg atom EDM.

Relevant PDG rows:
  S017EDM neutron EDM table units: 10^-25 e cm.
  Sahoo 2017 row: <0.22 at 95% CL, comment "199Hg atom EDM + theory".
  Graner 2016 row: <0.16 at 95% CL, comment "MRS 199Hg atom EDM + theory".

Relevant PDG note:
  Sahoo 2017 develops theory to calculate the neutron EDM limit from the
  measured limit by Graner 2016 of the 199Hg atom EDM.

  Electric Dipole Moment of 199Hg".
