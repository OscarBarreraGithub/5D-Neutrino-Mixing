Experimental system:
  Neutral 199Hg atoms in fused silica vapor cells, using optical pumping and
  Faraday rotation to measure an electric-field-induced perturbation to the
  Larmor precession frequency.

Numerical result recorded by the arXiv abstract:
  d_Hg = (2.20 +/- 2.75_stat +/- 1.48_syst) x 10^-30 e cm.

Canonical limit:
  |d_Hg| < 7.4 x 10^-30 e cm at 95% C.L.

Notes:
  The abstract states that this improves the previous limit by a factor of 4
  and discusses implications for CP-violating observables beyond the Standard
  Model.
  PDG Live S017EDM cites the published PRL and its PRL 119, 119901 (2017)
  erratum in the neutron EDM datablock because the Hg atom result is used
  for theory-derived neutron EDM limits.
