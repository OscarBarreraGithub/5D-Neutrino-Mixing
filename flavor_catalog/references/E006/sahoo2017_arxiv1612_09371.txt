Scope:
  Combines the recently reported 199Hg atom EDM with improved relativistic
  atomic calculations to constrain tensor-pseudotensor electron-nucleus
  coupling and nuclear Schiff-moment interactions.

Selected numerical limits from the arXiv abstract:
  d_n < 2.2 x 10^-26 |e| cm.
  d_p < 2.1 x 10^-25 |e| cm.
  |theta_bar| < 1.1 x 10^-10.
  |tilde{d}_u - tilde{d}_d| < 5.5 x 10^-27 |e| cm.

Catalog note:
  These are interpretation-level limits from a diamagnetic atom, not direct
  measurements of neutron, proton, or quark chromo-EDMs.  They are useful for
  the E006 model-dependence discussion and for later E008/E009 cross-checks.
