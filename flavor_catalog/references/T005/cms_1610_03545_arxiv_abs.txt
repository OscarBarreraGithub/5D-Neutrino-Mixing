Title:
Search for anomalous Wtb couplings and flavour-changing neutral currents in
t-channel single top quark production in pp collisions at sqrt(s) = 7 and
8 TeV

Authors: CMS Collaboration
Submitted: 2016-10-11; last revised: 2017-02-10, version v2
Journal reference: JHEP 02 (2017) 028
Related DOI: 10.1007/JHEP02(2017)028
arXiv DOI: 10.48550/arXiv.1610.03545

Relevant arXiv abstract values:

The CMS analysis uses t-channel single top-quark events in pp collisions at
sqrt(s) = 7 and 8 TeV, corresponding to integrated luminosities of
5.0 fb^{-1} and 19.7 fb^{-1}, respectively. Events with one muon and two or
three jets are analyzed with a Bayesian neural-network discriminant.

For the FCNC couplings kappa_tug and kappa_tcg, the observed 95% CL upper
limits are:
- |kappa_tug| / Lambda < 4.1e-3 TeV^{-1}.
- |kappa_tcg| / Lambda < 1.8e-2 TeV^{-1}.

The corresponding FCNC top-decay branching-fraction limits are:
- B(t -> u g) < 2.0e-5.
- B(t -> c g) < 4.1e-4.
