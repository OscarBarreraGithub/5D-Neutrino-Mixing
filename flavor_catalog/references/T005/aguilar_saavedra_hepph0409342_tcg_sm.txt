Top flavour-changing neutral interactions: theoretical expectations and

Relevant TeX-source excerpts:

Section 2 gives the Standard Model calculation for top FCNC interactions.
For t -> c g, the paper writes
Br(t -> c g) = (4.6^{+1.1}_{-0.9} +/- 0.2 +/- 0.4
^{+2.1}_{-0.7}) x 10^{-12}.

Table 1 collects branching ratios for top FCN decays. The Standard Model
entry for t -> c g is 4.6 x 10^{-12}. The table also lists representative
extended-model scales, including t -> c g values of about 1.5e-7 for
up-type quark singlets, about 1e-4 for a general 2HDM, about 1e-8 for a
flavour-conserving 2HDM, about 8e-5 in the MSSM, and about 2e-4 with
R-parity-violating SUSY.

The paper emphasizes that top FCNC interactions are negligible in the SM but
can be enhanced in extensions, so an observed t -> c g signal would indicate
new physics.
