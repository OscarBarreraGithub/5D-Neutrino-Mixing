Title: Searches for Lepton Flavor Violation in the Decays tau -> e gamma
       and tau -> mu gamma
Authors: B. Aubert et al. (BaBar Collaboration)
Journal: Phys. Rev. Lett. 104 (2010) 021802
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.104.021802
Submitted: 2009-08-17; last revised: 2010-01-25

Relevant excerpt:

Searches for lepton-flavor-violating decay of a tau lepton to a lighter
mass lepton and a photon were performed with the entire data set of
(963 +- 7) x 10^6 tau decays collected by BaBar near the Upsilon(4S),
Upsilon(3S), and Upsilon(2S) resonances. No evidence of signals was found.
The 90% confidence-level upper limits were:

  BR(tau -> e gamma)  < 3.3 x 10^-8
  BR(tau -> mu gamma) < 4.4 x 10^-8

Extraction note:
This is the primary experiment behind the current PDG L008 tau -> e gamma
limit. It is a post-2008 full-data BaBar result and remains stronger than
the later Belle full-data limit for the electron mode.
