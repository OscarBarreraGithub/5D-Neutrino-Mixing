Relevant text extract from the t' listing:

  Citation: S. Navas et al. (Particle Data Group), Phys. Rev. D 110,
  030001 (2024) and 2025 update.

  t' (2/3)-quark/hadron mass limits in pp and p pbar collisions
  VALUE (GeV)  CL%  DOCUMENT ID    TECN  COMMENT
  >1530        95   AAD 24BP       ATLS  B(t' -> W q) = 1 (q=d,s)
  >1700        95   AAD 24N        ATLS  B(t' -> W b) = 1
  >1360        95   AAD 24N        ATLS  B(t' -> W b : H t : Z t) = 1/2 : 1/4 : 1/4
  >1600        95   AAD 23AV       ATLS  B(t' -> Z t) = 1
  >1500        95   TUMASYAN 23V   CMS   B(t' -> h t) = 1
  >1310        95   AABOUD 18CR    ATLS  singlet t'. ATLAS combination.
  >1370        95   AABOUD 18CR    ATLS  t' in a weak isospin doublet (t', b'). ATLAS combination.

  AAD 24N note: based on 140 fb^-1 of pp data at sqrt(s)=13 TeV.
  Pair production of vector-like t' is searched for in the lepton+jets
  mode. The data are consistent with the SM background predictions and
  limits are obtained for different branching ratios.

  AABOUD 18CR note: based on 36.1 fb^-1 of pp data at sqrt(s)=13 TeV.
  A combination of searches for the pair-produced vector-like t' in
  various decay channels (t' -> W b, Z t, h t). Also a model-independent
  limit is obtained as m_t' > 1.31 TeV, assuming that the branching
  ratios into Z t, W b, and h t add up to one. The weak-isospin-doublet
  limit is for t' in a doublet (t', b') and |V_t'b| << |V_tb'|.

Relevant text extract from the b' listing:

  Citation: S. Navas et al. (Particle Data Group), Phys. Rev. D 110,
  030001 (2024) and 2025 update.

  b' (-1/3)-quark/hadron mass limits in pp and p pbar collisions
  VALUE (GeV)  CL%  DOCUMENT ID       TECN  COMMENT
  >1530        95   AAD 24BP          ATLS  B(b' -> W u) = 1
  >1540        95   HAYRAPETYAN 24AQ  CMS   B(b' -> Z b) = 1
  >1570        95   HAYRAPETYAN 24AQ  CMS   B(b' -> H b) = 1
  >1560        95   TUMASYAN 23V      CMS   B(b' -> W t) = 1
  >1220        95   AABOUD 18CR       ATLS  singlet b'. ATLAS combination.
  >1370        95   AABOUD 18CR       ATLS  b' in a weak isospin doublet (t', b'). ATLAS combination.

  HAYRAPETYAN 24AQ note: based on 138 fb^-1 of pp data at sqrt(s)=13 TeV.
  Pair production of vector-like b' is searched for in fully hadronic
  final states and those containing l+ l- from a Z boson. The data are
  consistent with the SM background predictions and limits are obtained
  for different branching ratios B(b' -> H b), B(b' -> Z b), B(b' -> W t).

  TUMASYAN 23V note: based on 138 fb^-1 of pp data at sqrt(s)=13 TeV.
  Pair production of vector-like b' is searched for in the single-lepton,
  same-sign charge dilepton and multilepton channels. The data are
  consistent with the SM background predictions and limits are obtained
  for different branching ratios.

  AABOUD 18CR note: based on 36.1 fb^-1 of pp data at sqrt(s)=13 TeV.
  A combination of searches for the pair-produced vector-like b' in
  various decay channels (b' -> W t, Z b, h b). Also a model-independent
  limit is obtained as m_b' > 1.03 TeV, assuming that the branching
  ratios into Z b, W t, and h b add up to one. The weak-isospin-doublet
  limit is for b' in a doublet (t', b') and |V_t'b| << |V_tb'|.
