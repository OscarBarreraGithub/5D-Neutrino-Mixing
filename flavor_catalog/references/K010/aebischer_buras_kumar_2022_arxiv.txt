Title: On the Importance of Rare Kaon Decays: A Snowmass 2021 White Paper
Authors: Jason Aebischer, Andrzej J. Buras, Jacky Kumar
Submitted: 2022-03-17

Relevant arXiv abstract-page text snapshot:

The white paper stresses the importance of precise measurements of rare kaon
decays including K_{L,S}->mu+mu- and K_{L,S}->pi0 l+l- for new-physics
searches. It emphasizes both branching ratios and q^2 distributions, their
correlations with epsilon'/epsilon, epsilon_K, and Delta M_K, and the role of
SMEFT in interpreting short-distance physics.
