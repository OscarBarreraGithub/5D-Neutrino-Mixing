Title: The rare decay KL -> pi0 mu+ mu- within the SM
Authors: Gino Isidori, Christopher Smith, Rene Unterdorfer
Submitted: 2004-04-15

Relevant arXiv abstract-page text snapshot:

The authors present an updated Standard Model analysis of rare
K_L -> pi0 mu+ mu- and discuss the role of the two-photon CP-conserving
amplitude. The abstract states that the error in the SM prediction is mainly
parametric and could be substantially reduced with better data on the
K_S -> pi0 l+ l- modes. It also discusses differential distributions and
sensitivity to possible new-physics effects.

Numeric value in the abstract, retained only as paper-era theory context and
not as the K010 measured observable:
  B(K_L -> pi0 mu+ mu-)_SM = (1.5 +/- 0.3) x 10^{-11}.
