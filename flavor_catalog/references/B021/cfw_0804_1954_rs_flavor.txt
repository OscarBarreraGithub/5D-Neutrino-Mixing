Context extract:
  The paper re-examines flavor constraints in Randall-Sundrum-type warped
  models and reports that the KK gluon mass should generically be heavier than
  about 21 TeV in standard RS scenarios.
  It emphasizes RS-GIM suppression, possible stronger bounds in composite
  pseudo-Goldstone Higgs variants, and the model dependence from the gluon
  boundary kinetic term.
