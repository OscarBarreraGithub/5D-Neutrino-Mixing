Title: Lambda_b -> Lambda l+ l- form factors and differential branching fraction from lattice QCD
Local snapshot type: minimal arXiv metadata and summary; no publisher PDF stored.

Summary extract:
  The paper presents the first lattice-QCD determination of Lambda_b -> Lambda
  transition form factors governing rare Lambda_b -> Lambda l+ l- decays at
  leading order in heavy-quark effective theory.
  The calculation uses 2+1 flavors of domain-wall fermions, two lattice
  spacings, and pion masses down to 227 MeV.
  The authors use the form factors to calculate SM differential branching
  fractions for l = e, mu, tau and compare to the then-recent CDF measurement.

Catalog note:
  This is a post-2008 theory input showing why B021 cannot be reduced to the
  mesonic B -> K(*) l+l- form-factor treatment.
