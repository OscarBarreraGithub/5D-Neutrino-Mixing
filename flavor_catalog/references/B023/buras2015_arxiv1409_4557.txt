Title: B -> K(*) nu nubar decays in the Standard Model and beyond
Authors: Andrzej J. Buras, Jennifer Girrbach-Noe, Christoph Niehoff,
David M. Straub
Year: 2015
Local snapshot purpose: post-2008 theory reference for SM normalization,
right-handed-current sensitivity, and model-independent b -> s nu nubar EFT.

Source-extracted arXiv abstract values:
The paper combines lattice-QCD and light-cone-sum-rule form factors with
complete two-loop electroweak corrections to obtain
BR(B+ -> K+ nu nubar) = (4.0 +/- 0.5) x 10^{-6} and
BR(B0 -> K*0 nu nubar) = (9.2 +/- 1.0) x 10^{-6}.

Source-extracted model-dependence note:
The analysis relates new-physics effects in b -> s nu nubar to b -> s l+l-
and studies correlations with B_s -> mu+ mu- and b -> s l+l- transitions,
including models with partial compositeness.
