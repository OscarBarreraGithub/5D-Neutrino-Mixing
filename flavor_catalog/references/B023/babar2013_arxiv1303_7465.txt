Title: Search for B -> K(*) nu nubar and invisible quarkonium decays
Authors: BaBar Collaboration, J. P. Lees et al.
Year: 2013
Local snapshot purpose: primary BaBar hadronic-tag B -> K(*) nu nubar source
listed in the HFLAV/PDG B023 tables.

Source-extracted abstract/result text:
The search used 471 x 10^6 BB pairs collected by BaBar. No significant excess
was observed. The 90% confidence-level limits included
B(B+ -> K*+ nu nubar) < 11.6 x 10^{-5},
B(B0 -> K*0 nu nubar) < 9.3 x 10^{-5}, and the combined
B(B -> K* nu nubar) < 7.9 x 10^{-5}.
