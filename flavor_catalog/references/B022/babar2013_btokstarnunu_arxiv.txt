    <title>Search for B --&gt; K(*) nu nubar and invisible quarkonium decays</title>
    <updated>2013-06-11T18:41:41Z</updated>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1303.7465v2" rel="alternate" type="text/html"/>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/1303.7465v2" rel="related" type="application/pdf" title="pdf"/>
    <summary>We search for the flavor-changing neutral-current decays B-&gt;K(*)nu nubar, and the invisible decays J/psi-&gt;nu nubar and psi(2S)-&gt;nu nubar via B-&gt;K(*)J/psi and B-&gt;K(*)psi(2S) respectively, using a data sample of 471 x10^6 BB pairs collected by the BaBar experiment. We fully reconstruct the hadronic decay of one of the B mesons in the Upsilon(4S)-&gt;BB decay, and search for the B-&gt;K(*)nu nubar decay in the rest of the event. We observe no significant excess of signal decays over background and report branching fraction upper limits of BR(B+-&gt;K+nu nubar)&lt;3.7 x10^-5, BR(B0-&gt;K0nu nubar)&lt; 8.1 x10^-5, BR(B+-&gt;K*+nu nubar)&lt;11.6 x10^-5, BR(B0-&gt;K*0nu nubar)&lt;9.3 x10^-5, and combined upper limits of BR(B-&gt;Knu nubar)&lt;3.2 x10^-5 and BR(B-&gt;K*nu nubar)&lt;7.9 x10^-5, all at the 90% confidence level. For the invisible quarkonium decays, we report branching fraction upper limits of BR(J/psi-&gt;nu nubar)&lt;3.9 x10^-3 and BR(psi(2S)-&gt;nu nubar)&lt;15.5 x10^-3 at the 90% confidence level. Using the improved kinematic resolution achieved from hadronic reconstruction, we also provide partial branching fraction limits for the B-&gt;K(*)nu nubar decays over the full kinematic spectrum.</summary>
