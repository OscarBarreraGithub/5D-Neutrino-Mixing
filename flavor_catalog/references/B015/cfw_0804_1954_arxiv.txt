Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler

Relevant arXiv abstract/metadata snapshot:

The paper re-examines flavor constraints in standard Randall-Sundrum-type
scenarios and composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs models. The abstract states
that generic KK gluon bounds are about 21 TeV in standard RS-type scenarios
and about 33 TeV in the pseudo-Goldstone scenario, with sensitivity to the
gluon boundary kinetic term. It concludes that fully anarchic warped flavor
is implausible without additional partial flavor symmetries.
