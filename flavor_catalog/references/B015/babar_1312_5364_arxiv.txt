Title: Measurement of the B->Xsl+l- branching fraction and search for direct CP violation from a sum of exclusive final states

Relevant arXiv abstract/metadata snapshot:

Data sample: 471 x 10^6 Upsilon(4S) -> B Bbar events recorded with BABAR.
Reconstruction: sum over 10 X_s final states with one charged or neutral kaon,
two or fewer charged pions, and at most one pi0.
Lepton-flavor-averaged inclusive branching fraction:
  BF(B -> X_s l+ l-) =
  (6.73 +0.70 -0.64 [stat] +0.34 -0.25 [exp syst] +/- 0.50 [model syst])
  x 10^-6 for m(l+l-)^2 > 0.1 GeV^2/c^4.
Direct CP asymmetry over the full dilepton mass range:
  A_CP = 0.04 +/- 0.11 +/- 0.01.

Note: HFLAV combines the systematic uncertainties in quadrature and lists
the BABAR input as 6.73 +0.70 -0.64 +0.60 -0.56 in units of 10^-6.
