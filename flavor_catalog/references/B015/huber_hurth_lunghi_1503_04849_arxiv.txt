Title: Inclusive B -> X_s l^+ l^-: Complete angular analysis and a thorough study of collinear photons
Authors: Tobias Huber, Tobias Hurth, Enrico Lunghi
Journal reference: JHEP 06 (2015) 176

Relevant arXiv abstract/source snapshot:

The paper includes NNLO QCD, NLO QED and power corrections for inclusive
B -> X_s l+ l- angular observables, studies collinear-photon effects, and
examines Belle II sensitivity.

Perturbative dilepton windows:
  low q^2: 1 GeV^2 < q^2 = m_ll^2 < 6 GeV^2
  high q^2: q^2 > 14.4 GeV^2, or q^2 > 14.2 GeV^2 in some conventions
  expected theory precision in these windows: order 10 percent

Experimental weighted averages quoted in the paper:
  B(Bbar -> X_s l+ l-)_low^exp = (1.58 +/- 0.37) x 10^-6
  B(Bbar -> X_s l+ l-)_high^exp = (0.48 +/- 0.10) x 10^-6

SM branching-ratio predictions quoted in the paper:
  B[1,6]_ee = (1.67 +/- 0.10) x 10^-6
  B[1,6]_mumu = (1.62 +/- 0.09) x 10^-6
  B[>14.4]_ee = (2.20 +/- 0.70) x 10^-7
  B[>14.4]_mumu = (2.53 +/- 0.70) x 10^-7

Belle II assumptions/prospects:
  Expected final integrated luminosity: 50 ab^-1.
  The paper rescales from BABAR's 0.4242 ab^-1 and studies 95 percent CL
  constraints in the R9-R10 plane from future Belle II measurements.
