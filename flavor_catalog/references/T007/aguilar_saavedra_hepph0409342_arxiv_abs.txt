Title: Top flavour-changing neutral interactions: theoretical expectations and
experimental detection.
Author: J. A. Aguilar-Saavedra.
Submitted: 2004-09-29; last revised: 2005-07-06.
Journal reference: Acta Phys. Polon. B35, 2695-2710 (2004).

Relevant abstract excerpt:
Top flavour-changing neutral interactions with a light quark q = u,c and a
gauge or Higgs boson are very suppressed within the Standard Model, but can
reach observable levels in many extensions. The paper reviews effective
vertices Ztq, gamma tq, gtq, and Htq and discusses their LHC and linear-collider
signatures.
