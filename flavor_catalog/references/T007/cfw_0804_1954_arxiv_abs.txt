Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs.
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler.
Submitted: 2008-04-14; last revised: 2008-04-24.
Journal reference: JHEP 0809:008 (2008).
Related DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008.

Relevant abstract excerpt:
The paper studies flavor in 5D warped models with a composite pseudo-Goldstone
Higgs, re-examines RS flavor constraints, and argues that RS-GIM suppression
still leaves important flavor-violation amplitudes. It identifies the top
sector and composite-Higgs structure as central pieces of the model context
for later top-Higgs FCNC searches.
