Relevant PDG note for AAD 2024AU:
AAD 2024AU is based on 140 fb^-1 at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV of pp data. The
processes considered include both associated production of a single top quark
with a Higgs boson and the decay t -> H c in ttbar production using five Higgs
decay modes.

Reference table entries:
AAD 2024AU: Eur. Phys. J. C 84, 757. "Search for flavour-changing
neutral-current couplings between the top quark and the Higgs boson in
multi-lepton final states in 13 TeV pp collisions with the ATLAS detector."

HAYRAPETYAN 2025AD: Phys. Rev. D 112, 032008. "Search for flavor-changing
neutral current interactions of the top quark mediated by a Higgs boson in
