The PDG review defines x = Delta m / Gamma and y = Delta Gamma / (2 Gamma).
It describes the weak phase phi as Arg(q/p) and summarizes HFLAV global-fit
results for the CP-violation parameters in neutral-D mixing.

Selected PDG Table 70.7 HFLAV global-fit values:

All-CPV fit:
|q/p|      0.983 +/- 0.015       95% C.L. interval [0.96, 1.01]
phi        -1.51 +/- 1.04 deg    95% C.L. interval [-3.63, 0.51]
x (%)      0.405 +/- 0.043       95% C.L. interval [0.320, 0.489]
y (%)      0.636 +/- 0.024       95% C.L. interval [0.590, 0.682]

The review notes that D0-D0bar mixing is presumably dominated by long-distance
amplitudes that are difficult to calculate, limiting new-physics
interpretations.
