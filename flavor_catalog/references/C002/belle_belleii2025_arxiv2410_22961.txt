Title: Model-independent measurement of D0-D0bar mixing parameters in
       D0 -> K_S^0 pi+ pi- decays at Belle and Belle II
Authors: Belle and Belle II Collaborations
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. D 111, 112011 (2025)

Selected arXiv abstract metadata:
- The analysis uses e+e- collision samples with integrated luminosities of
  951 fb^-1 from Belle and 408 fb^-1 from Belle II.
- Approximately 2.05 x 10^6 neutral D mesons are reconstructed in the
  D0 -> K_S^0 pi+ pi- channel.
- Assuming charge-parity symmetry, the measured mixing parameters are
  x = (4.0 +/- 1.7 +/- 0.4) x 10^-3 and
  y = (2.9 +/- 1.4 +/- 0.3) x 10^-3.

Catalog use:
This is a post-2008 experimental update included as context for the current
HFLAV CKM25 charm-mixing landscape. It is not the C002 headline CPV fit value,
because the abstracted result assumes charge-parity symmetry.
