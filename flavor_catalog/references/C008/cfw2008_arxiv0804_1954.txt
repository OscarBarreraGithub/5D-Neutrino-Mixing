Catalog use:

  post-2008 comparison. It predates the BaBar 2011 and LHCb 2021
  semileptonic rare/forbidden charm searches used for C008. The C008

  prefactors as PDG/equivalent measured observables.

Minimal metadata note:
  The paper studies flavor constraints in composite/warped Higgs models
  with anarchic flavor and is used here only as historical context for
  what was available before the modern rare-charm experimental program.
