Title: Searches for Rare or Forbidden Semileptonic Charm Decays
Authors: BaBar Collaboration

Abstract-page metadata extract:

The analysis searches for rare or forbidden charm decays of the form
X_c+ -> h^+- l^-+ l^(')+, where X_c+ is a charm hadron, h is a pion,
kaon, or proton, and the leptons are electrons or muons. It uses
384 fb^-1 of e+e- annihilation data collected at or close to the
Upsilon(4S) resonance with the BaBar detector.

Source extract from results.tex:

Upper limits on branching ratios are calculated at 90% confidence level.
For comparison with previous measurements, the upper limits on branching
fractions at 90% CL are given in the results table. For 32 of the 35
decay modes the analysis is more sensitive than existing measurements;
the LFV decays are improved by at least a factor of 10.
