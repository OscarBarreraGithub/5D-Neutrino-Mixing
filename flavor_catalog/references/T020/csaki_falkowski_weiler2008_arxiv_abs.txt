Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
arXiv: 0804.1954v2
Submitted: 2008-04-14; last revised: 2008-04-24
Journal reference: JHEP 0809:008 (2008)
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008

Relevant abstract/context extract:

The paper studies flavor constraints in 5D warped models with a composite
pseudo-Goldstone Higgs. Its abstract reports that in standard RS-type
scenarios the KK-gluon mass should generically be heavier than about 21 TeV,
and that in the pseudo-Goldstone setup the corresponding scale can be about
33 TeV. These are paper-era theory context values, not measured h -> e mu
observables.

The paper predates the direct LHC Higgs LFV program. T020 therefore treats
the ATLAS and CMS Run-2 searches as post-2008 experimental developments.
