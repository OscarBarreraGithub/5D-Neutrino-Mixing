Title:
Search for the Higgs boson decays H -> ee and H -> e mu in pp collisions at
sqrt(s) = 13 TeV with the ATLAS detector

Authors: ATLAS Collaboration
arXiv: 1909.10235v2
Submitted: 2019-09-23; last revised: 2020-03-30
Journal reference: Phys. Lett. B 801 (2020) 135148
DOI: 10.1016/j.physletb.2019.135148

Relevant arXiv abstract extract:

ATLAS used 139 fb^-1 of pp collision data collected at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV. For a
Higgs boson mass of 125 GeV, no significant signal was observed.

The observed (expected) 95% CL upper limit on the branching fraction is:
  B(H -> e mu) < 6.1 x 10^-5 (5.8 x 10^-5).

The same abstract reports the H -> ee limit and states that the H -> e mu
result improved the previous best limit by about a factor of six.
