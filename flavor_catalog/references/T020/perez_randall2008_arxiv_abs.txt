Title: Natural Neutrino Masses and Mixings from Warped Geometry
Authors: Gilad Perez, Lisa Randall
arXiv: 0805.4652v3
Submitted: 2008-05-30; last revised: 2009-01-28
Journal reference: JHEP 0901:077 (2009)
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2009/01/077

Relevant abstract/context extract:

The paper demonstrates how flavor symmetries in warped geometry can provide a
natural explanation for large neutrino mixing angles and the distinction
between quark and lepton flavor sectors. The repo's current lepton-flavor
backend uses the paper-era mu -> e gamma dipole estimate, making it relevant
context for lepton-sector extensions.

This source does not provide a measured h -> e mu value. It is included only
as paper-era warped-lepton context.
