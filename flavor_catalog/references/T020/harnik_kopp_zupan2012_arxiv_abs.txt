Title: Flavor Violating Higgs Decays

Submitted: 2012-09-06; last revised: 2015-08-14
Journal reference: JHEP 03 (2013) 026
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP03(2013)026

Relevant abstract/context extract:

The paper studies flavor-violating couplings of the newly discovered 125 GeV
Higgs-like resonance to leptons and quarks. It notes that such interactions
can arise in electroweak-scale new physics frameworks including two-Higgs-
doublet models, extra dimensions, and compositeness.

For lepton flavor violation, the abstract states that h -> tau mu and
h -> tau e branching ratios of order 10% can be allowed by low-energy
constraints, and that direct LHC searches can improve over rare tau decay
bounds. This is theory motivation only for T020; the measured h -> e mu
observable is taken from PDG/ATLAS/CMS.
