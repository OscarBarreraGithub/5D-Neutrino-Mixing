Title: Collider aspects of flavour physics at high Q
Authors: T. Lari, L. Pape, W. Porod, J.A. Aguilar-Saavedra, F. del Aguila, B.C. Allanach, J. Alwall, Yu. Andreev, D. Aristizabal Sierra, et al.

Primary category: hep-ph
Journal reference: Eur. Phys. J. C 57 (2008) 183-308
DOI: 10.1140/epjc/s10052-008-0713-4
Comments: Report of Working Group 1 of the CERN Workshop "Flavour in the era of the LHC", Geneva, Switzerland, November 2005 -- March 2007.

Abstract:
This review presents flavour related issues in the production and decays of heavy
states at LHC, both from the experimental side and from the theoretical side. It
reviews top quark physics and discusses flavour aspects of several extensions of
the Standard Model, such as supersymmetry, little Higgs models, and models with
extra dimensions. This includes discovery aspects as well as measurement of
several properties of these heavy states. The review also presents publicly
available computational tools related to this topic.
