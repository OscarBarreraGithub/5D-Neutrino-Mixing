Title: A handbook of vector-like quarks: mixing and single production
Authors: J.A. Aguilar-Saavedra, R. Benbrik, S. Heinemeyer, M. Perez-Victoria
Published: 2013-06-03
Updated: 2013-12-07
Primary category: hep-ph
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. D 88, 094010 (2013)
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.88.094010
Comments: LaTeX 36 pages; version in Phys. Rev. D.

Abstract:
The paper obtains constraints on the mixing of vector-like quarks coupling
predominantly to the third generation. It considers all seven relevant types of
vector-like quarks individually. The constraints are derived from oblique
corrections and Z -> b bbar measurements at LEP and SLC. The paper investigates
implications for LHC phenomenology, including heavy-quark decays and single
production, and explores indirect effects of heavy-quark mixing in top and
bottom couplings. It also studies the impact of new quarks on single Higgs
production at the LHC and Higgs decay.
