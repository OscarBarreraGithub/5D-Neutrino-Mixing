Title:
Measurement of CP violating asymmetries in B0 -> K+ K- K0_S decays with a
time-dependent Dalitz approach

Authors:
Y. Nakahama et al. (Belle Collaboration)

Publication:
Phys. Rev. D 82 (2010) 073011

Minimal source extract:
The abstract reports a time-dependent Dalitz measurement of CP-violating
asymmetries in B0(B0-bar) -> K+ K- K0_S using 657 x 10^6 B B-bar pairs
collected with the Belle detector at KEKB.  The fit obtains effective
CP-violating phases and direct CP parameters for B0 -> phi(1020) K0_S,
B0 -> f0(980) K0_S, and other B0 -> K+ K- K0_S components.  The abstract
states that the phi_eff_1 solutions for B0 -> phi(1020) K0_S are consistent
with values measured in B -> (c c-bar) K0 decays, and that no significant
direct CP violation is found.
