Title:
Study of CP violation in Dalitz-plot analyses of B0 -> K+K-KS, B+ -> K+K-K+,
and B+ -> KSKSK+

Authors:
J. P. Lees et al. (BaBar Collaboration)

Publication:
Phys. Rev. D 85 (2012) 112010

Minimal source extract:
The abstract reports amplitude analyses of B0 -> K+K-K0_S, B+ -> K+K-K+,
and B+ -> K0_S K0_S K+ using approximately 470 x 10^6 B B-bar decays
collected with the BABAR detector.  For B0 -> K+K-K0_S, the extracted
CP-violating phase for phi(1020) K0_S is
beta_eff(phi(1020) K0_S) = (21 +/- 6 +/- 2) degrees.

Use in B033:
This is the BaBar post-2008 input included in the HFLAV Summer 2025
phi K0 average.  HFLAV converts/combines it as
sin(2 beta_eff) = 0.66 +/- 0.17 +/- 0.07 and
C_CP = 0.05 +/- 0.18 +/- 0.05.
