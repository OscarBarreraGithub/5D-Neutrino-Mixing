Minimal source extract:
The arXiv abstract says the paper re-examines flavor constraints in
Randall-Sundrum-type warped models, finds generic KK-gluon mass bounds around
21 TeV in standard RS scenarios, compares composite-Higgs flavor structure
with RS, and argues that anarchic warped flavor is strongly constrained unless
additional flavor symmetries remove part of the flavor-violation sources.
