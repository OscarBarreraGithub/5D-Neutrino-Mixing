Snapshot purpose: Minimal theory-systematics reference for B002.

Extracted from the arXiv abstract:
  The precision of CP phases 2 beta and 2 beta_s measured from mixing-induced
  CP asymmetries in B_d -> J/psi K_S and B_s -> J/psi phi is limited by an
  unknown long-distance penguin contribution involving up quarks.

Numerical bound quoted in the abstract:
  |Delta phi_d| <= 0.68 degrees

The paper also reports polarization-dependent bounds for |Delta phi_s| in
B_s -> J/psi phi and bounds for related B_d -> psi(2S) K_S and B_d -> J/psi K*
channels.
