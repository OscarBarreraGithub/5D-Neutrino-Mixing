Snapshot purpose: Minimal post-2008 primary experimental input for B002.

Extracted from the arXiv abstract:
  Data sample: 6 fb^-1 at sqrt(s)=13 TeV with the LHCb detector.
  Final states: J/psi(mu+mu-) K0_S, psi(2S)(mu+mu-) K0_S,
  and J/psi(e+e-) K0_S, with K0_S -> pi+ pi-.

Reported CP-violation parameters:
  S_{psi K0_S} = 0.717 +/- 0.013(stat) +/- 0.008(syst)
  C_{psi K0_S} = 0.008 +/- 0.012(stat) +/- 0.003(syst)

The abstract states that this S_{psi K0_S} result was the most precise
single measurement of the CKM angle beta to date and more precise than the
then-current world average.
