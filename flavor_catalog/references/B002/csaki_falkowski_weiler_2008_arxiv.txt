Extracted from the arXiv abstract:
  The paper re-examines flavor constraints on warped models and finds that the
  KK gluon mass in standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios should generically
  be heavier than about 21 TeV.

It further reports that in the composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs scenario,
new flavor-violation contributions and gauge-size Yukawa couplings imply a
KK gluon mass of at least about 33 TeV, with possible variation depending on
boundary kinetic assumptions.
