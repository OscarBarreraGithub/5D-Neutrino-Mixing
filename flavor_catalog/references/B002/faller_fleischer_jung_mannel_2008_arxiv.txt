Extracted from the arXiv abstract:
  The B0 -> J/psi K_{S,L} channels are described as outstanding probes of CP
  violation. The paper studies Standard-Model uncertainties from doubly
  Cabibbo-suppressed penguin contributions and proposes using CP asymmetries
  and the B0 -> J/psi pi0 branching ratio to control them.

The abstract also notes that this strategy can constrain a possible new-physics
phase in B0-B0bar mixing and is important for exploiting LHC and future
super-flavor-factory precision.
