Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Published: 2008-04-14
Primary category: hep-ph
Journal reference: JHEP 09 (2008) 008
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008
Authors: Csaba Csaki; Adam Falkowski; Andreas Weiler

Abstract extract:
The paper studies the flavor structure of five-dimensional warped models that
provide a dual description of a composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs. It
re-examines flavor constraints on the mass scale of new physics in standard
Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios, compares the flavor structure of composite
Higgs models to those in the RS model, and discusses new contributions to
flavor violation that remain suppressed by the RS-GIM mechanism. The quoted
constraints are quark-flavor constraints; this source is used here only as
paper-era context for why a quark-only RS flavor pipeline does not by itself
generate the lepton-flavor-violating K_L -> e mu final state.
