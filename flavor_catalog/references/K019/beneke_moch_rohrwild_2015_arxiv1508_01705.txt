Title: Lepton flavour violation in RS models with a brane- or nearly brane-localized Higgs
Updated: 2016-04-07T08:15:51Z
Published: 2015-08-07T14:24:46Z
Primary category: hep-ph
DOI: 10.1016/j.nuclphysb.2016.02.037
Authors: M. Beneke; P. Moch; J. Rohrwild

Abstract:
The paper performs a comprehensive study of charged lepton flavour violation in
Randall-Sundrum models in a fully 5D quantum-field-theoretical framework. It
considers minimal and custodially protected RS models and several
implementations of an IR-brane localized Higgs, including non-decoupling
effects of narrow-bulk-Higgs KK excitations. It provides a complete result for
the flavour-violating electromagnetic dipole operator in RS models and scans
branching fractions for mu -> e gamma, mu -> 3e, mu N -> e N, tau LFV decays,
and the electron EDM. The combination of mu -> e gamma and mu N -> e N is
identified as providing the most stringent constraint on the model parameter
space in that study.
