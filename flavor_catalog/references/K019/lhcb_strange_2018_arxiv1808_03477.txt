Title: Prospects for Measurements with Strange Hadrons at LHCb
Updated: 2019-07-31T14:32:54Z

Primary categories: hep-ex; hep-ph
Journal reference: JHEP 05 (2019) 048

Authors: A. A. Alves Junior et al.

Abstract:
This report details the capabilities of LHCb and its upgrades toward the study
of kaons and hyperons. It reviews analyses performed so far, elaborates on
prospects for selected key decay channels, and proposes new measurements at
LHCb to expand the strangeness research program.

K019 relevance note:
This is not a new K_L -> e mu measurement. It is included as post-2008
experimental/prospective context showing renewed interest in strange-hadron
rare decays at LHCb and related LFV strange-hadron modes.
