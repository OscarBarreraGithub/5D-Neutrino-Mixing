Title: Lepton Flavour Violation in the Presence of a Fourth Generation of Quarks and Leptons
Updated: 2010-08-16T07:15:45Z
Published: 2010-06-28T14:07:05Z
Primary category: hep-ph
Journal reference: JHEP 1009:104, 2010
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP09(2010)104
Authors: Andrzej J. Buras; Bjorn Duling; Thorsten Feldmann; Tillmann Heidsieck; Christoph Promberger

Abstract:
The paper calculates charged-LFV rates in the SM3 extended by a fourth
generation of quarks and leptons. It includes l_i -> l_j gamma, tau
semileptonic modes, mu-e conversion, and also branching ratios for
K_L -> mu e, K_L -> pi0 mu e, B_{d,s} -> mu e, B_{d,s} -> tau e, and
B_{d,s} -> tau mu. The authors emphasize that the pattern of LFV branching
ratios can distinguish this scenario from the MSSM and the Littlest Higgs
model with T parity.
