Title: Neutrino Trident Production from NuTeV
Authors: T. Adams et al. (NuTeV Collaboration)
Venue note: Talk at ICHEP 98, Vancouver; 4 pages

Minimal extracted facts from arXiv source tridproc.tex:
- The analysis studies zero-hadronic-energy dimuon production,
  nu_mu (anti-nu_mu) + N -> nu_mu (anti-nu_mu) + mu+ + mu- + N.
- Combined table entry: Data = 17 events; background estimate =
  9.8 +/- 2.9 events; Standard Model = 10.8 +/- 0.3 events; V-A =
  18.3 +/- 0.6 events.
- The arXiv source text says the background estimate does not account for all
  signal events and the excess is attributed to neutrino trident production.
- The same source text calls the result preliminary and says the excess is
  consistent with the Standard Model.
- The DUNE 2019 trident paper compiles this as
  sigma_NuTeV/sigma_SM = 0.72^{+1.73}_{-0.72}, with the caution that no signal
  could be established at NuTeV.
