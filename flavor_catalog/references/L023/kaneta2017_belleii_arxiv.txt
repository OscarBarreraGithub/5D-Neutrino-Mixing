Title: On the possibility of search for L_mu - L_tau gauge boson at Belle-II
  and neutrino beam experiments
Authors: Yuya Kaneta and Takashi Shimomura
Publication: Prog. Theor. Exp. Phys. 2017, 053B04

Minimal extracted facts from arXiv source:
- The paper studies one-photon plus missing-energy events at Belle-II and
  neutrino trident production at neutrino beam experiments in the gauged
  L_mu - L_tau model.
- It quotes the CCFR trident ratio R_CCFR = sigma_CCFR/sigma_SM =
  0.82 +/- 0.28 and states that CCFR gives the most stringent bound among the
  reported CCFR and CHARM-II searches.
- It states that Belle-II is an e+e- collider at center-of-mass energy
  sqrt(s) = 10.58 GeV with a goal of 50 ab^-1 integrated luminosity.
- It emphasizes complementarity: Belle-II photon plus missing-energy searches
  probe kinetic-mixing-driven production, while neutrino-beam trident
  production probes muon-neutrino/muon contact or gauge interactions and can
  retain sensitivity where small kinetic mixing weakens Belle-II.
