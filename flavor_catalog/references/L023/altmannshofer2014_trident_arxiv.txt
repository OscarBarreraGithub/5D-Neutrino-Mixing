Minimal extracted facts from arXiv source trident_constraints.tex:
- CHARM-II used a beam with mean energy about 20 GeV and a glass target.
- CCFR used a beam with mean energy about 160 GeV and an iron target.
- The paper quotes sigma_CHARM-II/sigma_SM = 1.58 +/- 0.57.
- The paper quotes sigma_CCFR/sigma_SM = 0.82 +/- 0.28.
- The paper notes that NuTeV results can also be used, but with caution due to
  differences in background treatment between the initial report and later
  publication.
- The captioned constraint plot states that CCFR excludes parameter space at
  95% confidence level for the L_mu - L_tau style Z' interpretation.
- The paper finds that a gauged-muon-force explanation of the muon g-2 anomaly
  is disfavored for m_Z' greater than about 400 MeV.
