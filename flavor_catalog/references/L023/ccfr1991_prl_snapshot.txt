Title: Neutrino tridents and W-Z interference
Authors: S. R. Mishra et al. (CCFR Collaboration)
Publication: Phys. Rev. Lett. 66, 3117-3120 (1991)

Minimal extracted facts:
- CCFR measured muon pairs induced by neutrino scattering in the Coulomb field
  of a target nucleus.
- The public APS abstract reports 37.0 +/- 12.4 observed tridents after
  geometric and kinematic corrections.
- The same abstract gives the Standard Model prediction as 45.3 +/- 2.3
  events.
- Ratio inferred from those two numbers is 37.0/45.3 = 0.817; propagating the
  two quoted uncertainties gives about 0.28. This matches the commonly used
  ratio sigma_CCFR/sigma_SM = 0.82 +/- 0.28.
- The abstract states that the measurement demonstrates destructive W-Z
  interference and rules out the V-A prediction without the interference at
  99% confidence level.
