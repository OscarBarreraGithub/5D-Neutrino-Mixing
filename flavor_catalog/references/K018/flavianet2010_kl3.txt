Relevant extraction for K018:

Section 2.2 gives the Standard Model photon-inclusive K_l3(gamma)
rate expression. It uses the combination |Vus| f_+^{K0 pi-}(0),
phase-space integrals I_Kl, the short-distance electroweak correction
S_EW, long-distance electromagnetic corrections, and SU(2)-breaking
corrections.

Section 4.1, "Determination of |Vus| f_+(0)", states that for the five
decay modes with rate measurements the FlaviaNet analysis evaluates
|Vus| f_+(0) from decay-rate data, phase-space integrals from
dispersive fits, long-distance radiative corrections, and SU(2)
breaking corrections. The principal experimental result is:

|Vus| f_+(0) = 0.2163(5)

It reports chi2/ndf = 0.77/4, P = 94%.

Table 14 values:

Mode             |Vus| f_+(0)
K_L -> pi e nu    0.2163(6)
K_L -> pi mu nu   0.2166(6)
K_S -> pi e nu    0.2155(13)
K+- -> pi e nu    0.2160(11)
K+- -> pi mu nu   0.2158(14)
Average           0.2163(5)

Section 4.3 compares Ke3 and Kmu3 values as a lepton-universality
test and gives:

r_mu e = 1.002(5)

Section 4.5 uses |Vus| f_+(0) = 0.2163(5) with f_+(0) = 0.959(5)
and K_l2 information for a then-current CKM unitarity test; it finds:

Delta_CKM = -0.0001(6)

Catalog note:
This is the post-2008 FlaviaNet consolidation that the 2025 PDG review
still labels as the K_l3 product convention. It is the historical bridge
between older PDG averages and the modern K018 input used by EW002.
