Relevant extraction for K018:

FLAG Section 5 covers leptonic and semileptonic kaon and pion decay,
|Vud|, and |Vus|. Section 5.1 states that precision experimental data
on kaon decays determine:

|Vus| f_+(0) = 0.21654(41)
|Vus / Vud| f_K+- / f_pi+- = 0.27599(41)

FLAG defines f_+(q^2) as a form factor relevant for the semileptonic
decay K0 -> pi- l nu and emphasizes the q^2 = 0 value:
f_+(0) = f_+^{K0 pi-}(0).

FLAG Section 5.3.1 states that the Nf = 2 + 1 + 1 average for f_+(0)
is based on FNAL/MILC 18 and ETM 16, while the Nf = 2 + 1 average is
based on FNAL/MILC 12I and RBC/UKQCD 15A:

direct, Nf = 2 + 1 + 1: f_+(0) = 0.9698(17)
direct, Nf = 2 + 1:     f_+(0) = 0.9677(27)

FLAG Section 5.3.3 converts these averages into CKM elements. For
Nf = 2 + 1 + 1, Eq. (72) maps into:

|Vus| = 0.22328(58)

FLAG Section 5.4 tests first-row CKM unitarity. Combining f_+(0) for
Nf = 2 + 1 + 1 with the PDG estimate of |Vud| gives:

|Vu|^2 = 0.99802(66)
