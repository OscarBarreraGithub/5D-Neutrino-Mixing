Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler

Relevant extraction for K018:

The arXiv abstract says the paper studies the flavor structure of 5D
warped models and re-examines flavor constraints on the new-physics
mass scale in standard Randall-Sundrum scenarios. It quotes generic
KK-gluon mass scales of about 21 TeV in standard RS-type scenarios and
about 33 TeV in the composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs scenario, with
stronger or weaker bounds possible depending on boundary kinetic terms.
The paper frames anarchic warped flavor constraints mainly through
flavor violation and RS-GIM suppression.
