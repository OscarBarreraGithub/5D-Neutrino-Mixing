Relevant extraction for K018:

PDG Section 67.2 gives the photon-inclusive K_l3 rate formula
for K -> pi l nu, l = e or mu. It states that the experimental
measurements are the semileptonic decay widths from branching
fractions and lifetimes, plus measured form factors for phase-space
integrals. Theory enters through short-distance electroweak
corrections, long-distance radiative corrections, isospin-breaking
corrections, and f_+(0).

The review says that following Moulson and the FlaviaNet group, with
recent QED-radiative-correction improvements and the isospin-violating
effect included, the K_l3 products are:

Table 67.1: |Vus| f_+(0) from K_l3, based on Refs. [25,56]

Decay mode        |Vus| f_+(0)
K+- e3            0.2169(6)
K+- mu3           0.2168(10)
K_L e3            0.2162(5)
K_L mu3           0.2165(6)
K_S e3            0.2169(8)
K_S mu3           0.2125(47)
Average           0.21656(35)

Equation (67.11): f_+(0) |Vus| = 0.21656(35), labeled FLAVIANET.

The same passage states that the result leads to
|Vus| = 0.22330(35)_exp (39)_LAT (8)_RC+IB (53)_TOT.

FLAG lattice inputs quoted by PDG in Eq. (67.12):
f_+(0) = 0.9677(27), Nf = 2 + 1
f_+(0) = 0.9698(17), Nf = 2 + 1 + 1

PDG Eq. (67.13) updates the K_l3 extraction to:

|Vus| = 0.22330(4)_exp+RC (4)_lattice for Nf = 2 + 1 + 1

Catalog note:
