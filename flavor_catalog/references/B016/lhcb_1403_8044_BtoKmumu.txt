Title: Differential branching fractions and isospin asymmetries of B -> K(*) mu+ mu- decays
Authors: LHCb Collaboration
Local snapshot purpose: LHCb primary source for B0 -> K0 mu+ mu- and B+ -> K+ mu+ mu- differential branching fractions and isospin asymmetries used in HFLAV context.

Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:
The isospin asymmetries of B -> K mu+ mu- and B -> K* mu+ mu- decays and the partial branching fractions of B0 -> K0 mu+ mu-, B+ -> K+ mu+ mu-, and B+ -> K*+ mu+ mu- are measured as functions of q^2. The data correspond to 3 fb^-1 collected by LHCb at 7 TeV and 8 TeV in 2011 and 2012. The isospin asymmetries are consistent with Standard Model expectations, while the measured branching fractions favor lower values than their Standard Model predictions.
