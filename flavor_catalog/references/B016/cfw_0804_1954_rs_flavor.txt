Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
Local snapshot purpose: paper-era RS/anarchic-flavor baseline for post-2008 comparison.

Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:
The paper studies flavor constraints in five-dimensional warped models and composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs models. It re-examines flavor constraints on the mass scale of new physics in Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios, emphasizes RS-GIM suppression and additional flavor-violating amplitudes in composite-Higgs variants, and argues that fully anarchic warped flavor is strongly constrained.
