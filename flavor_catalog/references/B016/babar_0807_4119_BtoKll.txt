Title: Direct CP, Lepton Flavor and Isospin Asymmetries in the Decays B -> K(*) l+ l-
Authors: BABAR Collaboration
Local snapshot purpose: older BaBar input appearing in HFLAV B -> K l+l- tables.

Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:
BaBar measured rate asymmetries for B -> K(*) l+ l- with l+ l- equal to e+ e- or mu+ mu- using 384 million B Bbar events collected at PEP-II. The paper reports no evidence for direct CP or lepton-flavor asymmetries and reports unexpectedly large low-dilepton-mass isospin asymmetries in B -> K l+ l- and B -> K* l+ l- relative to Standard Model expectations.
