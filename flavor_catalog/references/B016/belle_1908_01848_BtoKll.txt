Title: Test of lepton flavor universality and search for lepton flavor violation in B -> K l l decays
Authors: Belle Collaboration
Local snapshot purpose: Belle primary source for B -> K e+e-, B -> K mu+mu-, R_K, and HFLAV B -> K l+l- average inputs.

Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:
The paper presents measurements of branching fractions for B -> K mu+ mu- and B -> K e+ e-, and their ratio R_K, using 711 fb^-1 with 772 x 10^6 B Bbar events collected at the Upsilon(4S) resonance by Belle. It reports R_K in several q^2 bins and states that all R_K results are consistent with Standard Model predictions. The measured differential branching fractions are consistent with theoretical predictions for charged B decays, while neutral-B values are below expectations.
