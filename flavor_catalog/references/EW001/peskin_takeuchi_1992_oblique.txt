Definition context:
This is the standard reference for the S, T, and U oblique electroweak
parameters used to describe new-physics contributions to electroweak gauge-boson
self-energies. The PDG and Gfitter snapshots for EW001 quote their S/T/U fits in
this Peskin-Takeuchi convention, up to each source's stated Standard Model
reference point.
