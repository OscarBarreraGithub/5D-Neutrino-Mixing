Relevant extracted context:
The page states that HEPfit implements the standard set of electroweak precision
observables, including Z-pole decay widths, left-right and forward-backward
asymmetries, the effective weak mixing angle, the W mass and width, top and
Higgs masses, alpha_s, and Delta alpha_had^(5).

The page lists implemented models including:
1. The Standard Model of electroweak interactions.
2. The Standard Model plus new physics as contributions to oblique parameters
   S, T, U.
3. The Standard Model plus epsilon_{1,2,3,b} parameters.
4. New physics modifying bottom-quark neutral-current couplings.
5. New physics modifying Higgs couplings to electroweak vector bosons.
6. The dimension-six Standard Model Effective Lagrangian.
7. Two-Higgs-doublet models.
