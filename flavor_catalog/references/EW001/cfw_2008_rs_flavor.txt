Relevant baseline context:
The paper is the CFW-era baseline requested by the catalog prompt. It
re-examines flavor constraints in Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios and finds
generic KK-gluon mass bounds of about 21 TeV, with stronger bounds around
33 TeV in the composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs case. It also notes that
electroweak precision observables are important and that the S parameter is
within experimental bounds if the KK scale is raised to roughly 2-3 TeV.
