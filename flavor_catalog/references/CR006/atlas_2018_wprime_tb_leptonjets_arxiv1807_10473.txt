Title: Search for vector-boson resonances decaying to a top quark and bottom quark in the lepton plus jets final state in pp collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV with the ATLAS detector
Authors: ATLAS Collaboration
Journal reference: Phys. Lett. B 788 (2019) 347
DOI: 10.1016/j.physletb.2018.11.032
Report number: CERN-EP-2018-142

Abstract/result extract:
A search for new charged massive gauge bosons W' is performed with the
ATLAS detector at the LHC. Data were collected in proton-proton collisions
at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV and correspond to 36.1 fb^{-1}. The analysis searches
for W' -> t bbar in final states with an electron or muon plus jets. The
search covers resonance masses between 0.5 and 5.0 TeV and considers
right-handed W' bosons. No significant deviation from the Standard Model
expectation is observed. Upper limits are set on W' -> t bbar cross
section times branching ratio and on effective couplings. For
right-handed W' bosons with coupling equal to the SM weak coupling,
masses below 3.15 TeV are excluded at 95% confidence level. Combined with
the ATLAS fully hadronic result, right-handed W' bosons with masses below
3.25 TeV are excluded at 95% confidence level.
