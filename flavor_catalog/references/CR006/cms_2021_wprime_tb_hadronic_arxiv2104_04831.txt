Title: Search for W' bosons decaying to a top and a bottom quark at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV in the hadronic final state
Authors: CMS Collaboration
Journal reference: Phys. Lett. B 820 (2021) 136535
DOI: 10.1016/j.physletb.2021.136535
Report number: CMS-B2G-20-005, CERN-EP-2021-049

Abstract/result extract:
A search is performed for W' bosons decaying to a top and a bottom quark
in the all-hadronic final state in proton-proton collisions at sqrt(s) =
13 TeV. The data were collected by CMS in 2016-2018 and correspond to
137 fb^{-1}. Deep neural network algorithms identify the bottom-quark jet
and the jet containing the hadronic top-quark decay products. No excess
above the estimated Standard Model background is observed. Upper limits
are set on production cross sections of W' bosons decaying to a top and a
bottom quark. Both left- and right-handed W' bosons with masses below
3.4 TeV are excluded at 95% confidence level, giving the most stringent
all-hadronic W' -> tb limits at the time.
