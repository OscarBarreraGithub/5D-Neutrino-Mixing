Title: Search for W' -> tb decays in the hadronic final state using pp collisions at sqrt(s)=13 TeV with the ATLAS detector
Authors: ATLAS Collaboration
Journal reference: Phys. Lett. B 781 (2018) 327
DOI: 10.1016/j.physletb.2018.03.036
Report number: CERN-EP-2017-340

Abstract/result extract:
A search for W'-boson production in the W' -> t bbar -> q q' b bbar decay
channel is presented using 36.1 fb^{-1} of 13 TeV proton-proton collision
data collected by ATLAS in 2015 and 2016. The search is interpreted for
both left-handed and right-handed chiral W' bosons in the mass range
1-5 TeV. Hadronically decaying top quarks are identified using jet
substructure tagging based on a shower-deconstruction algorithm. No
significant deviation from the Standard Model prediction is observed.
Results are expressed as upper limits on W' -> t bbar production cross
section times branching ratio versus W' mass. The limits exclude
right-handed W' bosons below 3.0 TeV and left-handed W' bosons below
2.9 TeV at the 95% confidence level.
