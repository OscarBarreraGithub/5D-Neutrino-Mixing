Title: Direct CP violation and the Delta I=1/2 rule in K->pipi decay from the Standard Model
Authors: Ryan Abbott et al. (RBC and UKQCD Collaborations)
Year: 2020
Local purpose: Post-2008 lattice-QCD Standard Model prediction.

Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:

  The calculation improves the 2015 A0 and epsilon' calculation with nearly
  four times the statistics and improved ground-state isolation.
  It reports Re(A0) = 2.99(0.32)(0.59) x 10^{-7} GeV and
  Im(A0) = -6.98(0.62)(1.44) x 10^{-11} GeV.
  Combining with the earlier A2 calculation gives
  Re(epsilon'/epsilon) = 21.7(2.6)(6.2)(5.0) x 10^{-4};
  the third error is omitted isospin breaking.
