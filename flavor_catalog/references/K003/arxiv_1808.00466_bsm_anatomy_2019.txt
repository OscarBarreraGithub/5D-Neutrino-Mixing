Title: Anatomy of epsilon'/epsilon beyond the Standard Model
Authors: Jason Aebischer, Christoph Bobeth, Andrzej J. Buras, David M. Straub
Year: 2019
Local purpose: Model-independent BSM interpretation and correlations.

Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:

  The paper studies epsilon'/epsilon in the Delta S = 1 effective theory
  and in SMEFT.  It derives a master formula applicable when the Wilson
  coefficients of contributing operators are known at the electroweak scale.
  It notes that K0 and D0 mixing and EDM constraints significantly limit
  possible SMEFT contributions, and discusses correlations with
  K -> pi nu nubar decays.
