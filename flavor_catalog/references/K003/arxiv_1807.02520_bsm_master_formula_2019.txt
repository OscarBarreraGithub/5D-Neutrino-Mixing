Title: Master formula for epsilon'/epsilon beyond the Standard Model
Authors: Jason Aebischer, Christoph Bobeth, Andrzej J. Buras, Jean-Marc Gerard, David M. Straub
Year: 2019
Local purpose: BSM operator-basis relevance and chromomagnetic/penguin sensitivity.

Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:

  The paper gives a master formula for epsilon'/epsilon, valid in any BSM
  theory, using BSM hadronic matrix elements from Dual QCD and SM matrix
  elements from lattice QCD.  It emphasizes large scalar and tensor BSM
  operator effects.

Relevant arXiv source excerpt, letter.tex:

  The authors identify solutions through the dominant electroweak left-right
  penguin operator Q8 and the dominant QCD left-right penguin operator Q6.
  They also discuss matrix elements of scalar, tensor, and chromo-magnetic
  operators, and list electro- and chromo-magnetic dipole operators including
  O_8g = m_s (bar s sigma^{mu nu} T^a P_L d) G^a_{mu nu}.
