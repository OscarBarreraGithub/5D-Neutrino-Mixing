Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
Year: 2008
Local purpose: Reference point for the requested post-2008 comparison.

Relevant arXiv abstract excerpt:

  The paper re-examines flavor constraints in standard Randall-Sundrum-type
  scenarios and finds that generic KK-gluon masses should be heavier than
  about 21 TeV.  It then compares composite Higgs flavor structure to RS and
  argues that fully anarchic warped flavor is implausible without additional
  flavor symmetries.
