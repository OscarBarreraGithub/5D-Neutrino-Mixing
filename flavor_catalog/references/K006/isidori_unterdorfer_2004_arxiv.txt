arXiv:hep-ph/0311084
Title: On the short-distance constraints from K_{L,S} -> mu^+ mu^-
Authors: Gino Isidori, Rene Unterdorfer
Submitted: 2003-11-06
Journal reference: JHEP 0401 (2004) 009
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2004/01/009

Abstract excerpt:
Motivated by new precise results on several K_L decays, sensitive to the K_L -> gamma gamma
form factor, we present a new analysis of the K_L -> mu^+ mu^- long-distance amplitude
based on a semi-phenomenological approach. Particular attention is devoted to the evaluation
of the uncertainties of this method and to the comparison with alternative approaches. Our
main result is a conservative upper bound of 2.5 x 10^-9 on B(K_L -> mu^+ mu^-)_short,
which is compatible with the SM expectation and which provides significant constraints on
new-physics scenarios. The possibility to extract an independent short-distance information
from future searches on K_S -> mu^+ mu^- is also briefly discussed.
