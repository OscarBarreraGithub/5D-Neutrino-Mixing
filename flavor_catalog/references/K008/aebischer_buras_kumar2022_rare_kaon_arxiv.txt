Title: On the Importance of Rare Kaon Decays: A Snowmass 2021 White Paper
Authors: Jason Aebischer, Andrzej J. Buras, Jacky Kumar

Relevant abstract extraction:
The white paper stresses precise measurements of rare decays including
K+ -> pi+ nu nubar, KL -> pi0 nu nubar, K_{L,S} -> mu+ mu-, and
K_{L,S} -> pi0 l+ l- for new-physics searches. It emphasizes branching ratios
and q^2 distributions, correlations with epsilon'/epsilon, epsilon_K, and
Delta M_K, sensitivity to |V_cb| for most observables, and the role of SMEFT in
interpreting very short-distance scales beyond direct LHC reach.
