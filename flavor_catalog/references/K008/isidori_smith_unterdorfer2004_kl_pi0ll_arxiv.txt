Title: The rare decay KL -> pi0 mu+ mu- within the SM
Authors: Gino Isidori, Christopher Smith, Rene Unterdorfer

Relevant extraction:
The paper writes the neutral-kaon dilepton rate in terms of mixing, direct,
interference, and CP-conserving pieces:
B(KL -> pi0 l+ l-) =
[C_mix^l +/- C_int^l (Im lambda_t / 10^-4)
 + C_dir^l (Im lambda_t / 10^-4)^2 + C_CPC^l] x 10^-12.

For the electron mode, the coefficients quoted are:
C_mix^e = (15.7 +/- 0.3) |a_S|^2
C_int^e = (6.2 +/- 0.3) |a_S|
C_dir^e = 2.4 +/- 0.2
C_CPC^e approx 0

The same paragraph gives |a_S| = 1.2 +/- 0.2 and
(Im lambda_t / 10^-4)_SM = 1.36 +/- 0.12. The paper reports both signs of the
interference because the sign of a_S was not fixed model-independently, while
noting that theoretical arguments point toward constructive interference. For
the electron mode it states that the decay is completely dominated by the CPV
part; the CP-conserving two-photon piece is relevant mainly for the muon mode.
