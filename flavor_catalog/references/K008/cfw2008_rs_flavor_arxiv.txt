Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler

Relevant abstract extraction:
The paper studies the flavor structure of 5D warped models with a composite
pseudo-Goldstone Higgs. It re-examines flavor constraints in standard
Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios and finds that the KK gluon mass should
generically be heavier than about 21 TeV. In composite-Higgs variants, new
flavor-violation contributions remain RS-GIM suppressed but enhance flavor
amplitudes; kinetic mixing among SM fields tightens bounds, and the KK gluon
mass is quoted as at least about 33 TeV. The abstract concludes that fully
anarchic flavor in warped extra dimensions is implausible without partial
flavor symmetries that remove some flavor-violation sources.
