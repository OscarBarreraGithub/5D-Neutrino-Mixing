Abstract/context extract:
The paper studies flavor constraints in five-dimensional warped models with a
composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs. It re-examines flavor constraints on the mass
scale of new physics in standard Randall-Sundrum scenarios and finds that the
KK gluon mass should generically be heavier than about 21 TeV. In the
pseudo-Goldstone scenario the abstract quotes a bound of at least about 33 TeV.
The abstract also states that flavor bounds depend on assumptions such as the
gluon boundary kinetic term and that fully anarchic flavor in warped extra
dimensions is implausible without additional flavor structure.
