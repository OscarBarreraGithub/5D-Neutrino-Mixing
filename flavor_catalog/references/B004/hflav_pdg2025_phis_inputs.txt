Source title:
Heavy Flavor Averaging Group (HFLAV), PDG 2025.
Table 1: Direct experimental measurements of phi_s^ccs and, wherever applicable,
DeltaGamma_s using J/psi K+K-, psi(2S)K+K-, J/psi pi+pi-, and Ds+ Ds- decays.
The first error is statistical and the second systematic. The last two lines
give HFLAV averages, with DeltaGamma_s uncertainties scaled for inconsistencies
between Bs0 -> J/psi K+K- measurements.
