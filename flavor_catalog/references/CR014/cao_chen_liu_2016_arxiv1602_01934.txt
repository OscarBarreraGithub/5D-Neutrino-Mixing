    <title>Probing Higgs Width and Top Quark Yukawa Coupling from $t\bar{t}H$ and $t\bar{t}t\bar{t}$ Productions</title>
    <updated>2016-02-05T07:13:54Z</updated>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1602.01934v1" rel="alternate" type="text/html"/>
    <link href="https://arxiv.org/pdf/1602.01934v1" rel="related" type="application/pdf" title="pdf"/>
    <summary>We demonstrate that four top-quark production is a powerful tool to constrain the top Yukawa coupling. The constraint is robust in the sense that it does not rely on the Higgs boson decay. Taking into account the projection of the $t\bar{t}H$ production by the ATLAS collaobration, we obtain a bound on Higgs boson width, $Γ_H\leq 3.1~Γ_H^{\rm SM}$, at the 14 TeV LHC with an integrated luminosity of $300~{\rm fb}^{-1}$. Increasing the luminosity to $500~{\rm fb}^{-1}$ yields $Γ_H\leq 2.1~Γ_H^{\rm SM}$.</summary>
