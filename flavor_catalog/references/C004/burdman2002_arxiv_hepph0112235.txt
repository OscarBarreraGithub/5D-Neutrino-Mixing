Minimal theory snapshot:
  The abstract states that the work studies rare charm decays, including
  short-distance QCD corrections and long-range effects.
  For c -> u l+ l- transitions, it states that sensitivity to short-distance
  physics exists in kinematic regions away from vector-meson resonances that
  dominate the total rate.

TeX-source extraction for D0 -> mu+ mu-:
  The two-photon unitarity contribution is modeled through
  D0 -> gamma gamma -> l+ l-.
  The relation quoted in the source is
    Br(D0 -> mu+ mu-)^(gamma gamma) ~= 2.7 x 10^-5 Br(D0 -> gamma gamma).
  The source summary says the long-distance contribution favors a branching
  ratio somewhere in excess of 10^-13 and, using Br(D0 -> gamma gamma) >= 10^-8,
  anticipates at least 3 x 10^-13 for D0 -> mu+ mu-.
  The same paper quotes a VMD estimate
    Br(D0 -> gamma gamma)^(vmd) = (3.5 +4.0 -2.6) x 10^-8.
