arXiv:0804.1954 [hep-ph]
Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
Submitted: 14 Apr 2008; last revised: 24 Apr 2008, v2
Journal reference: JHEP 0809:008 (2008)
DOI: 10.1088/1126-6708/2008/09/008

Minimal baseline snapshot:
  The abstract says the paper re-examines flavor constraints in standard
  Randall-Sundrum scenarios and finds the KK gluon mass should generically be
  heavier than about 21 TeV.
  It also says pseudo-Goldstone composite-Higgs flavor effects imply a KK
  gluon mass of at least about 33 TeV.
  The abstract concludes that fully anarchic warped flavor is implausible
  without partial flavor symmetries that remove some sources of flavor violation.
