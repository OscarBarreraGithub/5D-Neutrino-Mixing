Title: Search for rare decays of D0 mesons into two muons
Authors: LHCb collaboration
Submitted: 21 Dec 2022
Last revised: 14 Nov 2023, v2
Report numbers: LHCb-PAPER-2022-029, CERN-EP-2022-273
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. Lett. 131 (2023) 041804
DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevLett.131.041804

Minimal result snapshot:
  Data: pp collisions at sqrt(s) = 7, 8, and 13 TeV.
  Integrated luminosity: 9 fb^-1.
  Search strategy: optimized for D0 mesons from D*+ -> D0 pi+ decays, also
  sensitive to D0 mesons from other sources.
  Result: no evidence for an excess above expected background.
  Limit: B(D0 -> mu+ mu-) < 3.1 x 10^-9 at 90% CL.
  The arXiv abstract describes this as the world's most stringent limit at
  the time of the LHCb publication.
