Title: Effective field theory analysis of rare |Delta c| = |Delta u| = 1 charm decays
Authors: Hector Gisbert, Gudrun Hiller, Dominik Suelmann
Published: 13 December 2024
Journal reference: JHEP 2024, article 102 (2024)
DOI: 10.1007/JHEP12(2024)102

Minimal context snapshot:
  The abstract describes a global analysis of |Delta c| = |Delta u| = 1
  transitions using recent data on D0 -> mu+ mu-, D+ -> pi+ mu+ mu-,
  Lambda_c -> p mu+ mu-, and D0 -> pi+ pi- mu+ mu-.
  It works out constraints on new-physics Wilson coefficients
  C7, C9, C10 and primed counterparts.
  The article states that results are consistent with the Standard Model but
  leave sizeable room for new physics that can be cleanly signaled with null
  test observables not probed in kaon and b-decay searches.
