CMS-BPH-23-008; CERN-EP-2025-108
Title: Search for the rare decay D0 -> mu+ mu- in proton-proton collisions at
sqrt(s) = 13.6 TeV
Collaboration: CMS Collaboration
Public date: 6 June 2025
Publication: Phys. Rev. Lett. 135 (2025) 151803
arXiv: 2506.06152
DOI: 10.1103/zc76-rgcp

Minimal result snapshot:
  Dataset: pp collisions at sqrt(s) = 13.6 TeV, 2022-2023.
  Integrated luminosity: 64.5 fb^-1.
  Production/selection: D0 mesons from D*+ -> D0 pi+ decays.
  Result: no significant excess is observed.
  Limit: B(D0 -> mu+ mu-) < 2.4 x 10^-9 at 95% confidence level.
  CMS summary states this was the most sensitive search to date and improved
  on the previous best result.
