Abstract extract:
  The experiment measured the neutron electric dipole moment at the Paul
  Scherrer Institute using Ramsey's method of separated oscillating magnetic
  fields with ultracold neutrons.
  The salient features were a Hg-199 co-magnetometer and optically pumped
  cesium vapor magnetometers, blinded statistical analysis by two groups, and
  improved magnetic-field systematic control.

Numerical result:
  d_n = (0.0 +/- 1.1_stat +/- 0.2_sys) x 10^-26 e cm.

This source is the primary experimental input for the PDG 2026 direct neutron
EDM bound |d_n| < 1.8 x 10^-26 e cm at 90% CL.
