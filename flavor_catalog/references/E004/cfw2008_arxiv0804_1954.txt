Abstract extract:
  The paper studies flavor structure of 5D warped models and composite
  pseudo-Goldstone Higgs models.
  It re-examines flavor constraints on the scale of new physics in
  Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios and finds that the KK gluon mass should
  generically be heavier than about 21 TeV.
  In the pseudo-Goldstone scenario, kinetic mixing and gauge-set Yukawa
  couplings imply a KK gluon mass of at least about 33 TeV.
  The abstract states that fully anarchic flavor in warped extra dimensions is
  implausible without partial flavor symmetries.
