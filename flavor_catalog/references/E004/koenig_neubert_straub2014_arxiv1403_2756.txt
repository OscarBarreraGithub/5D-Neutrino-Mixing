Abstract extract:
  Flavor- and CP-violating electromagnetic or chromomagnetic dipole operators
  in the quark sector are generated in a large class of new physics models.
  These operators are strongly constrained by measurements of the neutron EDM
  and by flavor-changing neutral-current observables including B -> X_s gamma
  and epsilon'/epsilon.
  The scenarios analyzed include partial compositeness, composite Higgs, and
  warped extra-dimensional models.
  In models with wrong-chirality Yukawa couplings, the authors find a strong
  bound from the neutron EDM irrespective of flavor structure.
