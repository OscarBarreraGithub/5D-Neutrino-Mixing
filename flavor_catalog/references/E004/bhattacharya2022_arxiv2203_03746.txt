Abstract extract:
  The work presents results for the neutron electric dipole moment due to
  dimension-4 and dimension-6 gluonic CP violation and the isovector quark
  chromoelectric dipole moment.
  The calculation uses clover valence quarks on HISQ dynamical ensembles
  generated by the MILC Collaboration.
  For gluonic operators, the authors use the gradient flow scheme to obtain
  divergence-free continuum results.
  For the chromoelectric dipole moment operator, the authors discuss
  nonperturbative control of quadratically divergent mixing with the
  pseudoscalar operator.
