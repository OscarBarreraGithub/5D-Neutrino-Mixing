Title:
Search for lepton-flavor-violating tau^- -> mu^- mu+ mu^- decays at Belle II.

Authors:
Belle II Collaboration: I. Adachi et al.

Submission and publication metadata:
Submitted 12 May 2024; last revised 28 Nov 2024.
Report number: Belle II Preprint 2024-012, KEK Preprint 2024-6.
Journal reference: J. High Energ. Phys. 2024, 62 (2024).
Related DOI: 10.1007/JHEP09(2024)062.

Relevant abstract extract:
The search uses a 424 fb^-1 sample recorded by Belle II at SuperKEKB.
The selection is based on inclusive reconstruction of the non-signal tau
decay and a boosted decision tree for background suppression. One signal
candidate is observed, compatible with the expected background.

Canonical result:
BR(tau^- -> mu^- mu+ mu-) < 1.9 x 10^-8 at 90% confidence level.
The arXiv abstract states this was the most stringent bound to date at
the time of the paper.
