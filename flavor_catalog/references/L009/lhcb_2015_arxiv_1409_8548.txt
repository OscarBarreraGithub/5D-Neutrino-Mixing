Title:
Search for the lepton flavour violating decay tau^- -> mu^- mu+ mu^-.

Authors:
LHCb Collaboration: R. Aaij et al.

Metadata:
Submitted 30 Sep 2014; last revised 1 Apr 2015.
Report numbers: LHCb-PAPER-2014-052, CERN-PH-EP-2014-236.
Journal reference: JHEP 02 (2015) 121.
Related DOI: 10.1007/JHEP02(2015)121.

Relevant abstract extract:
The search uses 1.0 fb^-1 of pp data at 7 TeV and 2.0 fb^-1 at 8 TeV
recorded by LHCb. No signal evidence is found.

Canonical result:
BR(tau^- -> mu^- mu+ mu-) < 4.6 x 10^-8 at 90% confidence level.
