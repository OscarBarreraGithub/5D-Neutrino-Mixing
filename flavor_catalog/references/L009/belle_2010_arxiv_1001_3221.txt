Title:
Search for Lepton Flavor Violating Tau Decays into Three Leptons with
719 Million Produced Tau+Tau- Pairs.

Authors:
K. Hayasaka, K. Inami, Y. Miyazaki, Belle Collaboration.

Metadata:
Submitted 19 Jan 2010.
Journal reference: Phys. Lett. B 687:139-143, 2010.
Related DOI: 10.1016/j.physletb.2010.03.037.

Relevant arXiv abstract extract:
The search uses 782 fb^-1 of Belle data at the KEKB asymmetric-energy
e+e- collider. No evidence for the six three-lepton LFV tau decays is
observed, and 90% CL upper limits lie between 1.5 x 10^-8 and 2.7 x 10^-8.

Relevant TeX-source table/result extract:
For tau^- -> mu^- mu+ mu^-, the nominal-selection efficiency is 7.6%,
the expected background is 0.13 +/- 0.06 events, the systematic uncertainty
is 7.4%, no events are observed in the signal region, and
BR(tau^- -> mu^- mu+ mu^-) < 2.1 x 10^-8 at 90% confidence level.

Summary extract:
The paper reports BR(tau^- -> mu^- mu+ mu^-) < 2.1 x 10^-8 and says the
six limits improved the best previous published upper limits by factors
from 1.3 to 1.6.
