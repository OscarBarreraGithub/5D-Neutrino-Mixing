Title:
Limits on tau Lepton-Flavor Violating Decays in three charged leptons.

Authors:
BaBar Collaboration: J. P. Lees et al.

Metadata:
Submitted 24 Feb 2010.
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. D 81:111101, 2010.
Related DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.81.111101.

Relevant arXiv abstract extract:
The search uses 468 fb^-1 collected with the BaBar detector at PEP-II.
For all six decay modes the observed event counts are compatible with
background expectations. Upper limits on branching fractions are set in
the range (1.8--3.3) x 10^-8 at 90% confidence level.

Relevant TeX-source table/result extract:
For tau^- -> mu^- mu+ mu^-, the efficiency is 6.6 +/- 0.6%, the expected
background is 0.44 +/- 0.17 events, the expected 90% CL branching-fraction
upper limit is 4.0 x 10^-8, zero events are observed, and the observed
90% CL upper limit is 3.3 x 10^-8.
