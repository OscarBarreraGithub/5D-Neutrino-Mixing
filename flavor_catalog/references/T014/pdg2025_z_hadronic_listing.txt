Minimal local text extract for T014 (down-sector FCNC Z decays).
This source does not list dedicated Z -> bs, Z -> bd, or Z -> sd branching
limits. It is retained as the PDG provenance for the inclusive hadronic-Z
width whose agreement with the SM is used by later sources to quote the
generic non-standard hadronic Z -> qq' bound.

Citation line in listing:
S. Navas et al. (Particle Data Group), Phys. Rev. D 110, 030001 (2024)
and 2025 update.
