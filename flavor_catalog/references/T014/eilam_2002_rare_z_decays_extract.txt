Abstract extract:
Motivated by the impact of rare decays and the Giga-Z option at TESLA, the
paper investigates the rare decay Z -> b sbar in extensions of the SM.

Text extract:
When referring to the bsbar mode, the paper means Z -> b sbar + bbar s. It
states that in the SM:

  BR(Z -> b sbar) ~= 3 x 10^-8.

It also quotes a preliminary DELPHI limit:

  sum_{q=d,s} BR(Z -> b qbar) <= 1.8 x 10^-3 at 90% CL,

based on about 3.5 x 10^6 hadronic decays.

Table 1 extract, maximal Br(Z -> b sbar) in extensions of the SM:

  SM, W+ (no scalars): 3 x 10^-8
  2HDMII, H+:          10^-10
  T2HDM, H+:           10^-8
  sb-ss mix:           10^-6
  st-sc mix:           10^-8
  trilinear-trilinear R-parity violation: 10^-10
  trilinear-bilinear R-parity violation:  10^-6

The paper concludes that Giga-Z experiments could place significant limits or
discover beyond-SM scenarios through hadronic neutral-current flavor-changing
transitions.
