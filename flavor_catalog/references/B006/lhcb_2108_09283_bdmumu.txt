Title:
Measurement of the B0_s -> mu+ mu- decay properties and search for the B0 -> mu+ mu- and B0_s -> mu+ mu- gamma decays

Dataset:
LHCb pp collisions at sqrt(s) = 7, 8, and 13 TeV, integrated luminosities 1, 2, and 6 fb^-1.

Relevant extracted results:
No significant signal for B0 -> mu+ mu- was found.
B(B0 -> mu+ mu-) < 2.3 x 10^-10 at 90% CL.
B(B0 -> mu+ mu-) < 2.6 x 10^-10 at 95% CL.
Ratio R_{mu+mu-} = B(B0 -> mu+ mu-) / B(B_s0 -> mu+ mu-) < 0.081 at 90% CL and <0.095 at 95% CL.
