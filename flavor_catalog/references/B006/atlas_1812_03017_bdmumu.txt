Title:
Study of the rare decays of B0_s and B0 mesons into muon pairs using data collected during 2015 and 2016 with the ATLAS detector

Relevant extracted results:
Using 26.3 fb^-1 of 13 TeV pp data, ATLAS obtains:
B(B0 -> mu+ mu-) < 4.3 x 10^-10 at 95% CL.

Combined with the Run 1 ATLAS result:
B(B0 -> mu+ mu-) < 2.1 x 10^-10 at 95% CL.
