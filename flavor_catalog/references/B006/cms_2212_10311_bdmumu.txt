Local snapshot purpose: primary CMS result used by PDG for B0 -> mu+ mu-.

Title:
Measurement of the B0_s -> mu+ mu- decay properties and search for the B0 -> mu+ mu- decay in proton-proton collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV

Dataset:
CMS pp collisions at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV, 2016-2018, integrated luminosity 140 fb^-1.

Relevant extracted result:
No evidence for the B0 -> mu+ mu- decay was found.
The 90% and 95% confidence-level upper limits from the CLs criterion are:
B(B0 -> mu+ mu-) < 1.5 x 10^-10 at 90% CL.
B(B0 -> mu+ mu-) < 1.9 x 10^-10 at 95% CL.
