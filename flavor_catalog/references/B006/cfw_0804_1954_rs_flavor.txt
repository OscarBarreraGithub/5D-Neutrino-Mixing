Title:
The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs

Relevant abstract extract:
The authors study flavor in 5D warped models and re-examine flavor constraints on new-physics scales in standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios. They find generic KK-gluon lower scales around 21 TeV in the standard RS case and around 33 TeV in the composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs setup, with RS-GIM suppression but enhanced flavor-violating amplitudes from new model ingredients.
