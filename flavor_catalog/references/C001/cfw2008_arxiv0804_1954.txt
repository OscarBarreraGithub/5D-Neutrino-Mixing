Title: The Flavor of the Composite Pseudo-Goldstone Higgs
Authors: Csaba Csaki, Adam Falkowski, Andreas Weiler
Publication: JHEP 09 (2008) 008

Selected abstract metadata:
- Studies flavor constraints in 5D warped models and composite pseudo-Goldstone
  Higgs scenarios.
- Re-examines flavor constraints in standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios.
- Reports generic KK-gluon mass bounds around 21 TeV in standard RS scenarios.
- Reports stronger bounds around 33 TeV in the composite pseudo-Goldstone case.
- Concludes that fully anarchic warped flavor is strongly constrained and may
  require partial flavor symmetries.
