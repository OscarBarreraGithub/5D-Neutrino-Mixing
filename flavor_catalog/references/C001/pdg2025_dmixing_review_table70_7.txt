Minimal text excerpt from the PDG review:

The review defines x = Delta m / Gamma and y = Delta Gamma / (2 Gamma). It states
that the HFLAV global fit excludes the no-mixing point x = y = 0 at more than
11.5 sigma and is consistent with CP conservation at the two-sigma level.

Table 70.7, HFLAV global fit results, selected values:

x12 (%)    0.406 +/- 0.043      95% C.L. interval [0.321, 0.490]

The review notes that D0-D0bar mixing is presumably dominated by long-distance
amplitudes that are difficult to calculate, limiting new-physics interpretations.
