Relevant arXiv-source excerpt from results.tex / conclusion.tex:
The analysis uses all pp collision data collected by LHCb from 2011 to 2018,
corresponding to 9 fb^{-1}. The measured R_K* values are:
  low q^2 R_K* = 0.927^{+0.093}_{-0.087} (stat) ^{+0.036}_{-0.035} (syst)
  central q^2 R_K* = 1.027^{+0.072}_{-0.068} (stat) ^{+0.027}_{-0.026} (syst)
where low q^2 is 0.1 < q^2 < 1.1 GeV^2/c^4 and central q^2 is
1.1 < q^2 < 6.0 GeV^2/c^4.

SM-prediction table excerpt:
  R_K* low q^2 SM prediction = 0.9832
  R_K* central q^2 SM prediction = 0.9964
  additional QED uncertainty assigned = 0.01 for each R_K* bin

Conclusion excerpt:
The four measured relative decay rates are compatible with the SM, with the
combined result agreeing with the SM prediction at 0.2 standard deviations.
The results supersede previous LHCb measurements of R_K and R_K*; both R_K*
central values move upward relative to the previous R_K* publication.
