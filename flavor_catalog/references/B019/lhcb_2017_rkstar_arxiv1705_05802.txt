Title: Test of lepton universality with B0 -> K*0 l+ l- decays
Authors: LHCb Collaboration, R. Aaij et al.
Year: 2017
Local snapshot purpose: historical post-2008 R_K* anomaly measurement, now superseded by LHCb 2023.

Relevant arXiv-source excerpt from title/results/conclusion:
The measured ratios were
  R_K* = 0.66^{+0.11}_{-0.07} (stat) +/- 0.03 (syst)
    for 0.045 < q^2 < 1.1 GeV^2/c^4,
  R_K* = 0.69^{+0.11}_{-0.07} (stat) +/- 0.05 (syst)
    for 1.1 < q^2 < 6.0 GeV^2/c^4.

The publication reported compatibility with SM expectations at approximately
2.1--2.3 standard deviations in the low-q^2 region and 2.4--2.5 standard
deviations in the central-q^2 region, depending on the theory prediction used.
