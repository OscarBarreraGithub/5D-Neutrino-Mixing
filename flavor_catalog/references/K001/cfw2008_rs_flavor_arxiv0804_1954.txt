Relevant extracted context:
- The paper studies flavor in five-dimensional warped models with a composite pseudo-Goldstone Higgs.
- The abstract reports that, after reexamining flavor constraints in standard Randall-Sundrum-type scenarios, the KK gluon mass should generically be heavier than about 21 TeV.
- For the pseudo-Goldstone scenario, the abstract reports a KK gluon mass of at least about 33 TeV.
- The abstract identifies strong flavor bounds as pressure on fully anarchic warped flavor and motivates partial flavor symmetries.
