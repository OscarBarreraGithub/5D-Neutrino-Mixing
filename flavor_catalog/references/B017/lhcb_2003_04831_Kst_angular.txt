Abstract excerpt:
An angular analysis of B0 -> K*0(-> K+ pi-) mu+ mu- is presented using a data
set corresponding to 4.7 fb^{-1} of pp collision data collected with the LHCb
experiment. The full set of CP-averaged observables are determined in bins of
the invariant mass squared of the dimuon system. Contamination from decays
with the K+ pi- system in an S-wave configuration is taken into account.

The arXiv abstract states that the tension seen between previous LHCb results
and Standard Model predictions persists with the new data, with the precise
significance depending on the choice of theory nuisance parameters.
