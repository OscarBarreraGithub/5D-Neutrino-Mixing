Title: The Global Fits of New Physics in b -> s after R_{K(*)} 2022 Release
Authors: Qiaoyi Wen and Fanrong Xu
Year: 2023
Local snapshot purpose: post-R_K(*) global-fit status for C9/C10-style Wilson constraints.

ArXiv metadata excerpt:
Submitted on 30 May 2023; last revised 29 Oct 2023.
Version accepted by Physical Review D.

Abstract excerpt:
The measurement of lepton universality parameters R_{K(*)} was updated by
LHCb in December 2022, indicating that the well-known anomalies in FCNC
processes of B-meson decays have faded away. The work asks whether all new
physics possibilities related to b -> s l+l- have been excluded.

The paper adopts an effective Hamiltonian for b -> s transitions and encodes
BSM effects in Wilson coefficients. It uses around 200 observables in leptonic
and semileptonic decays of B mesons and bottom baryons, measured by LHCb, CMS,
ATLAS, Belle, and BaBar. It performs fits before and after the 2022 R_K(*)
release and reports that in three of four scenarios, Delta C9^mu still has a
deviation around or more than 4 sigma from the Standard Model.
