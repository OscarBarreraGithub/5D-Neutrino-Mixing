Abstract excerpt:
The paper studies the flavor structure of 5D warped models with a composite
pseudo-Goldstone Higgs. It re-examines flavor constraints on the new-physics
scale in standard Randall-Sundrum scenarios and finds that the KK gluon mass
should generically be heavier than about 21 TeV. It compares composite-Higgs
flavor structure with RS flavor and notes that flavor bounds can be stronger
or weaker depending on assumptions such as the gluon boundary kinetic term.
