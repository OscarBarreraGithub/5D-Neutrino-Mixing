Title:
Search for flavour-changing neutral-current couplings between the top quark
and the photon with the ATLAS detector at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV

Authors: ATLAS Collaboration
Report number: CERN-EP-2022-042
Journal reference: Phys. Lett. B 842 (2023) 137379; erratum Phys. Lett. B 847
(2024) 138286
Related DOI: 10.1016/j.physletb.2022.137379

Relevant source excerpt, normalized:

The analysis uses pp collision data at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV collected during
LHC Run 2, corresponding to 139 fb^-1. Both FCNC top-quark production and
top-quark decay are considered. Events contain a charged lepton, missing
transverse momentum, a b-tagged jet, one high-momentum photon, and possibly
additional jets. No significant excess is observed.

Observed 95% CL upper limits on branching fractions for left-handed tqgamma:
  B(t -> u gamma) < 0.85 x 10^-5
  B(t -> c gamma) < 4.2 x 10^-5

Observed 95% CL upper limits on branching fractions for right-handed tqgamma:
  B(t -> u gamma) < 1.2 x 10^-5
  B(t -> c gamma) < 4.5 x 10^-5

This is the ATLAS number often quoted before the later CMS-TOP-21-013
charm-specific improvement.
