Title:
Search for flavor changing neutral current interactions of the top quark in
final states with a photon and additional jets in proton-proton collisions at
sqrt(s) = 13 TeV

Authors: CMS Collaboration
Report number: CMS-TOP-21-013, CERN-EP-2023-214
Journal reference: Phys. Rev. D 109 (2024) 072004
Related DOI: 10.1103/PhysRevD.109.072004
arXiv: 2312.08229, v2 submitted 2024-04-06

Relevant source excerpt, normalized:

The analysis uses pp collision data at sqrt(s) = 13 TeV corresponding to
138 fb^-1. It searches for single top plus photon production and top-pair
events in which one top decays through a tqgamma FCNC vertex, with q = u or c.
No significant excess over the Standard Model prediction is observed.

Observed (expected) 95% CL upper limits on branching fractions:
  B(t -> u gamma) < 0.95 x 10^-5 (1.20 x 10^-5 expected)
  B(t -> c gamma) < 1.51 x 10^-5 (1.54 x 10^-5 expected)

CMS also quotes the corresponding anomalous coupling limits:
  kappa_tugamma < 6.2 x 10^-3
  kappa_tcgamma < 7.7 x 10^-3

The CMS public page states that the t -> c gamma limit is significantly tighter
than previous results.
