ArXiv metadata excerpt:
Submitted 6 Nov 2024; last revised 6 Feb 2026. The abstract says the review
reports heavy-quark decay constants, form factors, and mixing parameters of
bottom mesons relevant for CKM and unitarity-triangle fits.

Extracted B-mixing context:
The FLAG Review 2024 section "Neutral B-meson mixing matrix elements" states
that the Standard Model prediction for Delta m_q is proportional to
f_{B_q}^2 * Bhat_{B_q}, and that experimentally the mass difference is
determined from the oscillation frequency. It also says the SU(3)-breaking
ratio xi can be obtained more precisely than the individual B_q-mixing matrix
elements because some statistical and systematic errors cancel.

Extracted N_f = 2+1+1 FLAG averages used as auxiliary theory inputs:

  f_{B_s} sqrt(Bhat_{B_s}) = 256.1(5.7) MeV
  Bhat_{B_s} = 1.232(53)
  xi = 1.216(16)
  B_{B_s}/B_{B_d} = 1.008(25)

Correlation caution extracted from the FLAG text:
FLAG cautions that decay-constant and mixing quantities are often calculated
on the same or similar ensembles and use the same actions and setups, so
correlations should be considered in unitarity-triangle fits. FLAG recommends
using the quoted mixing numbers directly rather than reconstructing
f_{B_s} sqrt(Bhat_{B_s}) from separate f_{B_s} and Bhat_{B_s} averages.
