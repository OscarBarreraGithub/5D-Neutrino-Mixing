Abstract/result excerpt, paraphrased from the arXiv source:
The paper presents a measurement of Delta m_s using B_s -> D_s^- pi^+
decays in pp collisions collected with the LHCb detector. The Run-2 data set
corresponds to 6 fb^-1 collected in 2015-2018. The oscillation frequency is
found to be

  Delta m_s = 17.7683 +/- 0.0051(stat) +/- 0.0032(syst) ps^-1.

Combining this result with previous LHCb measurements gives

  Delta m_s = 17.7656 +/- 0.0057 ps^-1,

described by the paper as the legacy measurement of the original LHCb detector.

Catalog note:
This LHCb result is not the catalog average by itself, but it explains why the
post-2008 world average is now limited by sub-per-mille experimental precision.
