Source title:
Heavy Flavor Averaging Group (HFLAV), PDG 2025 averages.

Context:
All results published before February 1, 2025 were included in the averages
computed by the lifetime and oscillations subgroup of HFLAV for the 2025 Review
of Particle Physics by the Particle Data Group.

Extracted value used as cross-check in B003:
Section "B_s mixing: oscillations and mass difference" says the combined result
on B_s^0 mixing is obtained from time-dependent measurements of the oscillation
frequency Delta m_s at high-energy hadron colliders:

  Delta m_s = 17.765 +/- 0.006 ps^-1  CDF, LHCb, CMS

The same section states that, with 1/Gamma_s = 1.516 +/- 0.006 ps,
DeltaGamma_s = +0.082 +/- 0.005 ps^-1, and the assumption of no CP violation
in B_s^0 mixing, this leads to:

  x_s = 26.93 +/- 0.10
  chi_s = 0.499314 +/- 0.000005

Notes for catalog use:
- This is the published-only PDG 2025 preparation value.
- The HFLAV Fall 2024 recommended page is used as the primary current HFLAV
  equivalent value in B003 because HFLAV labels it RECOMMENDED.
