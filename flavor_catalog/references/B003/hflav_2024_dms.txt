Source title:
Heavy Flavor Averaging Group (HFLAV), 2024 averages for HFLAV write-up.

Context from HFLAV landing page:
The HFLAV lifetime and oscillation parameters page labels the Fall 2024
averages as RECOMMENDED, includes preprints, and says they are described in
Chapter 5 of S. Banerjee et al. (HFLAV), Phys. Rev. D 113 (2026) 012008,
arXiv:2411.18639.

Extracted value used in B003:
Section "B_s mixing: oscillations and mass difference" says the combined result
on B_s^0 mixing is obtained from time-dependent measurements of the oscillation
frequency Delta m_s at high-energy hadron colliders:

  Delta m_s = 17.766 +/- 0.006 ps^-1  CDF, LHCb, CMS

The same section states that, with 1/Gamma_s = 1.516 +/- 0.006 ps,
DeltaGamma_s = +0.0781 +/- 0.0035 ps^-1, and the assumption of no CP violation
in B_s^0 mixing, this leads to:

  x_s = 26.94 +/- 0.10
  chi_s = 0.499314 +/- 0.000005

Notes for catalog use:
- The measured observable for pdg_or_equivalent is Delta m_s.
- Lifetime, width-difference, x_s, and chi_s values are auxiliary derived or
  companion quantities, not replacements for the measured mass splitting.
