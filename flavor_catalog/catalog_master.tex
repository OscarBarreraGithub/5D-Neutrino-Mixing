\section{Collider RS Resonances}

These entries are direct LHC searches for RS-specific resonances and
heavy partners (KK gauge bosons, custodial top/bottom partners,
vector-like quarks, KK-graviton, and EW-precision-tail observables).
They are complementary to the low-energy flavor entries: LHC reach
($M_{KK}\sim\text{TeV}$) is weaker than the flavor-implied bound
($M_{KK}\sim 47$~TeV at $g_*=3$), but the entries are
RS-distinguishing under custodial / non-anarchic RS scenarios.
