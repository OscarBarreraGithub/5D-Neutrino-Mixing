Scope and triage
A modern Randall–Sundrum flavor analysis should be organized by operator classes, not by a short list of
“famous decays.” In today’s data, the observables that most directly probe warped flavor models are those
sensitive to tree-level or one-loop effects from heavy KK vectors and fermions: ΔF=2 four-fermion
operators, modified Z and W couplings, dipole operators, semileptonic FCNCs, and CP-odd dipoles
such as EDMs. That is why a credible 2026-era inventory must simultaneously cover kaon, Bd,s , charm, top,
electroweak, charged-lepton-flavor-violation, and EDM observables. Recent measurements underscore this

The most useful working labels for a fit are:
• Critical: should be in the baseline likelihood now.
• Important: include unless you are deliberately narrowing the model space.
• Secondary: informative and often diagnostic, but presently weaker or theory-limited.
• Future-facing: not yet decisive, but likely to matter in the near term.

Kaon observables
The kaon sector is still the single most punishing arena for generic warped flavor. The reason is structural:
RS models naturally generate new ΔS = 2 operators, especially chirally enhanced LR structures, and
modified Z -penguins that feed directly into rare kaons. Even in aligned or MFV-inspired versions, kaon
observables remain the cleanest way to test whether down-sector FCNC protection is truly working.

Open questions and limitations
A few parts of the modern landscape remain intrinsically less settled than others. The most important
limitations are the theory status of ε′ /ε, long-distance contributions in KL → μ+ μ− , nonlocal charm
effects in exclusive b

→ sℓ+ ℓ− , and the long-distance SM background in charm mixing and rare charm

decays. Those observables should still be in the map, but not all of them should carry equal likelihood
weight. 44
The highest-confidence conclusion is straightforward: a serious 2026-era RS flavor inventory is
necessarily multi-sector. For generic quark-sector RS it begins with kaons, B -mixing, dipoles, rare
semileptonic B decays, Z

→ bbˉ, and EDMs. For down-aligned or shining constructions it must elevate

charm and top FCNCs. For bulk-lepton versions it must add the muon golden channels and electron EDM
immediately. That is the observable map most likely to survive into a full numerical fit without major
omissions. 45
