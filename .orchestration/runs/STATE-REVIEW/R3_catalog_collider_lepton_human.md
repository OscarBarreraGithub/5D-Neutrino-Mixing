### CHOICES MADE (conventions, model decisions, defaults — with rationale + where set)
- Veto policy: HARD means a failed experimental bound can veto, SOFT flags tension, and INFO must never veto (`flavor_catalog_constraints/base.py:56`, `flavor_catalog_constraints/base.py:59`, `flavor_catalog_constraints/base.py:63`, `flavor_catalog_constraints/base.py:67`). The scan implements two gates: strict survival excludes only rigorous HARD failures, while inclusive survival also excludes proxy HARD failures (`scripts/run_full_catalog_scan.py:908`, `scripts/run_full_catalog_scan.py:930`, `scripts/run_full_catalog_scan.py:932`, `scripts/run_full_catalog_scan.py:933`). Rationale: keep hard measured constraints live, but do not silently mix benchmark/proxy exclusions into the rigorous floor.
- Quark-only scan scope: the production harness uses a 46-ID allowlist, forbids lepton extras including `lepton_lmfv_parameters`, sets `lepton_sector = "dropped (not rigorous)"`, and defers EW002 plus CR005/006/009/011/014 as lepton-followup/deferred scope (`scripts/run_full_catalog_scan.py:64`, `scripts/run_full_catalog_scan.py:69`, `scripts/run_full_catalog_scan.py:73`, `scripts/run_full_catalog_scan.py:75`, `scripts/run_full_catalog_scan.py:521`, `scripts/run_full_catalog_scan.py:523`). - CR001 active budget choice: use the current CMS2026 5.5 TeV benchmark mass-exclusion upper edge as the active HARD budget; older CMS/PDG/ATLAS rows remain loaded for audit/history but are not the active edge (`flavor_catalog_constraints/primary/collider_rs/CR001.py:59`, `flavor_catalog_constraints/primary/collider_rs/CR001.py:282`, `flavor_catalog_constraints/primary/collider_rs/CR001.py:284`, `flavor_catalog/processes/collider_rs/CR001.yaml:82`, `flavor_catalog/processes/collider_rs/CR001.yaml:110`). Rationale: current canonical edge is strongest and directly stated for the benchmark, but it is still a model-dependent mass proxy.

- LMFV carrier convention: the builder constructs a read-only `LMFVLeptonParameters` carrier from the Yukawa result, stores the physical M_KK, and attaches it under `lepton_lmfv_parameters` when lepton sweep inputs are supplied (`flavor_catalog_constraints/physics_adapters/lepton.py:270`, `flavor_catalog_constraints/physics_adapters/lepton.py:308`, `flavor_catalog_constraints/physics_adapters/lepton.py:314`, `flavor_catalog_constraints/physics_adapters/lepton.py:315`, `flavor_catalog_constraints/rs_ew_builder.py:126`, `flavor_catalog_constraints/rs_ew_builder.py:203`).
