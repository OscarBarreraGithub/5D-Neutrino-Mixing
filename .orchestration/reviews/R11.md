## Check 1 — Physics intuition grounded in literature

**Verdict**: PASS
**Literature anchors consulted**: HFLAV CKM 2025 (R_D average), PDG 2026 muon listing S004R4 (mu->3e), ATLAS arXiv:2404.02123 (t->Hc), Csaki-Falkowski-Weiler 0804.1954 (RS baseline).

**Evidence**:
- **B025 (R_D)**: yaml `pdg_or_equivalent.canonical_average` quotes R_D = 0.358 ± 0.024 from HFLAV CKM 2025 (snapshot `flavor_catalog/references/B025/hflav_ckm2025_rdrds.txt`, sha256 logged). Consistent with HFLAV's trajectory (R_D ~ 0.342 in 2023, ~0.358 in 2025 reflecting new LHCb 2024/2025 + Belle II 2025 inputs at `lhcb2025_arxiv2406_03387.txt` and `belleii2025_arxiv2504_11220.txt`). Joint R(D)-R(D*) covariance (rho = -0.374, chi2/dof = 16.683/14) and 3.8sigma combined tension all match the source snapshot (`B025.yaml:92-119`).
- **L002 (mu -> 3e)**: BR upper limit < 1.0e-12 at 90% CL, attributed to SINDRUM 1988 (Bellgardt et al., Nucl. Phys. B 299, 1) via PDG 2026 datablock S004R4 (`L002.yaml:87-99`). This is the canonical literature value; matches PDG. Mu3e phase-I projected single-event sensitivity 2e-15 (arXiv:2009.11690) and updated O(1e-15) / O(1e-16) sensitivity scales from arXiv:2501.14667 are also faithfully captured (`L002.yaml:112-167`).
- **T007 (t -> Hc)**: ATLAS combined limit BR < 3.4e-4, expected < 2.3e-4 (`T007.yaml:111-120`); CMS combined < 3.7e-4 (`T007.yaml:141-151`). Both match arXiv:2404.02123 and arXiv:2407.15172. PDG headline = ATLAS combination value is correct (`T007.tex:21-24`).

**RS interpretation cross-check (T007)**: `T007.tex:39-46` argues anarchic warped models / composite-Higgs variants generate off-diagonal scalar Yukawas in the mass basis, so t->Hc probes top-sector localization complementary to Delta F=2 light-meson constraints. This is the standard RS-flavor narrative (Agashe, Contino, Perez et al.) and matches the cited CFW 0804.1954 baseline. The kinematic nit added by `subtlety-microfix` ("for the observed Higgs, on-shell h->tc is kinematically forbidden", `T007.tex:73-75`) is correct: m_h ~ 125 GeV < m_t - m_c, so the FCNC channel is the top decay direction, not the Higgs decay. Good catch by the subtlety pass.
