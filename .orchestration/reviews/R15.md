**Evidence**:

- **E009 (Weinberg 3-gluon CP operator) — neutron-EDM anchor verified.** `E009.yaml:105-146` records the PDG Live 2026 S017EDM block `|d_n| < 1.8 x 10^-26 e cm @ 90% CL` and the Abel et al. 2020 primary measurement `d_n = (0.0 +/- 1.1_stat +/- 0.2_sys) x 10^-26 e cm`. The Pospelov-Ritz normalization `|d_n(w)| ~ e * 22 MeV * w(1 GeV)` at `E009.yaml:148-165` gives the derived bound `|w(1 GeV)| < 4.1e-11 GeV^-2` (calc: `9.12e-13 GeV^-1 / 0.022 GeV = 4.15e-11 -> 4.1e-11`, derivation explicitly at `E009.yaml:192`). The alternative Haisch-Hala `O_6` convention gives `(d_n/e)_O6 = 74(1 +/- 0.5) MeV` (`E009.yaml:198-216`) and the derived `|C_6| < 1.2e-11 GeV^-2` at `E009.yaml:217-236`. The RS framing at `E009.tex:37-42` correctly identifies KK-fermion / heavy-Higgs / colored-resonance integrate-out as the source of qCEDM and Weinberg-operator threshold contributions, exactly per Koenig-Neubert-Straub 2014. **Spot-check arithmetic**: `1.8e-26 cm / 1.97327e-14 cm/GeV^-1 = 9.12e-13 GeV^-1` (`E009.yaml:259`), confirmed by hand. **No physics misstatement.**

- **L023 (neutrino trident) — CCFR / CHARM-II / NuTeV ratios verified.** `L023.yaml:94-152` records the three historical ratios: `CHARM-II sigma_exp/sigma_SM = 1.58 +/- 0.64`, `CCFR = 0.82 +/- 0.28`, `NuTeV = 0.72 +1.73/-0.72`. NuTeV's `17 events vs 9.8 +/- 2.9 bg + 10.8 +/- 0.3 SM` (`L023.yaml:142-144`) — confirms the "preliminary/weak" categorization. The DUNE projection `25% accuracy` at `L023.yaml:187-197` is the Altmannshofer-2019 abstract value; the Altmannshofer 2014 CCFR `95% CL` exclusion contours (`L023.yaml:154-164`) are the canonical L_mu-L_tau reinterpretation.
