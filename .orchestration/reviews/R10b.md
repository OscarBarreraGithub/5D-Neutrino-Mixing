## Physics

**Verdict**: APPROVE

Spot-checked three high-impact entries: B005, B011, B002 (per task seed).

- **B005 Bs0 -> mu+ mu-** (`flavor_catalog/processes/beauty/B005.yaml:64-99`,
  `.tex:21-42`): canonical anchor is PDG live/API 2026
  `BR = (3.34 +/- 0.27) x 10^-9` (yaml:70-79; tex:22-26). The HFLAV 2023
  world average `3.45(29) x 10^-9` cited in the task seed is retained as
  auxiliary `hflav_historical_average` (yaml:80-88; tex:36) — fully
  compatible (the two numbers agree at ~0.3σ; PDG live is the newer
  weighted combination). SM prediction Czaja-Misiak 2024 `3.64(12) x 10^-9`
  (yaml:89-99, arXiv:2407.03810) is the standard post-2020 reference.
  Individual experiment inputs CMS `3.83 x 10^-9`, LHCb `3.09 x 10^-9`,
  ATLAS `2.8 x 10^-9` (yaml:100-122; tex:37-42) are the published values.
  Framing as "robust short-distance rare-decay constraint" with
  C10/CS/CP Wilson sensitivity (`.tex:65-73`) is standard.

- **B011 Bbar -> X_s gamma** (`B011.yaml:75-130`, `.tex:21-44`): canonical
  anchor is HFLAV 2024 `BR(E_gamma > 1.6 GeV) = 3.49(19) x 10^-4`
  (yaml:75-90; tex:22-27). This matches the task seed
  `3.49(19) x 10^-4` exactly. SM benchmarks Misiak-Rehman-Steinhauser 2020
  `3.40(17) x 10^-4` (yaml:91-102, arXiv:2002.01548) and PDG-reviewed
  Misiak et al. 2015 `3.36(23) x 10^-4` (yaml:103-117, arXiv:1503.01789)
  are both real, both quoted correctly. RS framing as C7/C8 dipole
  observable complementing the Delta F=2 lane (`.tex:46-77`) is standard.

- **B002 S_psiKS / sin 2beta** (`B002.yaml:70-149`, `.tex:22-39`):
  canonical anchor is HFLAV Summer 2025 all-charmonium
  `sin(2 beta) = 0.710 +/- 0.011` (yaml:71-83; tex:28-29); mode-specific
  J/psi K_S average `S_psiKS = 0.712 +/- 0.011` (yaml:84-95; tex:34-36).
  The task seed `0.708(11)` is the slightly earlier HFLAV value; the
  catalog uses a newer (Summer 2025) snapshot. The two are compatible at
  ~0.2σ; the upward shift is consistent with the post-2023 addition of
  LHCb `0.717(13)(8)` (yaml:110-124, arXiv:2309.09728) and Belle II
  `0.724(35)(9)` (yaml:125-140, arXiv:2402.17260) primary inputs.
  Penguin-pollution bound `|Delta phi_d| <= 0.68 deg` from
  Frings-Nierste-Wiebusch 2015 (yaml:141-149) is the canonical bound.
  Implementation_difficulty=LOW is well-justified: existing Delta F=2
  Wilson + QCD-running machinery covers the operator basis; only complex
  M12_d retention and penguin policy are missing.

**RS cross-check (B011 = b -> s gamma)**: in anarchic warped models the
electromagnetic and chromomagnetic dipole operators C7/C8 receive
KK-fermion / KK-gauge / charged-scalar loop contributions even when
tree-level Delta F=2 mixing is satisfied (Agashe-Contino-Pomarol
hep-ph/0412089; Csaki-Falkowski-Weiler 0804.1954). The catalog framing
(`B011.tex:46-77`) — that this loop-level chirality-flipping observable
is a sharp complement to the tree-level four-quark constraint surface —
is the standard channel decomposition in the post-2008 RS-flavor
literature. The CFW 2008 baseline snapshot is referenced in B005 only,
not B011; this is a stylistic choice (B011 leans on the more directly
relevant Misiak SM-theory references rather than CFW Delta F=2 framing).
