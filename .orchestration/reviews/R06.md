## Physics

**Verdict**: APPROVE.

**Reference equations cross-checked** against `docs/audits/cfw_convention_extract.md`:

- CFW operator basis (`C1`, `C4`, `C5`) — 0804.1954 Sec. 2, eq. 2.18, PDF p. 7; Appendix A, eq. A.7, PDF pp. 36-37. KK-gluon tree matching `C4=-g_R g_L/M_G^2`, `C5=+g_R g_L/(3M_G^2)`. Same sign pattern as repo's `quarkConstraints/deltaf2.py` post-R04 BMU-mapped scalar-LR basis. **No sign flip needed** (`cfw_convention_extract.md:31-49`).
- CFW c-pattern — Sec. 2 + Fig. 1 caption, PDF pp. 9-10. `c_q3 in [0.4, 0.45]`, `c_u3 in [-0.3, -0.05]`, `c_d3=-0.55`, `|Y_*| in [1, 3]`. Inverse-c design; the repo's forward fixed-c scan cannot replicate this, only project (`cfw_vs_ours.md:8`).
- `g_s^*` convention — Sec. 2, eqs. 2.20-2.21, PDF p. 10. `g_s^*~6` -> `21 TeV` (default boundary-term); no-UV-boundary-term -> `g_s^*~3` -> `10.5 TeV`; `g_s^*~4pi` -> `42 TeV` (`cfw_convention_extract.md:92-117`). This is the convention error caught in review-v1.
- PDG-30% gate — Sec. 5.3, eqs. 5.26-5.27, PDF pp. 32-33. Symmetric stored-residual factor `1/(1-0.30) = 1.428571` (`cfw_convention_extract.md:118-131`).
- CFW hadronic inputs: UTfit `Delta F = 2` model-independent bounds (`Lambda_Im C4K = 1.6e5 TeV`), NOT explicit bags. Their pre-audit budget was `|2.228e-3 - 1.81e-3| = 4.18e-4`; the post-audit BGS 2020 budget is `|2.228e-3 - 2.161e-3| = 6.7e-5`. Ratio `4.18e-4 / 6.7e-5 = 6.2388`, hence M_KK rescales by `sqrt(6.24) = 2.498` (`cfw_reproduction.md:115-122`).

**Factor-2.2 result, sourced**: `23.37157 TeV / 10.5 TeV = 2.226` at `g_s^*=3` matched comparison, identically `46.74314 / 21.0 = 2.226` at `g_s^*~6` (`cfw_reproduction.md:74-89,170-175`). This is NOT a matrix-element doubling or a stray sqrt(2); it is the combination of (i) ~2.498x from the BGS 2020 epsilon_K^SM budget tightening, plus a residual ~1.235x from the harder forward-only subset selected by the CFW 30% gate (only `n=217` rows survive in the forward fixed-c ensemble — `cfw_reproduction.md:127-140`).

**Peer-review history is documented and honest**: `docs/phase_logs/phase2_h7_review.md` REJECT-WITH-REVISIONS caught the original "11% agreement" framing as a `g_s^*` convention error; commits `11e0d58`, `321fe0f`, `7c284a8`, `2a3a2c0` correctly fixed both the plot, the prose, and added a regression test pinning the corrected ratio. `phase2_h7_review_v2.md` APPROVE confirms `grep -nE "11%|qualitatively consistent"` returns zero hits in `docs/audits/cfw_*.md` and `docs/quark_scan_methodology_note.tex`. Independently verified (zero hits).
