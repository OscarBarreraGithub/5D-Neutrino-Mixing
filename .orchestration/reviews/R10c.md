**RS cross-check (T010 = Z bbar b pole observables)**: in anarchic warped
models the third-generation left-handed doublet is typically IR-localized
to fit the top mass, which makes the tree-level Z b_L b_L coupling
sensitive to gauge-fermion KK mixing (Agashe-Contino-Pomarol
hep-ph/0412089; Carena-Ponton-Santiago-Wagner hep-ph/0607106; Casagrande
et al. 0807.4937). The catalog framing (`T010.tex:31-40`) — that R_b
constrains the total partial width while A_b/A_FB^{0,b} probe the chiral
balance, with custodial protection of Z b_L b_L being decisive for
whether this becomes a leading or diagnostic bound — is the standard
post-2008 EW-precision interpretation in RS, and matches the Casagrande
2008 RS EWPT reference snapshotted at `references/T010/casagrande_2008_rs_ewpt.txt`
and the CFW 2008 RS-flavor baseline at `references/T010/cfw_2008_rs_flavor.txt`.
