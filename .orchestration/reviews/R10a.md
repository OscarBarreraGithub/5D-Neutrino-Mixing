## Physics

**Verdict**: APPROVE

Spot-checked three high-impact entries: K001, K003, K005 (the task seed
named "K005 = K+->pi+nunu" but the catalog assigns that mode to K004, with
K005 = KL->pi0nunu; both are present and the K004 anchor was also cross-checked).

- **K001 epsilon_K**: yaml `pdg_or_equivalent.canonical_experimental_value.value
  = 0.002228 +/- 0.000011` (`flavor_catalog/processes/kaon/K001.yaml:73-76`)
  matches PDG-2026 |epsilon| = (2.228 +/- 0.011)e-3. SM benchmark Brod-Gorbahn-
  Stamou |epsilon_K|_SM = 2.16(18)e-3 (`K001.yaml:83-92`, arXiv:1911.06822)
  is the standard post-2020 reference. tex (`K001.tex:22-24`, `:30-34`)
  reproduces both numbers verbatim. RS context cited to Csaki-Falkowski-Weiler
  2008 (KK-gluon scale ~21 TeV anarchic / ~33 TeV pseudo-Goldstone,
  `K001.yaml:113`), consistent with the canonical anarchic-RS Delta F=2
  literature.
- **K003 Re(eps'/eps)**: yaml `value = 0.00166 +/- 0.00023`
  (`K003.yaml:66-68`) is the current PDG-live S013 value, equivalent to
  (16.6 +/- 2.3)e-4. tex (`K003.tex:22-25`) renders both forms. Supporting
  values KTeV (19.2 +/- 2.1)e-4, NA48 (14.7 +/- 2.2)e-4, RBC/UKQCD
  21.7(2.6)(6.2)(5.0)e-4, Aebischer-Bobeth-Buras octet/nonet (17.4 +/- 6.1)
  / (13.9 +/- 5.2)e-4 (`K003.yaml:95-144`) are real published values from
  cited arXiv IDs. RS interpretation (LR penguins Q_6, Q_8, chromomagnetic
  O_8g; `K003.tex:40-44`) is the standard channel decomposition.
- **K005 KL->pi0nunu**: yaml limit `<2.2e-9 at 90% CL` (`K005.yaml:97`)
  matches PDG-live S013R40 (KOTO 2025, PRL 134 081802; arXiv:2411.11237).
  SM benchmarks 2.59(29)e-11 (Brod-Gorbahn-Stamou) and 2.94(15)e-11
  (Buras-Venturini, V_cb/V_ub-independent) are the two standard post-2020
  predictions. tex (`K005.tex:21-31`) preserves the numbers and the KOTO
  background / single-event-sensitivity / zero-candidate provenance.
- **K004 spot anchor**: B(K+->pi+nunu) = (13.0 +3.3/-3.0)e-11 from NA62
  JHEP 02 (2025) 191 (`K004.yaml:88-100`) and preliminary 2026 update to
  (9.6 +1.9/-1.8)e-11; both real, both quoted to within stated uncertainty.

**RS cross-check (K001)**: epsilon_K in warped flavor receives KK-gluon-
exchange contributions to LR operators (chirally enhanced bag parameters
B_4, B_5). This is the canonical channel of Agashe-Contino-Pomarol
(hep-ph/0412089) and Csaki-Falkowski-Weiler (0804.1954); the catalog cites
the latter explicitly. The operator basis is correct and the KK-gluon
scale bound (~21 TeV for anarchic) is consistent with the post-2008
literature including Carena-Ponton-Santiago-Wagner (hep-ph/0701055).
