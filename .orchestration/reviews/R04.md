## Summary

The unit fixes the leading-log QCD RG running of the ΔF=2 Wilson coefficients in two corrective stages. Commit `7f71908` rewrote the BMU-LO RG running (replacing a wrong scalar-LR ADM and inverted alpha-ratio, adding top-threshold crossing, three-segment evolution `n_f = 6 → 5 → 4` for `3 TeV → 2 GeV`, and a closed-form audit reference). Commit `c80a8e0` then fixed a sign-convention bug exposed by peer review: the BMU vector-LR operator `Q1_LR^BMU` had been mapped as `+2 O5_LR` while the hadronic contraction used the conventional positive-sign `B5` input, leaving the chain internally sign-inconsistent. The fix flipped the scalar ADM off-diagonal from `+6` to `-6` (equivalently `Q1_LR^BMU = -2 O5_LR`). The benchmark `r=0.25` epsilon_K ratio changed from a tree-level pre-audit `0.5350` → post-RG-direction-fix `2.3677` → post-sign-fix `1.9286`. Cumulative hole-5+hole-6 invalidation-gate factor on the epsilon_K ratio: `6.2388 × 3.6052 = 22.49×`.

**Independent re-derivation of BMU ↔ scalar-LR sign**:
The reviewer independently reproduced the operator-to-coefficient map from the BMU operator definitions in `docs/audits/wilson_rg_reference_values.md:27-41` (which match the BMU eq. (2.3)-(2.5) conventions). Starting from
```
Q1_LR^BMU = (bar h γ^μ P_L q)(bar h γ_μ P_R q)
Q2_LR^BMU = (bar h P_L q)(bar h P_R q)
O4_LR    = (bar h^α P_L q^α)(bar h^β P_R q^β)
O5_LR    = (bar h^α P_L q^β)(bar h^β P_R q^α)
```
the standard color-Fierz identity `(γ^μ P_L)(γ_μ P_R)|_{color singlet} = -2 (P_L)(P_R)|_{color rearranged}` gives `Q1_LR^BMU = -2 O5_LR` and `Q2_LR^BMU = O4_LR` directly. Inverting the implied 2×2 operator map and applying the standard `gamma_code = A^-1 gamma_BMU A` similarity (`A` mapping coefficients) reproduces `[[-16, -6], [0, 2]]` exactly from the BMU coefficient ADM `[[2, 0], [12, -16]]`. The off-diagonal `-6` is therefore the literature-correct sign; the original `+6` was the bug.
- Reviewer derivation log: see the `numpy` block executed during this review, which prints `gamma_code = [[-16, -6], [0, 2]]` and eigenvalues `{-16, 2}` matching `_GAMMA_LR` at `quarkConstraints/qcd_running.py:54-57`.
