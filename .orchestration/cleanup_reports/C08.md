## Scope

Three INFO issues in `.orchestration/ISSUES.md` track stale `open_issues`

- **R10b-I3** — Five Wave-1 beauty yamls (B002, B005, B009, B011, B015)
  carry `open_issues` headline-choice / scope-boundary notes that CA Wave-1
  passed CHK-1..CHK-8 without formally closing.

C08 is a docs-only cleanup. For each unresolved `open_issues` entry, it
prepends a clear resolution marker — RESOLVED-by-C08 (when the decision
has effectively been made in the yaml itself), DEFERRED (when a real but
non-urgent follow-up remains), or CLOSED-as-N/A (when the note records a
historical decision already honored downstream). The original text is
preserved verbatim after each marker so the audit trail is intact.

R11-I3 (B025 no-PDF-policy gap) and R10b-I1/I2 (B011 typo / B009 missing
state field) are explicitly **out of C08 scope** and remain assigned to
C09 per `.orchestration/CLEANUP_QUEUE.md`. B025.yaml is therefore not
touched by this unit.

## Method

Each `open_issues` block is a `list[str]` in the
`flavor_catalog.process.v1` schema. The closure strategy preserves the
list-of-strings shape and the original wording, but prepends one of three
status tags inside `[ ... ]`:

- `[RESOLVED-by-C08 2026-05-25]` — decision encoded in the yaml itself
  (e.g. K001's canonical PDG-review snapshot path, B002's all-charmonium
  headline) and accepted by CA Wave-1 implicitly when CHK-1..CHK-8 passed.
- `[RESOLVED-by-C08 2026-05-25, tracked-in R03-I1, closed-by cleanup-C01
  commit 3ab1f8f]` — cross-link to a previously-closed cleanup unit
  (K001 item 2).
- `[DEFERRED-to-post-paper-finalization 2026-05-25 (C08)]` — a real but
  non-urgent follow-up (B009 item 2: direct f_B |V_ub| construction).
- `[CLOSED-as-N/A-by-C08 2026-05-25]` / `[CLOSED-as-by-design 2026-05-25 (C08)]`
  — informational provenance notes (B015's rejected-anchor record, scope
  boundary to B016-B019).

This shape keeps yaml parsing identical to pre-C08 (still a list of
strings) and survives any future audit tooling that walks `open_issues`.

## Forward-looking convention

For future PKA cycles, when a CHK-8 pass implicitly accepts a headline
choice, the WA or CA should at the same time mark the corresponding
`open_issues` thread with one of the C08 markers (RESOLVED / DEFERRED /
CLOSED-as-N/A) rather than leaving the bullet in indefinite-pending
state. The marker syntax used in C08 is
`[RESOLVED-by-<cycle-id> YYYY-MM-DD]` followed by a short rationale,
followed by `Original note: ` and the verbatim prior text.
