Invert the charged-lepton mass relation
$$
m_{E_i} \simeq 2\,v\,k\, f_L\, Y_{E_i}\, f_{E_i}
\quad\Rightarrow\quad
Y_{E_i}=\frac{m_{E_i}}{2 v k\, f_L\, f_{E_i}} \, ,
$$
to solve for the diagonal Yukawas $(Y_{E_e}, Y_{E_\mu}, Y_{E_\tau})$ given $f_L$, the three $f_{E_i}$, and the physical lepton masses (GeV). Also returns $\bar Y_{E_i}=2k\,Y_{E_i}$ for quick perturbativity checks.
